# Ship Combat Forces

## Overview

The ship maintains a standing military complement across eight tiers, from raw recruits through to specialist religious and technical personnel. Total combat-capable personnel: approximately 2,554.

---

---

## Notes

**Recruits (Tier 0)** represent the ship's training pipeline rather than a deployable force. In emergencies they can be armed, but should not be treated as equivalent to Tier 1 personnel.

**Veteran Infantry (Tier 2)** are notably few — 15 individuals among over a thousand basic soldiers. These are survivors of extended operations who have demonstrated capability above the baseline. Losing any of them is a significant loss that the pipeline cannot quickly replace.

**Enginseers (Tier 5)** bridge the gap between Damien's engineering staff and the combat complement — their role is keeping the weapons, vehicles, and field equipment running during operations, not pure combat.

**Daemonologists (Tier 6)** and **Chaplains (Tier 7)** reflect the ship's warp-adjacent culture. The daemonologists handle practical occult threats encountered in the field — warp entities, possessed equipment, anomalous phenomena. The chaplains serve the ship cult's observances and maintain cohesion and morale across the complement, particularly during and after warp transit.
