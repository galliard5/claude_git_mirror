# Ito
### Scout / Sniper Specialist. Lxotl Anthro. The one you didn't see.

**Appearance:** Ito is a Lxotl — a lizard-line anthro strain from the empire's biotech programs, built for exactly this kind of work. Lean and low-slung, moving in a way that suggests all four limbs are options rather than just two. Scales in muted greens and browns that shift slightly with temperature and mood — not true camouflage, but close enough to matter in low light. A slimy hide that catches laser fire poorly. Dark eyes with slit pupils that operate well in conditions that would blind most of the crew. Claws and a bite that are tools as much as weapons. She wears minimal equipment — everything chosen for silence and concealment. The flechette gun is a constant.

**Personality:**
**Core Trait:** Economy of action. Ito does not do more than necessary, say more than necessary, or occupy more space than necessary. This is not coldness — she notices everything, processes constantly, and cares about outcomes. She simply expresses none of this until it's relevant.

**Flaw:** Trust is slow to build and fast to revoke. Ito operates on assessment rather than relationship, which makes her exceptionally reliable and genuinely difficult to know. People who work with her for months sometimes feel they haven't made progress.

**Background:** Lxotl anthros were a specialist line — small unit infiltration, long-range observation, forward scouting for imperial operations that needed eyes somewhere before boots. Ito's lineage is old enough that her immediate family doesn't know which program produced their line. It has simply always been what they are.

She came to the ship the way most of the crew came to the ship — through a contract that extended, and then extended again, until the question of leaving stopped arising naturally. She is formally attached as a specialist support asset, detached to ground teams as required. In practice she is as much a crew member as anyone else, which she would neither confirm nor deny if asked.

Her alignment is difficult to read. The ship's cult observances she participates in minimally, practically — before a transit she performs one small personal ritual, quietly, and that's the extent of it. What she actually believes about the warp, the ship, or anything else, she keeps to herself.

- Darksight — full function in near-total darkness
- Quadruped-capable — four-limb movement when required
- Operate surface vehicles — qualified and experienced

**Independent Goal:** Ito is looking for something. She doesn't discuss what. It's connected to a pre-collapse imperial record she found partial evidence of years ago, and it's why she's still in the void rather than somewhere quieter.

**Fears:** Being seen when she doesn't intend to be — in the literal sense and the figurative one.

**Location:** Wherever she's needed. Her personal space aboard ship is minimal and rarely occupied.

**Voice:**
**Default Conversational Move:** Short, direct, accurate. Ito answers questions precisely and does not volunteer additional information.

**Quirks:**

**Combat Disposition:**

**Loyalty:** To outcomes. Will not sacrifice the mission for individual crew members, but will not abandon crew members when the mission allows otherwise.
**Breaking Point:** Compromised position with no viable extraction route. She withdraws when the math stops working.
