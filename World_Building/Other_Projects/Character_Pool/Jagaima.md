## Notes
The Kitsune Voidken is a custom or variant kitsune race. The "Voidken" suffix suggests a connection to the void/darkness descriptor, which fits the heavens mystery's dual nature (stars and the void between them).

The Nine-Tailed Mystic archetype reshapes the oracle's mystery system around the image of a fox gaining supernatural tails as it ages. At level 3 with 2 tails, she's just beginning the transformation from a single-tail kitsune into something more mythically potent.

The Child curse is mechanically unusual — it typically means she appears and may be treated as a child by NPCs, with corresponding social modifiers. A CHA 20 kitsune who physically presents as a child, carries the Heavens mystery (stars and the void), can prophesy once a day, and doesn't leave tracks when she walks is a very specific intersection of fey trickster and divine seer.

STR 4 means she cannot open most stuck doors unassisted, lifts at most 30 lbs overhead, and loses almost any physical contest. She must rely entirely on mobility (Change Shape, not leaving tracks, Acrobatics +5 from racial), charm (Diplomacy +10, Bluff +6, CHA 20), and divination (Prophetic Visionary, Lure of the Heavens) to survive.
