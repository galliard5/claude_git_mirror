## Notes

The filename "Dragon business tycoon" is the essential character concept: a mutant dragon who went into business rather than adventuring. The skills (Business and Finance, Law, Computer Operation, Research, Astrophysics) are entirely oriented toward corporate or academic success. The science program (Advanced Math, Chemistry, Archaeology, Astrophysics) combined with business law creates a character who could be a CEO, a research director, or both.

The four languages (Draconic + Chinese, German, Russian, English) alongside the business-program education suggest international operations — someone who negotiates across linguistic and cultural lines.

H2H Expert and PS 34 mean that when things do go wrong, he can handle it physically. The Electrical Field power (4d6/round in a 10+ foot radius) is devastating at crowd control. But the dominant character concept is the suit, not the claws.

HU Mutant Animals are animals that have undergone mutagenic transformation to become anthropomorphic. Anthony is a dragon who thinks like a person, went to school, and built a career.
