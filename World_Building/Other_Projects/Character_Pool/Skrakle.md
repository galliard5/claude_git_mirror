## Hooks & Notes
Skrakle is a simple, coherent build: a teenage raptor hunter built to stalk and pounce in woodland terrain. The armor-piercing (2) divisor on all natural weapons means she's specifically designed to fight armored opponents, which at TL4 (medieval) means most serious enemies. DR 3 from scales covers the basics without needing to put on a breastplate — which she can't anyway.

The Pounce + Biting Grapple combination is the intended opening: close distance fast, grapple with the bite, then work with the talons. Brawling 13 with a pounce charge and armor-piercing talons can open a fight decisively at low TL.

IQ 9 is genuinely low — below human average. The skill selection reflects this: she knows the forest, she knows combat, she knows plants and animals. Alchemy at IQ/VH with 1pt means she knows *about* alchemy more than she can actually do it. The Herb Lore is the more practical expression.

The sheet was saved mid-session with 3 HP — she was in a fight and took significant damage. A fresh Skrakle has 13 HP; 3 remaining puts her at the Reeling threshold (below 1/3). She either finished the session injured or this was a mid-combat save.

"Kai's quick fantasy" implies a short or one-shot campaign. 73 points is even lighter than Icasus (79pts) — this was built fast, probably for a pickup game.
