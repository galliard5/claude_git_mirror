## Notes
The Sickles game character is half-finished: no name, no bio, Maximize Spell as a feat that can't be used yet. He's either built for a session that happened without much further development, or the campaign stopped before it started.

The Death domain on a NG halfling is the interesting theological choice. Death domain in 3.5 doesn't require being evil — it just means the cleric has domain access to death magic. For a Healing+Death cleric, the narrative is usually "I am the arbiter of when life ends and when it continues." For a halfling cleric of level 3 in "Sickles game," that might be a bit ambitious.
