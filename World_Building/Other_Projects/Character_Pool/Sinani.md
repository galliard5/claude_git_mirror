## Notes

The Blessed Dragon racial template on a kobold is an interesting combination — she's a kobold who has been elevated or touched by true draconic heritage, hence the Draconic Aspect feat and Dragonbreath. The Light Sensitivity is the main racial tradeoff.

WIS 18 as the driver stat makes her an excellent healer and save-pusher (Will +9 at 7th level is strong). The combination of Heal +11, Channel 4d6 at 7/day, and Artificer's Touch (which repairs constructs) gives her a wide healing toolkit covering biological and mechanical targets.

Craft (traps) at +9 with 3 invested ranks is the kobold detail — she's a cleric who can also build traps. Linguistics at +6 with only 2 ranks means she's studied languages deliberately.

**Apsu connection:** Iskandai also worships Apsu. Having two characters in the pool who worship the same draconic god — one a Colossal dragon monk, one a tiny kobold cleric — suggests either they're from the same campaign or Apsu is a recurring element in Galliard's character concepts.

The Scalykind subdomain listed in the spell section is a third domain influence beyond Artifice and Dragon, suggesting the "Dragon" domain was taken as Scalykind in practice.

No gear on the sheet beyond an artisan's outfit. Either it was tracked elsewhere or this was an early build before equipment was assigned.
