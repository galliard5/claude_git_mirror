## Notes
Wemics are Large centauroid leonine creatures from 3.5 lore — they have the mobility of a big cat and the arms and cognition of a humanoid. Miria at 7'6" and 450 lbs is built at the scale her race implies: Large-sized equipment throughout, heavy barding rather than humanoid armor, oversized weapons.

Favored Enemy (Animals) on a lion-body creature is the same unusual choice as Jil (the hare ranger) — there's a pattern in this group of giving animal-race rangers the Animals favored enemy. Either it's a setting hook (predator/prey dynamics in an animal-people world) or a recurring design motif.

Favored Enemy (Undead) alongside Animals gives her two distinct battlefield roles: field tracker and beast-hunter during the day, undead-hunter when things go wrong at night. Survival 7, Knowledge (Dungeoneering) 2, and Knowledge (Nature) 1 suggest she knows the wilderness well but dungeons are secondary terrain.

Ring of Sustenance means she, like Jenney the paladin, doesn't need food or sleep. For a Large creature who would otherwise need to eat significantly more than a human, this is especially practical.
