## Notes
This is Galliard's own PC in what appears to be Cit's Dragonstar MnM campaign — the file is in the same folder as `Cits_MnM_dragonstar_game.por`. The Galactic Reputation benefit and Draconic language fit a far-future setting.

The Repulse Burst (gravity push, limited to flinging away) paired with Capoeira (a martial art built around acrobatic evasion and counterattack) creates a coherent defensive-movement style — keep opponents off balance, push them away, evade rather than stand and trade blows. The plasma pistol being Unreliable (5 uses) means it's a last resort.

Regeneration 20 is extraordinary, but the Tiring limitation and Increased Action 2 (standard action to activate) mean it's not always on — she has to spend an action and will tire if she keeps it running. Combined with Impervious Toughness from Scales, she's very durable but not invincible.

Wealth 5 (billionaire) and Cipher 2 combined with Galactic Reputation suggests someone known and wealthy but who has deliberately obscured their personal history. The Enemy and Reputation complications are the hooks she plays against.
