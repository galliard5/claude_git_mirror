## Notes
Two years old. A jaguar is adult at roughly 2-3 years — she's just reached adulthood and has already been awakened, learned three languages, and become a rogue. The Awakened template in 3.5 is granted by the Druid spell Awaken, which raises INT and CHA to 3d6 each and gives an alignment. Someone cast Awaken on her, and she immediately became Chaotic Good and started picking locks.

The growling speech pattern is noted in the character bio — she speaks, but it sounds like growling. All three of her languages presumably come out that way.

The Sleight of Hand skill is the most conceptually striking — a jaguar performing sleight of hand. The pouches on the harness at least explain what she'd be concealing things in.

No combat equipment because she doesn't need it. At level 1 with STR 15 and DEX 17, her natural attacks hit reasonably hard, and her rogue sneak attack adds on top when she can flank.
