## Notes

A dragon built at low PL in MnM2e — 75 PP at PL5 is a starting character. The fire weakness trade (Immunity to cold and acid, but always suffers a side-effect when hit with fire) is a deliberate drawback choice, suggesting a cold or acid-type dragon lineage.

Dragon Nobility is a 2-rank Benefit — in a Dragonstar setting this means actual societal rank, not just status. Fearsome Presence 3 combined with Diplomacy +5 and Intimidate +4 creates a character who's both threatening and negotiable, which fits draconic power dynamics.

Magic 10 with VSM (verbal/somatic/material) components as a custom descriptor means this is traditional spellcasting dragon — scholarly, with an arcane toolkit. Artificer lets her craft magic items. At PL5 with Magic 10, she's capable of significant magical effects despite the low overall power level.

The sheet was left unnamed, suggesting it was either a concept draft or the name was tracked outside the system.
