## Notes

Incognito is built around a coherent concept: hermetic mage who operates as a non-magical infiltrator first, with magic as support. Demolitions 5 and Locksmith 5 (both with specializations) are her primary non-magic tools — she can bypass almost any lock physically or electronically, and blow things up when she can't. Improved Invisibility and Silence cover the gaps. Magic Fingers handles the things her physical hands can't reach. Astral Chameleon and Astral Combat 2 mean she can defend herself astrally but leaves fewer traces.

The water theme is distinctive — allergy, prejudice, and spirit bane together suggest a specific event or trauma involving water spirits or water magic that rewrote how she interacts with that domain. It's not a common combination to choose voluntarily.

Four fake SINs with different quality levels (2 and 4) suggests a tiered identity system — the Rating 4 SINs for serious business, the Rating 2s for disposable cover identities. The "Will Smith" SIN with a year of DocWagon and four months of lifestyle indicates it's her primary long-running cover, which is either very funny or very deliberate.
