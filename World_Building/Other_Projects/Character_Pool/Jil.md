## Notes
Astalin as a birthplace appears only on this sheet in the whole PCGen batch. It's either a city in the campaign setting or a made-up hometown that never got expanded.

The "Copy of Copy of Hare" race label in PCGen means someone created a custom hare race, then copied it, then copied the copy. The Noble Wild supplement (referenced in the campaign settings alongside 3.5 RSRD) is a 3.5 sourcebook explicitly for playing animals as PCs. Jil is therefore a sapient hare in an animal-people setting.

The 0 XP confirms she was never played, or played in a single scene before the campaign stopped. Given the "Copy of Copy" race and the session-zero feel, this may be a character built during a game that never launched.
