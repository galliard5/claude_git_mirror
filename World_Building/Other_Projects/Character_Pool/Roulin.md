## Notes
A Lawful Good gnoll paladin who is also a zombie is the single most conceptually ambitious character in the PCGen batch. The "pc zombie" template applies Undead typing — which means: no Constitution score, immunity to poisons/sleep/paralysis/stunning/disease/death effects, darkvision, cold and electricity resistance. But he's also LG, has CHA 16 for paladin lay on hands and smite, and Diplomacy + Sense Motive for social interactions.

The Negotiate feat is the detail that makes him fully realized as a character concept: a decaying zombie gnoll in armor who walks into social situations and is specifically trained to talk through problems. The decaying yellow-eyed undead who smells terrible is the first to try diplomacy, and then uses Sense Motive to verify the response.

Only speaking Gnoll at level 8 equivalent (Humanoid 2 + Paladin 6) means he can't speak to most people he meets. He has Diplomacy 6 in a language almost nobody in a standard fantasy city speaks. That's either an intentional character flaw or a campaign where Gnoll was the common tongue.

His weapon (Inflict/heal himself) + his mount (Snuffles) + his tabard (sacred AC bonus) + his Negotiator feat together suggest a character built with a specific, settled self-conception: the Undead Paladin is who he is, not a temporary condition.
