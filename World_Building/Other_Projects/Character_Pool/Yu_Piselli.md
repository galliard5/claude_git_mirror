## Hooks & Notes

A young Deinonichus raptor who went into space command rather than anything involving her natural combat advantages — at 100 points, Strategy (Space) 14 and Leadership 15 are the overwhelming majority of her point investment. She can pilot a high-performance spacecraft, navigate through hyperspace, and command a crew. The sickle claw and Super Jump are racial hardware she carries without having invested in using them well (Karate at only 1pt).

The Sculpting hobby is the character detail that stands out most. A raptor space strategist who makes sculptures in her spare time. Speed-Reading supports the command role — she presumably reads a lot of tactical reports.

The Deinonichus as a species choice is unusual. They're mid-sized dromaeosaurids in paleontological terms — smaller than a Velociraptor in the pop-culture sense, but the GURPS stats here (SM 0, 5'10", 160lb) are built as an uplifted or anthropomorphic version. The sickle claw modeled as a ranged attack with 100-yard range is almost certainly a sheet construction artifact rather than intended to represent a literal ranged attack — raptor kicks are not that. The visual intent is a dramatic leaping kick.

At 100 points with 10pts in Strategy and 6pts in Leadership, she's a starting-career commander — young (22), capable, not yet tested.
