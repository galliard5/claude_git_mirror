## Hooks & Notes
Charlote is an engineer-hacker who has made herself into a dangerous spider-machine through the Arachnoid morph. The Blacklisted and Black Mark traits suggest she's burned bridges with some faction or corp — likely connected to her autonomist Titanian politics. Ghostrider Modules ×2 means she can host other egos, which has significant roleplay implications. Multitasking allows parallel cognitive threads. Hyper Linguist makes her a capable universal communicator. The combination of top-tier engineering/robotics skills, combat capability, and covert surveillance gear (microbugs, smart dust, sniffer) makes her equally at home in a workshop, an infiltration op, or a firefight. Morphing Disorder suggests she has psychological difficulty with resleeving — a meaningful vulnerability given the EP setting.
