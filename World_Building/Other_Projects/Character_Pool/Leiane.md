## Notes
A kobold bard who plays wind instruments, has more bardic music uses than standard, and knows Charm Person and Lullaby. The combination of performance, social manipulation, lock-picking (Nimble Fingers), and Sleight of Hand creates a consistent character concept: she talks her way in, charms who she needs to, picks the locks that can't be opened with words, and steals what she came for.

The 1,015 gp left over after the +1 Chain Shirt and MW rapier and MW instrument suggests she was given a higher starting wealth package. At level 3, a standard starting wealth for bard is ~2,700 gp, and she's spent about 1,700 gp on gear, which checks out.

CHA 16 on a kobold bard is doing a lot of work: it's her casting stat, her Bardic Music DC, her social skills modifier, and her face-of-the-party attribute. Combined with Diplomacy 6, she can function as the group's primary social interface.
