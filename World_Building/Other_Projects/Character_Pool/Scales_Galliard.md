## Notes

The Shark mentor creates tension with the Assassin's Creed code of honor. Shark pushes toward berserking when hurt; the Creed demands controlled, professional conduct. The threshold check (6 vs 3 for Shark, 6 vs 4 for the Code) means both fire regularly under pressure.

Light Body (2) combined with Gymnastics 9 and the jumping bonus makes him extraordinarily mobile — this is someone who enters through unusual routes and exits faster. Missile Parry (2) means he can deflect incoming fire as an interrupt, which pairs with the combat-aware Shark mentor.

No SURGE, no explosive foam, no demolitions — this version of Scales is purely a ghost who hits people. The "Ms Scales" persona he carries is operational cover, not his identity. Somewhere between him and the SURGE-modified versions the character concept split into two: the one who got extensive body modification, and the one who kept his original body and just became very good at hitting things quietly.
