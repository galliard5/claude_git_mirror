## Notes
Shami at witch 10 with Plague patron is explicitly an undead-raising witch — Animate Dead and Command Undead appear as patron spells, and her hex suite (Cursed Wound, Swamp's Grasp) creates a battlefield where wounded enemies can't heal and terrain becomes difficult. The Aura of Purity hex is the counterintuitive element: purifying something isn't a typical NE witch choice, but it could serve as area cleansing from her own plague effects, protecting allies while the disease does its work on enemies.

The STR 6 means she doesn't fight. She raises things to fight for her.

Knowledge (Engineering) at 17 is the unusual academic investment — a witch who studies structures and machines alongside arcana and nature. In a campaign called "Tempest Rising," understanding engineering might have nautical applications.

Comparison to Kani: Both are kobold witches from Galliard's campaigns. Kani (Sea Dragons, LE, level 5) is a sea-going tattooed crafter who makes individual magic items and keeps a venomous familiar. Shami (Tempest Rising, NE, level 10) is a plague-witch who raises armies of undead and entangles battlefields. Same base class, completely different expressions.
