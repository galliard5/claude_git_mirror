The twin thunder machine gun is an oversized weapon with a scope, dealing 3d12 fire damage — significantly more than standard. Paired with the laser SMG (for suppressed fire) and 12 grenades of two types, Marcos carries a full spectrum from stealthy to catastrophic.

The subcutaneous implants (radio, ID chip, anti-shock) are permanent body modifications. Combined with the luminous skin chronometer (a glowing display built into his skin), he's more modified than Kelly by a significant margin.

## Notes
Blood Hunger on a military character creates logistics: he needs to feed regularly on blood or suffer penalties. In a covert ops campaign where killing is the job, this is manageable — but it becomes conspicuous. Yellow eyes and grey wolf fur are already distinctive; add glowing skin and the biological need to feed on blood and he's not an easy person to pass off as ordinary.

The twin thunder machine gun is enormous for a medium creature — the file flags it as Large-sized. He carries it anyway.

**Campaign cross-reference:** Kelly (`Kelly.md`) is in the same Magnus campaign. Kelly is a small human infiltrator with silenced pistols; Marcos is a large wolf soldier with a machine gun and 12 grenades. They complement each other operationally if not aesthetically.
