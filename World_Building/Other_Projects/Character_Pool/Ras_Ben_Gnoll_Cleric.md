## Notes
"Turtles quick game" — a short or one-shot campaign, similar in framing to "Kai's quick fantasy." The Gnoll Ras Ben at XP 6,400 represents about 3 sessions of play at this level. He is fully unnamed except for the character name field.

The name "Ras Ben" appearing twice in the PCGen batch (here as a Gnoll Cleric, and in `Ras Ben.pcg` as a Kobold Wizard) is either coincidence, an homage between campaigns, or someone liked the name enough to reuse it. The kobold Ras Ben is an archaeologist with a wagon; the gnoll Ras Ben is a battle cleric with heavy armor and a mace. They share only the name.

Death + Healing domain is the same as the halfling in Sickles game. Two clerics in this batch have this exact pairing. It may reflect a house-rule or setting-specific thematic preference in the group.
