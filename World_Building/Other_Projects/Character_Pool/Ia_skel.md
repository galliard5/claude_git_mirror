## Hooks & Notes
The spell selection is broad rather than deep — she covers all four elements plus healing and necromancy, which at Magery 2 makes her versatile in the field if not overwhelming in any one area. Steal Energy/Vitality sitting alongside Minor Healing is the most interesting choice: she can give HP and take them. The elemental breadth (Air through Water) combined with Cartography, Archaeology, and Boating points toward an explorer build — someone who goes places and needs to handle whatever the environment throws at her.

The Short Attention Span (CR 9) rolls 11- to trigger, meaning she fails about 2/3 of the time on extended boring tasks. This is a constant background problem for someone whose spells require concentration and whose academic skills (Archaeology, History, Cartography) require sustained focus. It shapes how sessions play out more than most disadvantages.

Loves Practical Jokes is the pure kobold identity trait. The sheet notes describe kobolds as creatures who find hotfoots and buckets of slop "immensely amusing" — an exceptional kobold mage with Magery 2 and Steal Vitality would have access to considerably more creative pranks than a bucket of slop.

Will 14 against IQ 12 is the mechanical expression of stubborn focus despite scatter-brained tendencies: when she decides to concentrate, she can resist distractions. She just doesn't always decide to.
