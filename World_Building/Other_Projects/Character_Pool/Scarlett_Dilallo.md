## The Agoraphobia Note

A spacer character with Agoraphobia (Open Spaces) is the central tension of this build. She's designed for TL10 space travel — No Degeneration in Zero-G, Acceleration Tolerance, Radiation Tolerance — and she's afraid of open spaces. This likely means she functions fine in enclosed spaces (ship corridors, stations, caves) but struggles with open decks, planetary surfaces, or EVA. The phobia would rarely trigger inside a ship and regularly trigger the moment she steps outside one. Chosen rather than rolled, which means it was an intentional character hook.

## Hooks & Notes

The four-arm, four-leg profile with full ambidexterity and Power Grappling makes her an exceptional grappler in enclosed spaces — which is exactly where she's comfortable. She can bring all four arms and four legs to a grapple while maintaining stability on her quadruped legs. The Peripheral Vision and Infravision mean she's hard to surprise.

Longevity at age 83 leaves open how old "old" is for Rikbin. If her lifespan is 200+, she's approaching middle age. If it's 150, she's past it. The sheet doesn't specify, and Vikar presumably knew.

The name "Scarlett Dilallo" is notably more human-style than the names of other alien characters in this pool (Ia'skel, Riktiki, Mist in the Wind). Either Rikbin have human-adjacent naming conventions, or this is a chosen persona name rather than a birth name — a possibility for a 83-year-old spacer who's been operating in human-dominated space long enough to adopt a recognizable name.
