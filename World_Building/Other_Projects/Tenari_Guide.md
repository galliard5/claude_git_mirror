# Tenari Guide

**Type:** Setting document / Alien species writeup
**Setting:** Star League (sci-fi RPG setting)
**File:** `TenariGuide.txt`

This document is a player/GM guide to the Tenari species. Saved here as a reference for any campaign in the Star League setting.

---

## Quick Reference

**Planet:** Narimera ("home of the people" in Tenari)
**Government:** Planetary federation of nation-states + colonial breakaway republics/kingdoms
**Dominant culture:** Hospitality, entertainment, hedonism, philosophy
**Psionics:** Most Tenari have latent empathy; stronger individuals develop telepathy, clairsentience, or other abilities
**Military:** Minimal — scout, science, and diplomatic vessels; no true military
**Economy:** Entertainment + hospitality sector dominant; medicine, architecture, engineering secondary

---

## Three Subraces

| Subrace | Description | Psychics | Disposition |
|---------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **Tenari** ("people who do") | Most numerous; the cultural face | Widespread empathy; some telepathy | Gentle, libertine, welcoming |
| **Kynari** ("people who plan") | Former ruling class; Therian-like appearance | Rare, but powerful when manifest; pyrokinesis, PK | More reserved, historically dominant |
| **Lanari** ("wild people") | Distant continent origin; Feylid-like | No confirmed psychic powers | Averse to rigid authority; historically persecuted |

Intermarriage is becoming more common; the three are one species.

---

## Physiology
- Nearly-hairless skin; full spectrum of tones
- Furred ears and tails (may not match hair color)
- Hair: full spectrum plus unusual colors (pink, blue)
- Eyes: gold, purple, blue, orange as common as standard colors
- Height: 1.5–1.8m typical; up to 2.1m possible; males average 5cm taller
- Slender and light-framed

**Tenari specifically:** Many males lack a tail (dominant trait); universally attractive to almost all other known races — apparently cross-species aesthetic appeal

**Sequential hermaphrodites:** born female → shift to male with age (gradual biological transition)

---

## Psionics
- Tenari empaths cannot help picking up general mood of nearby beings
- Telepathy use without consent is illegal; enforced by psionic security forces
- Empathic societal cohesion: Tenari disagree but tend to understand each other

---

## Religion: Three Faiths
1. **Polytheistic (historical)** — gods from heaven created the Nari; used by Kynari to control Tenari during feudal period
2. **Maritanism (duotheistic, rare)** — good deity + Karita The Great Enemy; peaked during Renaissance, now ~1 million followers
3. **Somine ne Kiruhuhal** ("Serenity Arising From Chaos Arising From Serenity") — most popular today; meditation + ritual riddles + pleasure; functions as a psychic training program; both monastery and resort

**Highest virtue (Somine):** Hope, supported by Love, Knowledge, and Joy. Notably absent: Honor, martial Valor.

---

## Culture
- Everything legal on Narimera: alcohol, drugs, gambling, prostitution — regulated
- Cultural fusion: actively adopts and blends elements from other species' cultures
- Martial arts survive from feudal era as "dancing forms" (spear, sword, staff, whip, unarmed)
- Few Tenari in combat military roles; prefer pilots, medics, intelligence, logistics
- Diplomacy: extremely effective; cross-species empathy + multi-language acquisition + physical attractiveness = consistent diplomatic success

**Historical note:** Pre-industrial history was violent; Tenari-led revolution ended Kynari nobility rule. Environmental damage was subsequently cleaned up.

---

## Star League Role
Narimera is distant from Core Worlds; the Tenari are cultural exporters rather than military powers. Their total entertainment output reportedly equals the rest of the League combined. First contact via their diplomatic and outreach vessels — light-armed, charm-forward.
