The Captain
============

Minor NPC. Garrison captain of the small town where the witch was brought after the tower incident. Practical, competent, slightly out of his depth with magical matters. He held the witch, examined the necromancer's book (without understanding it), and ultimately deferred authority to the envoy when the investigation exceeded his jurisdiction.

appearance: Not described in detail. Military bearing. Drinks bitter morning tea.

personality: **Core Trait:** Practical authority. Handles what he can, defers what he can't.

**Flaw:** Limited understanding of magic makes him uncomfortable with anything beyond straightforward garrison duties.

Race: Human

Status: Garrison captain — remained in town when the escort departed for the capital

Age: Unknown

Voice: **Quirks:**
- Asks direct, practical questions.
- Shifts and fidgets when magical topics are discussed.

**Dialogue Sample:**
(Leaning back, rubbing sleep from his eyes.)
"This isn't just notes. These are copied from older texts."
(Nodding slowly.)
"Then this is bigger than our little town."

Location: The garrison town (unnamed). Did not travel with the escort to the capital.
