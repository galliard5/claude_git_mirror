## Summary

A full working day. Three rows of the east field in the morning — the body finding the load's rhythm by the second row, the third row taking Gemszi somewhere behind the work while the legs handled themselves. South quarter begun in the afternoon: west rise first, firmer ground, Gemszi already moving that direction before Oskar called it. Oskar noticed and said nothing. Something large in the south treeline — heavy, unhurried, territorial, not hunting — filed for the boundary rows tomorrow. Maren completed the third dairy cycle; the mind stepped back from the window this time. Yard circuits after to walk out the day. The *maybe* from midday is still present at nightfall. Unresolved. Not demanding anything.

**Foreshadowing Planted:**
