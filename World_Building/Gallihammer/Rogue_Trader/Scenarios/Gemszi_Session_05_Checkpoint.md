## Summary

The south quarter finished — boundary rows worked in deteriorating weather with the treeline thing keeping pace along the timber's interior throughout, never pressing, always present. First rain: arrived mid-boundary-rows, cold and novel, the body registering the sensation across its full surface with no filing system for it. Sheltered in the barn through the afternoon — the idle hours revealing the downside of the arrangement clearly: nothing to do except the circuit of one's own thoughts, the ship memories available and unavoidable, the long slow fear underneath. The fifth dairy cycle produced a significant exchange: Maren reported Oskar's observation about the west rise call, Gemszi grunted confirmation, Maren's hands stopped. The first direct acknowledgment of anything. Evening yard circuit in the rain's aftermath, the broken overcast producing the first sunset he has seen. Word-check holds. The *maybe* present for the fifth consecutive night — a fixture now.
