**RPG CONTEXT SUMMARY — NEON FANG PROTOCOL**

Setting: Cyberpunk megacity controlled by corporations (Fenrix Bio-Systems). Biotech experiments are used to create hybrid “feral integration” subjects.

Character:

* Name: Isandar
* Race: Kobold
* Class: Bio-Subject (Sorcerer reskin)
* Role: Emerging Alpha
* Personality: Curious, mischievous, honest (does not pretend to be stronger than he is)

Origin:
Isandar was taken by his parents (who sold him to pay debts) to undergo an experimental procedure called “Feral Integration.” Instead of being controlled, he formed a **mutual bond** with the implanted predator template, becoming something new—an **independent Alpha-type entity**.

Key Developments:

* Escaped the facility during containment breach
* Bonded with one stable wolf-like creature
* Stabilized and recruited additional unstable subjects into a **pack**
* Encountered the **True Alpha** (original source of the strain), who is intelligent and now traveling with the group (but not subordinate)
* Learned that the corporation did NOT create this power—they stole it from an older species

Current State:

* Isandar leads a **pack of 6 creatures**
* Has abilities: Feral Instincts, Feral Burst, Pack Bond, Tactical Predator, Feral Flow
* Has begun developing leadership through **stability, not domination**
* Corporation is actively hunting him as “F-9” (high-priority target)
* Stronger hunter units are deployed

Current Location:

* Escaped to the surface into a neon-lit cyberpunk city in the rain

Dynamic:

* Isandar = emerging Alpha (leader through choice and connection)
* True Alpha = teacher / observer (not submissive)
* Pack = loyal but still evolving

Tone:

* Survival + evolution
* Leadership and identity
* Predator instincts vs conscious choice
* Growing power vs corporate threat

Goal Going Forward:

* Find shelter
* Strengthen pack
* Understand Alpha nature
* Decide whether to hide, survive, or eventually take down Fenrix
