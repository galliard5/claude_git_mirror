Brac
====

Working hound, Maruvec garrison kennel. Olive-bronze coat. Selected by Isandar as donor hound after three weeks of deliberate observation and relationship building.

---

Profile
-------

**Coat:** Olive-bronze — same colour lineage passed to Isandar's transformed form.
**Temperament:** Patient, steady. Accepted Isandar's presence at the fence over three weeks without pressure. Closed his eyes with one foot of space between them — the moment the donor decision settled.
**Sex:** Male.
**Pack Position:** Established member. Not the anchor (that's Kess), but respected and stable within the hierarchy.

---

Key Events
----------

**Three-week pre-transformation period:** Isandar spent regular time at Brac's run fence. The relationship built incrementally — proximity, patience, mutual scenting through the wire. Brac responded to consistency.

**Morning of transformation day:** Isandar sat with Brac at the fence in early morning light until 0800. Kess observed from across the yard.

**Post-transformation introduction (Day after, Session 05):** Isandar walked to the east run fence, sat in the same position as the three-week routine. Brac approached and touched noses through the wire. Varre opened the gate. Brac leaned shoulder to shoulder and stayed. Full acceptance — physical and complete.

---

Significance
------------

Brac is the anchor for Isandar's pack integration. The three-week fence investment paid in full. His acceptance was the foundation that made the rest of the introduction sequence (Kess, then the remaining hounds, then Renne) possible.

He could smell the transformation from his run before the introduction. He knew something changed. The introduction confirmed that the fence relationship survived the transformation.
