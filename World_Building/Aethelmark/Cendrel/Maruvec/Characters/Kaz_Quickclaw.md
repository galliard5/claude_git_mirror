Kaz Quickclaw
==============

Kobold male. Isandar's father. Carpenter's labourer working day-hire through a labour exchange in The Chains, Maruvec. Originally from a settlement in Cendrel's eastern interior. Came to Maruvec seven years ago with his wife Yara, following work.

---

Appearance
----------

Older kobold, worn from years of physical labour. Hands roughened from carpentry. Quieter in bearing than his wife — the kind of man who takes up less space than he occupies.

---

Personality
-----------

Quiet, practical. When Isandar told him about the transformation, Kaz found the load-bearing facts and held them. This is how he processes — he identifies the concrete details he can hold onto and lets the rest settle around them. He is proud of Isandar's enlistment in a way he hasn't said directly.

---

Voice
-----

**Quirks:**
- Responds to difficult information by asking practical questions — when, how long, what happens after.
- Says less than he means. The gaps between his words carry weight.

---

Location
--------

Corders Lane, The Chains, Maruvec.

---

Relationships
-------------

**Isandar** — His son. Informed of the transformation on Day 1 of the final pre-transformation week. Held the facts. Did not break.
**Yara Quickclaw** — Wife. They process differently — he goes quiet, she goes controlled.
