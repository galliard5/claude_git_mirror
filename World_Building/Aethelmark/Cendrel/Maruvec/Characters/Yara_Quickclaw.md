Yara Quickclaw
==============

Kobold female. Isandar's mother. Takes in mending and laundry for households in Mercers' Row, Maruvec. Originally from a settlement in Cendrel's eastern interior. Sharper and more verbal than her husband Kaz.

---

Appearance
----------

Older kobold. Hands show the fine-work wear of years of mending. More expressive than Kaz — her worry is visible in the way she holds herself, even when she's controlling it.

---

Personality
-----------

Worried about the hound assignment in ways she's expressed clearly. Sharper and more verbal than Kaz. When Isandar came to tell them, she put her hand over his once and took it back. At the door: "Come back after." Not a request — she made it a fact he would return. This is how she manages fear: she states the outcome she requires and treats it as settled.

---

Voice
-----

**Quirks:**
- States outcomes as facts rather than hopes. "Come back after" — not "please come back."
- Physical gestures are brief and deliberate — a hand placed once, then withdrawn. Economy of touch that carries more weight than sustained contact would.

---

Location
--------

Corders Lane, The Chains, Maruvec.

---

Relationships
-------------

**Isandar** — Her son. Informed of the transformation on Day 1. "Come back after." She is waiting. Visit not yet possible during recovery period.
**Kaz Quickclaw** — Husband. They process differently but hold the same ground.
