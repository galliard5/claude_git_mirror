## Atmosphere & Mood

Deliberately concealed. Old-growth forest on three sides, off the main road, not on any map that matters. The facility smells of two layers — old animal-work underneath (decades of use) and newer chemical habitation on top. Calm, professional, and completely committed to its purpose. Nobody here was nervous before the squad arrived. That was the most informative thing about it.
