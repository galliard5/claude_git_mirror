Gregor
======

Contracted Alchemist, Buyer's Facility. The man who ran the procedures and claims he didn't ask questions he should have.

appearance: Lean, middle-aged. Hands had residue when interrupted mid-process. The kind of build that comes from laboratory work rather than physical labour — precise rather than strong. Professional manner throughout, even when contained at the shed wall with a direwolf standing over him.

personality: **Core Trait:** Technical detachment. Gregor states intentions as facts rather than questions. He approaches transformation work as procedure — inputs, outputs, process. The ethical layer is something he acknowledges he failed to apply.

**Flaw:** Wilful blindness. He claims he believed subjects were volunteers. He acknowledged he was wrong not to verify. The gap between "I believed" and "I chose not to check" is the gap that defines him.

background: Unknown in detail. Contracted for process only — not the facility's owner, not the planner. Paid via unmarked treasury bar — untraceable, deliberate. His shed contained four cylinders (not three), a transformation diagram with second-hand annotations, and a non-standard speaking collar. He spoke of a six-day window and agreed to one more draw maximum from the panther.

His workspace and materials indicate genuine alchemical competence. He is not a fraud — he knows transformation work. Where he trained and who certified him are unknown. His presence at an unlicensed facility suggests either programme defection or independent study.
