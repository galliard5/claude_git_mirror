Human, Male, Ruler of Empire. Enforces imperial law, hosts trial, upholds witness status.

background: Current Emperor. Assumed the throne following his father's death. His father and Duke Romaine Kreiger were adventuring companions before his father's ascension — the Kreiger connection to the imperial house predates Aldric's reign by a generation.

Isalia Kreiger served alongside Aldric personally during the campaigns of his youth, when he was still a prince. Roughly forty years ago. The bond formed in those years is direct and not ceremonial — Isalia is among a small number of people who knew the Emperor before he was one. He is aware of her estate, her work, and her family's history. The imperial protections on the Kreiger duchy and its operations reflect this relationship as much as they reflect any formal charter.
