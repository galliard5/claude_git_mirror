The Harbor District
===================

The natural harbor formed by the river bend, center of river trade and commerce.

Overview: The natural harbor formed by the Angerap's sharp bend is the lifeblood of Silberbach's trade. Sheltered from the main current, the docks can accommodate dozens of river barges at once. The district never truly sleeps — cargo is loaded and unloaded at all hours, and the taverns here cater to rivermen, merchants, and travelers.

**Notable Features:**
- **The Docks** — Wooden piers extending into the harbor
- **Warehouses** — Stone and timber buildings storing goods awaiting transport
- **The River Traders' Guild Hall** — Center of river commerce
- **Sailors' Taverns** — Rougher establishments catering to rivermen
- **Boat Builders' Yards** — Where barges and smaller vessels are constructed and repaired
