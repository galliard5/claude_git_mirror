The Old Temple
==============

The largest and oldest religious site in Silberbach, located within the Old Town walls.

Overview: The oldest religious site in Silberbach, the Old Temple predates the town walls. Its architecture reflects centuries of additions and renovations, creating a labyrinthine complex of shrines, chapels, and halls dedicated to various deities.

**Nature:**
As a mixed-faith temple, the Old Temple accommodates multiple religions under one roof. Different wings and chambers are dedicated to different deities, and the temple's priests represent various faiths.

**Services:**
- Daily rituals and prayers
- Marriage ceremonies
- Funeral rites
- Religious festivals
- Blessings and consecrations
- Spiritual counsel
