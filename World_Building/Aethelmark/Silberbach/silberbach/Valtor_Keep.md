Valtor Keep
===========

The fortified seat of House Valtor, rulers of the River Valley. Located within the Old Town walls.

Overview: A stone fortress overlooking the river bend, the Keep has been the seat of House Valtor for generations. Its walls are thick, its towers tall, and its dungeons deep. The Count maintains a garrison here, though the town's defenses have long since outgrown the need for such martial presence.

**Notable Areas:**
- **The Great Hall** — Where the Count holds court and receives petitioners
- **The Count's Chambers** — Private quarters of Count Elias Valtor
- **The Treasury** — Heavily guarded, storing tax revenues and the Count's personal wealth
- **The Dungeons** — Rarely used in peacetime, but maintained
- **The Garrison** — Barracks for the Count's personal guard
