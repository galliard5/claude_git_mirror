Fenk
====

Human, Male, forties. Field agent for Dietrich Haan's Mecklau facilitation operation. Used the name *Dressel* during his reconnaissance visit to Isalia's Manor. Identified by Marta Sehn as the man who followed her from Mecklau to Silberbach under the guise of *ensuring safe arrival.*

---

## Appearance

Heavyset through the chest — the build of someone who used to do physical work and has since gone soft at the edges. Clean-shaven. Good coat with no house mark. Good boots — a man who spends on his feet is a man who expects to be on them a long time. Dressed to be unremarkable and has thought about it.

Came to Isalia's Manor as *Dressel* without a servant, without a companion waiting at the gate. Knew the layout of the Apothecary well enough to go directly to the consultation alcove without being directed — been here before or had it described in detail.

In the Silberbach square: sitting on a low wall with a cup of something warm, facing the anchor inn door. Not hiding. Oriented toward it the way a man orients toward something he has been watching.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Measured calculation.**
Does not hurry. Does not show uncertainty — shows calculation instead. Acknowledges weak hands before being asked, because acknowledging them is strategy. *The argument has weaknesses. I'm aware of them.* A man who has been doing this long enough to know when to fold a position cleanly and when to hold it.

**Flaw: Loyalty is professional, not personal.** He dropped the Corvin claim with twelve words and no visible conflict. The claim was always an instrument, and he knew it.

---

## Background

Haan's field operation man. Has been in Silberbach for the four weeks since Marta brought Corvin out of Mecklau — following Marta under the framing of courtesy, managing the deadline pressure, conducting the estate reconnaissance on Haan's behalf. His visit to the estate as *Dressel* was nine days before the Nobles Commission Session 04 meeting.

At the estate: asked about full transformation, permanent, specific species (large working hound). Asked about reversion rights held by a third party — before asking about process, species, timeline, or cost. Already had a donor in mind. Stopped on the front step and looked at the east kennels on his way out.

In the square: waited outside the anchor inn, visible. When Isalia appeared from the Crescent House with Marta he did not move or hide. Held the meeting on the low wall with the same measured composure he brought to everything. Dropped the Corvin claim without negotiation when the real conversation opened. Named Haan. Walked east.

---

## Voice

**Default Conversational Move: Acknowledge and proceed.**
He names the weakness in his position before you find it. Not vulnerability — efficiency. *The argument has weaknesses. I'm aware of them.* Then he moves to what the position actually is.

**Quirks:**
- Unhurried. Every sentence at the same pace.
- Does not look away during important statements. When something costs him, he holds eye contact anyway.

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(Sitting on a wall, cup of something warm, facing an inn he's been watching)*
*"Fenk. Dressel was a precaution."*
*(A beat.)*
*"You have a good house. Careful people."*

**Body Language:**
**Hands:** Still. Always. Resting, not gripping.
**Eyes:** Reading continuously. He looked at you the moment you turned the corner from the Crescent House.
**Posture:** The posture of a man who has been sitting somewhere for a long time and intends to stay until the sitting is done.
