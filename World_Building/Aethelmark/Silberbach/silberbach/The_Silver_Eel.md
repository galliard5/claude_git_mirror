The Silver Eel
==============

A popular tavern in the New Town, known for good food, decent ale, and a lively atmosphere.

Description: **Location:** New Town, near Market Square

**Atmosphere:**
A warm, welcoming establishment with a large common room, a crackling hearth, and well-worn wooden tables. The walls are decorated with fishing nets, river memorabilia, and a massive silver-painted eel mounted above the bar.

**Clientele:**
Merchants, craftsmen, off-duty guild members, and travelers seeking a respectable night's rest. The Silver Eel is where business deals are made over ale, and where locals gather to share news and gossip.

**Offerings:**
- Good ale and wine at reasonable prices
- Hearty meals (river fish, stew, bread, cheese)
- Private rooms for rent upstairs
- A back room for private meetings

**Rumors:**
The taproom is a reliable source of town gossip, trade news, and occasionally darker whispers about the Count's court or the goings-on at Isalia's Manor.

Location: New Town
