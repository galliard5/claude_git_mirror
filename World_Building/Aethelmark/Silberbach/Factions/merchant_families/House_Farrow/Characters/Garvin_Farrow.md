# Garvin Farrow

Merchant. Operates Farrow & Co. Mercantile, Silberbach. Not affiliated with House Farrow — coincidence of name only, or a deliberate confusion he cultivates.

---

## Known Details

Married to Jesmine Farrow. Physically abusive — documented bruising on Jesmine confirmed by Dr. Korr. Used a collar as a control mechanism.

Forged Jesmine's witness signature on a transformation contract with Isalia's Manor, booking her for full feline conversion under his specified parameters. Filed the appointment under the name Vale, paid in advance through Farrow & Co. The contract would have bound Jesmine as manor property for approximately twenty-three years under the debt terms.

Sent a letter to the manor detailing his "wife's" desired transformation before she arrived independently. Was unaware she had gone herself — believed she was complying. Currently believes she is recovering at the manor from a difficult transformation procedure. This cover holds only as long as Isalia needs it to.
