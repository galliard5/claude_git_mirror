House Meerhold
==============

Barony. Coastal territory (distant). Controls salt production and sea trade routes. Wealthy but politically isolated.

Details: **Crest:** A blue wave with a white salt crystal
**Colors:** Blue and white
**Importance:** Controls salt production and sea trade routes. Wealthy but distant — their coastal territory is three days' ride from Silberbach. Politically isolated, they rarely attend court. Their salt is essential for food preservation. Currently negotiating a marriage alliance with House Rothwyn.
