Lukas Steinfeld
===============

Cousin to Viktor Steinfeld. Managed the Steinfeld estate for two months following the Baron's death, pending Viktor's return. Currently acting as Viktor's primary administrator.

appearance: Lean, careful eyes, the Steinfeld jaw. Ink stain on the right index finger. Dresses practically for a man of his station. Carries himself with the quiet confidence of someone who has spent two months being competent under pressure.

personality: Methodical and precise. Processes things out loud when thinking, which is useful to work with. Dry humor that surfaces when things are most serious. Does not make decisions beyond his authority — holds everything carefully until the right person can act on it.

Race: Human

Status: Estate Administrator (acting)

background: Second in line to the House Steinfeld title. Effective irrelevance as heir gave him freedom to develop real competence rather than political instincts. Viktor trusted him with the estate during transition; he repaid that trust by pulling three years of maintenance ledgers within his first week, identifying the embezzlement pattern, and holding everything neatly for Viktor's arrival rather than acting unilaterally.

Key Actions Taken:
- Maintained estate operations cleanly for two months
- Pulled and reviewed three years of maintenance ledgers
- Identified fictitious contractors (Brenn and Sons, Hartwell Works) and falsified completion records
- Consulted Aldren re: Brennmark deposit — letter drafted, waiting for Viktor's seal
- Kept Oswin in post without tipping hand
- Stacked all evidence neatly on the study desk

Voice: Measured, precise. Does not pad sentences. Comfortable making arguments and then deferring to Viktor's judgment. Dry observations that function as analysis.

Quirks:
- Says difficult things plainly and lets them sit
- Gets quieter when something is serious rather than louder
- Uses "approximately" when he doesn't have an exact figure, then gets the exact figure

Independent Goal: Ensure the Steinfeld estate becomes a model of clean administration — his two months of holding everything together has given him a personal stake in getting it right.

Relationships:
- Viktor Steinfeld — full trust, genuine working partnership
- Oswin Brandt — professional neutrality maintained under pressure; no personal animosity, no illusions
- Aldren — reserves judgment pending Voss and Kraemer assessment

Trust Level (4/5): High. Earned through demonstrated action.

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** Low in physical confrontation — Lukas is a man of ledgers and careful thinking, not a fighter.
**Loyalty:** To the estate's integrity and to Viktor.
**Breaking Point:** Immediately in any physical situation. He removes himself, secures the documents, and finds the right person.
