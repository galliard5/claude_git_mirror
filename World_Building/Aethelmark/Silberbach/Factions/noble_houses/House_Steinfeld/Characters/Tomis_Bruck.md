Tomis Bruck
===========

Son of Edric Bruck. Works the Bruck farm alongside his father. Quiet, capable, takes after his father — the same build starting to form, still with the unfinished quality of a man not quite thirty.

appearance: Not quite thirty. Edric's build in an earlier version. Hands that have done outdoor work since he was young. Listens more than he speaks.

personality: Speaks when he has something worth saying. Watches situations carefully before entering them. His offer to supply labor for the drainage repair was made quietly, while his father said nothing — neither endorsing nor contradicting. That's the dynamic.

Race: Human

Status: Tenant Farmer (working)

Age: Late twenties

background: Grew up on the Bruck farm. Elder brother left for Silberbach trades. Tomis stayed. Has been running the daily work alongside his father for years, increasingly picking up what his father's back can no longer do without either of them naming it.

Key Moments — First Viktor Meeting:
- Watched arrival with open curiosity rather than father's careful stillness
- First words spoken: offered his own and his father's labor for the drainage repair if materials were supplied
- Produced the midday meal in the kitchen without being asked, sized it practically
- Listened throughout with the attention of someone learning how these rooms work

Voice: First words are always worth hearing because he chose them. Does not decorate statements.

Independent Goal: Learn how estate relationships work — he'll be managing this tenancy eventually and he's paying attention now.

Relationships:
- Edric Bruck: quiet partnership, complete deference on major matters while carrying the physical load
- Viktor Steinfeld: respectful, watching

Trust Level (3/5): No history yet — but the first impression is solid.

**Loyalty:** To his father and the farm. To Viktor insofar as Viktor is someone who does what he says.
**Breaking Point:** Situations that leave his father exposed. Otherwise he holds and thinks.
