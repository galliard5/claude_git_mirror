House Kaelen
============

Barons of the Ford. Reform-minded family that advocates for transformed rights.

Crest & Colors: **Crest:** A black iron ford (bridge) over silver water, with a red heart above
**Colors:** Black, silver, and red
**Motto:** "All May Cross"

Holdings: **Primary Seat:** Kaelen Hall at the Ford
**Territory:** The crossing point of the Angerap River, controlling the main trade route
**Vassals:** None (barony, vassal to House Valtor)
**Income:** Bridge tolls, river crossing fees, horse breeding

Key Members: - **Baron Torvin Kaelen** — Current ruler, reform-minded, advocates for transformed rights
- **Baroness Elara Kaelen** — His wife, from a merchant family, shares his progressive views
- **Young Baron Aldric Kaelen** — Heir, studying law, hopes to change the system from within

Politics & Secrets: **Alliances:**
- Sympathetic to House Kreiger (Duke Romaine's progressive policies)
- Economic ties to House Vale (merchant)

**Rivalries:**
- House Valtor (ideological — Kaelen opposes the Count's treatment of transformed individuals)

**Secret Agenda:**
Baron Torvin is quietly building a coalition to push for legal reforms protecting transformed individuals. He sees Anthony's case as a potential catalyst for change. His wife's merchant connections provide funding for his political activities.

**Relationship to Manor:**
Quiet supporters. The Baron has offered to help any transformed individual who wishes to leave service and live independently. Isalia respects his ideals but worries his activism might draw unwanted attention to the manor.
