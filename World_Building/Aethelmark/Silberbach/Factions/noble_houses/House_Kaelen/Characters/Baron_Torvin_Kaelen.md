Baron Torvin Kaelen
===================

Human, Male, Baron. Reform-minded, advocates for transformed rights.

appearance: undefined

personality: **Core Trait: Reformist conviction.**
Kaelen believes law should evolve to reflect justice rather than tradition.

**Flaw: Political impatience.**
He sometimes pushes reforms faster than the political environment can tolerate. He is a man of action who finds the slow wheels of bureaucracy and noble tradition stifling.

background: Baron controlling the river ford and key trade crossing in the valley. Torvin Kaelen is a reform-minded noble who advocates for transformed rights. He views the current legal framework governing transformed individuals as antiquated and unjust, often putting him at direct odds with Count Valtor and more conservative houses. His family has governed the Ford for generations, but Torvin is the first to actively challenge the status quo regarding the treatment of those who have undergone transformation.
