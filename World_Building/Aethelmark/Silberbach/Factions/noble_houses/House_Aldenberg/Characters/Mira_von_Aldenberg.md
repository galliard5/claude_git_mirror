Mira von Aldenberg / Storm
==========================

Born: Mira von Aldenberg, youngest daughter of Baroness Elfriede von Aldenberg.
Now: Storm — a Meerhold-line dapple-gray stallion, registered to House Steinfeld.

---

appearance: **As Storm:**
A dapple-gray stallion of Meerhold breeding — the coat confirmed by Alric's assessment at transformation. Shoulders broad, haunches powerful, gait carrying the precision of Meerhold line even before training. The brand on her haunch is fresh, still slightly raised: a stylized S encircled by Steinfeld's crest.

Her new body is still unfamiliar. She moves with more confidence than most first-week transformations — Meerhold stock carries itself well — but the intelligence behind her eyes is readable to anyone paying attention.

**As Mira:**
Delicate features, honey-blonde hair, fine silk and wool in Aldenberg colors. Moved with the deliberate grace of someone accustomed to quality and trying not to show it. Smelled of lavender and fear.

---

personality: **Core Trait:** Resolved defiance. Mira did not flee passively — she planned, researched the contracts, and chose this specifically. She is not someone who was pushed into a corner and broke; she is someone who identified her options and selected the most complete one available.

**Flaw:** Resolution can shade into rigidity. She committed fully before she had experienced what she was committing to. The willingness to endure whatever comes next is real, but the knowledge of what comes next is still forming.

---

background: The betrothal to Count Valtor's nephew was politically arranged and non-negotiable by conventional means. Mira's sister had already suffered at his hands — the story went unsaid but the implication landed clearly. Mira had no intention of repeating it.

She researched Isalia's contracts thoroughly before arriving. Knew the transformation was permanent, knew the reversion rights remained with the estate, knew what she was signing. She signed it.

Her disappearance will be framed as a runaway. Aldenberg's guard has already been making inquiries near the manor's western pastures. Baron Fenrik Kaelen — her brother — has provided funds to Isalia as a precautionary measure and been told his sister is safe.

She chose the name Storm herself. Chose a stallion's form — not a mare's — and was clear about why. She is now registered in Steinfeld's books under Meerhold lineage, with falsified but meticulous provenance papers. Viktor Steinfeld, himself a transformed noble managing his own estate, will be her primary contact at Steinfeld.

Race: Human → Meerhold dapple-gray stallion (full transformation, permanent)

Location: House Steinfeld. Quarantine paddock on arrival; bachelor herd thereafter. Assigned the corner stall — quietest sightlines to the pastures, per Sable's arrangement.
