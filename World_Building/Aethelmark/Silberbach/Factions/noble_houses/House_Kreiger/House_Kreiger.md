House Kreiger
=============

Duchy. Romaine's principality encompasses his granted ducal lands, within which he created the County of the Dino Forest specifically for their marriage — giving Iaxiandra's long-held dragon territory formal standing within the realm's structure. One of the most quietly powerful noble houses in the region, with direct ties to Emperor Aldric himself.

Crest & Colors: **Crest:** A blue dragon coiled around an oak tree, roots and crown intertwined
**Colors:** Deep blue and forest green
**Motto:** "Root and Wing"

Holdings: **Primary Seat:** The Kreiger Principality — Romaine's duchy, within which the County of the Dino Forest sits as a formally created vassal territory
**Territory:** Romaine's principality with the County of the Dino Forest as an internal county, created at marriage specifically to give Iaxiandra's existing dragon holdings a legal title and standing. The county interior functions as a nature reserve; border settlements handle administration and trade.
**Vassals:** Several minor noble families
**Income:** Ducal taxation, trade route tolls, alchemical and druidic consultancy, manor estate operations (Isalia's estate)

Key Members:
- **Duke Romaine Kreiger** — Ruling Duke, retired 20th-level Archdruid, former adventuring companion of Emperor Aldric. Age 120, appears late 20s due to Timeless Body. Helped develop the transformation potions that form the basis of Isalia's manor services.
- **Duchess Iaxiandra Kreiger, Countess of the Dino Forest** — Duchess Kreiger by marriage, Countess of the Dino Forest by the county Romaine created for their union. Blue dragon presented as a blue kobold; open about her true nature. The dominant partner privately. Biological father of Isalia — Romaine chose to carry the egg deliberately, at a point when he had full control over his own form.
- **Isalia Kreiger** — Their daughter. Retired 20th-level Totem Warrior Barbarian. Owner and operator of [[Isalias_Manor_Kennels_and_Stables]] outside Silberbach.

Politics & Influence:
**Imperial Connection:**
House Kreiger holds imperial ties across two generations. Duke Romaine adventured alongside the previous Emperor — Aldric's father — before his ascension, forging a bond that outlasted both their campaigns and shaped the duchy's charter in ways that have never been fully tested by regional authority.

Isalia served alongside Prince Aldric before he became Emperor — roughly forty years ago, in the campaigns of his youth. That bond is personal and direct. The current Emperor knows her by name, by deed, and by shared history. Romaine's connection was to the father; Isalia's is to the son. Between them, the Kreiger family has not faced an Emperor who was a stranger in living memory.

This layered connection is what makes the estate's imperial protections structurally sound rather than merely inherited. Count Valtor petitioning the imperial magistrates is not just politically difficult — it is an action that would eventually reach the desk of a man who once fought beside Isalia Kreiger.

**Archdruid Authority:**
Romaine's position as Archdruid carries institutional religious authority across most of the continent — analogous to an Archbishop in the Church. Druidic councils seek his counsel. His word carries weight in matters of natural law and sacred tradition far beyond his duchy.

**Regional Standing:**
Duke Romaine is quietly sympathetic to House Kaelen's reform movement and progressive policies regarding transformed individuals. His support for Isalia's manor is absolute and understated — he trusts her to run it without requiring updates.

**Current Situation:**
Hannah Voss of the Steinfeld estate is en route to Isalia's manor carrying a verbal message regarding Aldus Corvel's money laundering network and the appearance of House Aldenberg on his client list. Given the regional and potentially imperial implications — Aldenberg supplies Valtor's weapons, Valtor is the most powerful house in the valley — this information is likely to reach Duke Romaine's attention through Isalia.

Rivalries:
- **Count Elias Valtor** — distrusts Isalia's estate, fears its legal implications, views the Kreiger influence as a check on his authority
- **Traditional nobility** — a kobold Duke and dragon Duchess challenge conventional hierarchies simply by existing and thriving

Alliances:
- **Emperor Aldric** — personal bond, imperial protection
- **House Kaelen** — shared progressive values regarding transformed individuals
- **House Rennic** — marriage ties

The Manor Connection:
Isalia's Manor Kennels & Stables operates as a private institution, not a house asset — but the Kreiger name provides it with a protective shadow. Anyone moving against the manor moves against the daughter of a Duke with imperial connections and an Archdruid's authority. This has kept the estate's more controversial operations from facing serious legal challenge despite Count Valtor's hostility.
