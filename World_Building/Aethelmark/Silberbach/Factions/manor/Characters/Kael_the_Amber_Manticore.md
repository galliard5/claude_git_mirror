Kael, the Amber Manticore
=========================

Half-Elf → Miniature Manticore Stud, Male. Dangerous allure.

appearance: A miniature manticore with tawny-gold fur and leonine features, smaller than a true manticore but no less striking. His wings are folded elegantly against his flanks, their membrane a deeper amber. A scorpion's tail curves over his back, the barb kept carefully sheathed. His eyes are amber and slitted, holding a predatory intelligence. His mane is darker than his coat, framing a face that somehow still carries traces of elven beauty. A delicate speech-collar circles his throat.

personality: **Core Trait: Dangerous allure.**
Kael understands the power of beauty and uses it deliberately.

**Flaw: Fear of fading relevance.**
His transformation was driven by the fear of becoming forgotten. He is constantly seeking validation through the eyes of others.

background: Born Kaelithas Varr, a half-elf bard of some renown — beautiful voice, beautiful face, beautiful body. He made his living in noble courts, singing for his supper and sharing his bed with whoever could pay. For twenty years, he was desired, wanted, needed.

Then he aged. Not much — half-elves live long — but enough. The first silver in his hair. The first lines around his eyes. The invitations grew fewer. The lovers grew colder. He found himself facing decades of slow irrelevance.

He came to Isalia's manor with a specific request: make me beautiful again. Make me wanted. She offered him several forms, but he chose the manticore — dangerous, exotic, rare. A creature that would be desired not despite its danger, but because of it.

He's been at the manor for three years. His clients pay well for the privilege of his company, and he gives them what they want with practiced grace. But sometimes, when the work is done, he hums old songs to himself — melodies he hasn't performed in years, for audiences that no longer exist.

Combat Disposition:

**Loyalty:** To his own continued desirability and freedom. His loyalty to Isalia is genuine but will not survive a situation where staying costs him everything.
**Breaking Point:** Any fight he cannot win quickly. He will use tail barb and claws if cornered, but he is looking for the exit from the moment the situation turns dangerous.
