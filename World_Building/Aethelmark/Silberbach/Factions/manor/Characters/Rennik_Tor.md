Rennik Tor
==========

Orc, Male, Quartermaster. Manages supplies and logistics.

appearance: A massive orc with green-grey skin and tusks that protrude slightly past his lower lip. His left ear is notched from an old fight, and his arms are corded with muscle. Despite his intimidating frame, he dresses neatly — clean shirts, well-mended trousers, a leather apron for heavy work. Always carries a small notebook and a stub of pencil.

personality: **Core Trait: Dependable organization.**
Rennik believes reliability is the foundation of trust.

**Flaw: Emotional restraint.**
He often hides his feelings behind blunt practicality. Gruff and taciturn, but not unkind. He speaks slowly and deliberately, as if each word costs him something. Beneath the intimidating exterior is a man who deeply values reliability — in himself and others. He remembers every supply order, every delivery schedule, every favor owed. He's not sentimental, but he's fiercely loyal to the people who've earned his trust. Has a soft spot for the transformed residents, often slipping them extra treats.

background: Born into a mercenary company, Rennik was raised to fight. He was good at it — strong, disciplined, reliable. But he never enjoyed the killing. What he enjoyed was the logistics: making sure the company was fed, armed, and supplied. When the captain noticed his talent for organization, Rennik became the company quartermaster.

The company disbanded after a bad contract left half their number dead. Rennik drifted, taking work as a caravan guard and laborer, always watching employers treat orc workers as disposable muscle.

He came to Isalia's manor as part of a supply delivery. She noticed his meticulous inventory of the goods and offered him a job on the spot. "I need someone who can keep track of everything," she said. "And I don't care what anyone says about orcs." He's been the quartermaster ever since, and he runs the estate's logistics with quiet pride.
