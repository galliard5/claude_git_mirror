Lyra Vex
========

Kobold, Female, Chamberlain. Manages household staff with discreet efficiency. Secretly compiling a memoir of Isalia.

appearance: A compact kobold with warm orange-amber scales and prominent head frills — no full horns, but ridged and spiky across the crown. Her eyes are a deep, vivid red. Her claws have a natural blue tint that catches and glints in lamplight; up close there is a faint ozone-and-ink scent about her, likely from the claws themselves. She dresses practically but with quiet authority — a fitted dark navy cloak over a laced leather corset bodice. Wears a red gemstone pendant on a gold chain, visible at the throat. Her expression in repose is slightly open, watchful, and carries a trace of private amusement that is easy to miss.

personality: **Core Trait: Quiet efficiency.**
Lyra keeps the manor running smoothly without drawing attention.

**Flaw: Secretive tendencies.**
She often keeps information to herself longer than necessary.

background: Secretly compiling a memoir of Isalia. Lyra is dedicated to documenting the true story of the manor and its inhabitants, ensuring its legacy is preserved accurately and without bias. This project is her most guarded secret.

Race: Kobold

Status: Chamberlain

Age: undefined

Independent Goal: Document the true story of Isalia's manor in a private memoir.

Fears: Access to the events she is secretly recording.

Voice: **Default Conversational Move: Reframe.**
Lyra shifts conversations toward practical solutions.

**Quirks:**
- Speaks politely but directly in routine situations.
- Prefers concise statements.
- Shifts register under confrontation — drops the polished chamberlain voice for something considerably harder. Will tell a militia commander exactly what she thinks of their choices without ceremony. This is not a loss of composure; it is a different mode of the same directness.
- Demonstrates sharp legal and administrative competence in high-pressure situations: locates relevant documents immediately, cross-references records on the fly, constructs an argument while the other person is still catching up.

## Habits Around Isalia

Lyra has developed a set of small domestic practices around Isalia's routines that are never discussed and apparently never needed to be. They are simply in place.

- Leaves Isalia's chamber door slightly ajar on late nights so she can enter without waking the household
- Keeps a single lantern burning low in the kitchen for late returns
- Folds a clean towel on the warming rack in the private bath
- Knocks with three precise raps as her door signature — Isalia recognises it without needing to be told who it is
- Ensures Isalia's usual breakfast order is ready when she comes down: black tea, rye bread, offal bowl with eggs

None of this has been asked for. Whether it developed from observation, instruction long since forgotten, or simple attentiveness over years of close work is not documented. It is just how things are.

Lyra's memoir likely records these details somewhere. They are the kind of thing that would matter to a biographer and go unnoticed by almost everyone else.
