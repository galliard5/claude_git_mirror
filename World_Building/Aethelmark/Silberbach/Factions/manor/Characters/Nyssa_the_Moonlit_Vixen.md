Nyssa, the Moonlit Vixen
========================

Transformed breeder resident at Isalia's Manor. A silver fox with fur that gleams like moonlight on snow, her coat thick and luxurious.

appearance: A silver fox with fur that gleams like moonlight on snow, her coat thick and luxurious. She's smaller than the other transformed residents, delicate and quick. Her eyes are bright amber, holding a mischievous spark that never quite fades. Her tail is full and plume-like, often held high with playful energy. The Broodmother's Brand marks her lower belly, its pawprint dark against her pale fur. A speech-collar circles her throat, nearly hidden in her ruff.

personality: **Core Trait: Mischievous devotion.** 
Nyssa mixes playful charm with deep emotional loyalty.

**Flaw: Hidden grief.**
Her daughter lives nearby as a noble's ward and does not know Nyssa is her true mother. This vulnerability is carefully guarded behind a mask of wit and playfulness.

background: Born Nyssa Vane, a human woman of common birth who caught the eye of a minor noble. He took her as his mistress, gave her a comfortable life, and when she bore him a daughter, he took the child to raise as his legitimate heir — with another woman as the mother on paper.

Nyssa was dismissed, paid off, forgotten. But she couldn't forget. She watched from a distance as her daughter grew up in luxury, never knowing the woman who gave her life.

When the noble's wife learned the truth, she began making arrangements to have Nyssa "disappeared." Nyssa fled, finding her way to Isalia's manor. She chose the silver fox form for its beauty and its cunning — and because it would let her stay near her daughter.

The noble's family sometimes visits the manor on business, bringing the daughter along. Nyssa watches from the kennels, never revealing herself, aching to speak to her child but knowing she cannot. The Broodmother's Brand she wears binds her to her new life, but her heart remains divided.
