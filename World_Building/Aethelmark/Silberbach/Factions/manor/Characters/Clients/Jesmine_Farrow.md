# Jesmine Farrow

Wife of Garvin Farrow, merchant operating as Farrow & Co. Mercantile. Currently under sanctuary at Isalia's Manor, west wing. Came in presenting as a voluntary transformation client. The situation resolved otherwise.

---

## Appearance

Well-dressed in merchant-class clothing, high lace collar. Bruising visible on the wrist beneath the sleeve — documented by Dr. Korr. Carries herself with the careful stillness of someone long practiced at not drawing attention.

---

## How She Arrived

Arrived at the manor with a sketch of a feline transformation form — elongated limbs, serpentine tail — claiming it represented her wishes. The sketch smelled of cheap ink and amateur enchantment work. The actual story emerged in private consultation with Isalia.

Her husband Garvin had pre-signed a transformation contract on her behalf with a forged witness signature and filed it under the name Vale (third tier alchemical consultation, paid in advance through Wesler & Co.). The contract bound her as manor property until the transformation debt cleared — approximately twenty-three years at quoted rates. She had never seen the contract, never consented, and her true signature bore no resemblance to the forged witness line.

She had come to the manor independently, ahead of the contract's activation date, intending to ask for help. She did not know the contract already existed until Lyra produced it.

---

## The Three Options Offered

Isalia presented three paths:

1. **Sign and accept** — undergo the transformation Garvin specified, enter the debt contract as written. Rejected immediately.
2. **New contract** — scrapped the original, drafted fresh terms. Jesmine chooses her form (if any), works for the manor under approved conditions, quarter of net earnings until debt clears.
3. **Legal action** — the forged signature constitutes felony forgery. Garvin Farrow owes debts to four creditors including House Steinfeld's factor. With Jesmine's testimony, a signed warrant could be obtained within a day and constabulary enforcement within the week.

**Jesmine chose option three.**

---

## Current Status

- Sanctuary granted, west wing, overlooking herb gardens

- Testimony being recorded — full account of abuse, coercion, and the collar
- Dr. Korr examining bruising and documenting injuries formally
- Garvin Farrow has been sent a letter stating his wife's transformation was difficult but she is recovering at the manor — buying time before he acts
- Warrant process initiated through Lyra; Garvin's debt records obtained and cross-referenced

## Character Notes

Not broken — frightened, but thinking clearly once she understood she had options. Moved quickly once the decision was made. The moment Lyra showed her the forged signature beside her real one, she didn't hesitate.

Quiet. Doesn't volunteer information but answers directly when asked. Has been managing a dangerous domestic situation for long enough that careful speech is habitual.

The bruising is not new. The collar was not decorative.
