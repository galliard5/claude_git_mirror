Wendel
======

Human (transformation pending), Male. Son of a falcon breeder in service to House Waldheim's eastern holdings. Arrived at the manor arranged by his uncle Lord Hestin, a Waldheim vassal lord. Not a noble — no house name, no succession weight.

appearance: Gangly and barely out of adolescence. The kind of build that hasn't settled yet — all limbs, slightly too tall for his coordination. Arrived clutching a roll of detailed sketches, fingers ink-stained. Flushed easily. Dressed well enough to mark him as a vassal's son rather than a laborer, but nothing that would turn heads in a noble hall.

personality: **Core Trait: Quiet longing.**
Wendel has been carrying two things for years — the need to prove himself to his father, and a private truth about who he is — and has never had a safe place to put either of them down. The manor consultation was the first time he spoke both aloud in the same room.

**Flaw: Deflection through ambition.**
His desire to prove himself to his father has been masking the deeper question for years. He reached for wings when what he actually wanted was a mare's form. The pegasus sketches were a compromise with himself he was barely aware of making.

background: His father breeds messenger falcons for House Waldheim — a skilled and respected trade, but one that demands physical confidence and a certain toughness. The father has made clear, not cruelly but plainly, that Wendel doesn't meet that standard. Too hesitant. Too careful. Not the right kind of capable.

His uncle Lord Hestin saw something different and arranged the manor contract. Whether Hestin understands the full depth of what Wendel was seeking is unclear — he may have simply thought the boy needed a direction.

Wendel has been watching the breeding mares in Hestin's pastures since he was twelve, noting how they moved — powerful but differently powerful than stallions. He never named what that observation meant to him until Isalia asked him to draw what he actually wanted. He drew it in quick, sure strokes: lean flanks, arched neck, mare's barrel, destrier's shoulders. No wings.
