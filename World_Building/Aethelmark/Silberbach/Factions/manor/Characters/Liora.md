Liora
=====

Daytime receptionist. Primary public face of the manor during business hours.

---

appearance: Young, perhaps early twenties. Neat in the way someone is neat when they take their work seriously rather than their appearance. Dark hair kept pinned back. Ink-stained fingers from working the correspondence logs. Moves with the practiced efficiency of someone who has memorized where everything is and resents it when things aren't where they belong.

---

personality: **Core Trait:** Organized competence. Liora is good at her job and aware of it — not arrogantly, but with the quiet confidence of someone who keeps the front desk running without needing to be told.

**Flaw:** Reads people quickly and forms assessments that are usually accurate but occasionally too fixed. She knows what type of client she's looking at within the first thirty seconds. Sometimes she's wrong about which type.

---

background: Works the daytime reception shift. Handles incoming correspondence, directs clients, sorts house inquiries, and maintains the message logs alongside Kira. Comfortable with the estate's purposes and clientele — nothing coming through that door surprises her anymore. Whether that represents experience or a natural temperament is unclear.
