Industrial Shops
================

Smithing, alchemy lab, and enchanting workshop.

Description: **Overview:**
A cluster of workshop buildings near the livestock barn, separated from the main building by practicality — fire risk, noise, and smell. Each shop has its own master and apprentices.

**Areas:**

*Smithy:*
A traditional forge with anvil, quenching trough, and workbenches. Produces practical metalwork — collar fittings, restraint hardware, branding irons. The smith (a dwarf named Haldrek) also handles repairs for the estate. Smoke vents through a tall chimney.

*Alchemy Lab:*
Alric Dain's domain. A stone building with reinforced walls (for safety). Approached via a narrow corridor lined with glass-fronted cabinets — their panes fogged with condensation, jars of murky liquids glowing faintly behind the glass. The corridor smells of alcohol and something bitter, like burnt coffee. A brass-bound door at the far end opens into the main workroom: a long bench strewn with vials and a single long-necked flask that glows faintly gold when active work is underway. Distillation apparatus, ingredient storage, and work tables fill the rest of the space. Transformation potions are brewed here, along with medicinal compounds and the famous reversion elixir. Strict safety protocols — no open flames.

*Enchanting Workshop:*
Elowen Vale's sanctuary. A quieter space, almost library-like, with rune-carved workbenches and shelves of reference texts. Magical items are crafted and maintained here — collars with speech enchantments, portal devices, branded sigils. The room hums faintly with stored magic. Elowen tolerates no disruptions when working.

*Storage Shed:*
Raw materials — metals, herbs, gemstones, monster parts — kept under lock and key. Inventory managed jointly by the workshop masters and Rennik the quartermaster.
