Market Square
=============

The bustling heart of the New Town, where weekly markets and daily commerce take place.

Overview: The beating heart of the New Town, Market Square is where Silberbach's commerce converges. Permanent stalls cluster around a central stone fountain, and on market days the square fills with farmers, craftsmen, and merchants from the surrounding countryside.

**Market Days:**
Weekly markets draw crowds from miles around. Special seasonal markets — harvest fairs, wool markets, and the like — bring even larger gatherings.

**Permanent Establishments:**
- **The Fountain** — A stone fountain depicting river spirits, a popular meeting place
- **Market Stalls** — Permanent wooden structures for established merchants
- **The Town Crier's Post** — Where official announcements are made
- **Street Vendors** — Food sellers, entertainers, and hawkers
