Isalia's Steel Plug
===================

A personal item. Not for sale, not on display, not discussed.
duplicates available for sale in the manor shop
---

## Description

Polished steel, heavier than it looks. The base is fitted with a small locking mechanism — a twist-and-click engagement that holds the plug in place and requires a deliberate motion to release. The lock is not elaborate. It does not need to be.

The steel has the same quality as the Tantalus Tease — smooth beyond what conventional smithing produces, the surface carrying a faint warmth even when unattended. Whether this is an artifact of the enchantment or the metal itself is not documented.

---

## Properties

**Self-Cleaning:** The primary enchantment. The plug maintains itself — no residue, no degradation, no maintenance required. Removing it produces a clean, dry surface regardless of how long it has been worn. This is the practical feature that makes nightly use unobtrusive.

**Locking Mechanism:** The twist-lock engages on insertion and holds until deliberately released. Not magically sealed — a physical mechanism that requires intention to operate. Simple and reliable.

---

## Use

Worn nightly as part of Isalia's private routine before sleep. Has been part of that routine long enough that it is simply what she does, in the same category as the decanter on the shelf — consistent, personal, unremarked upon.

The plug is kept beside the washbasin in her private quarters. Staff who have been in her rooms have seen it. Most have not asked. The few who know what it is treat the knowledge the same way they treat most things about Isalia's private life: with the discretion the estate generally runs on.
