## Transformation Services

*Intake & pricing: [[The_Apothecary]] | Brewed by: [[Alric_Dain]] ([[Industrial_Shops]]) | Monitored by: [[Infirmary]]*

Three variants offered. All standard contracts include the reversion elixir unless permanence is explicitly signed for.

**Genital-Only Transformation**
Transforms the external and internal genital anatomy to the target species template. Form only — reproduction remains that of the client's original species. The Reproductive Conversion Compound (see below) can be taken simultaneously if the client wishes breeding function to match the new form.

Requires consultation with Alric to confirm compound viability for the requested species. Reversion standard.

**Partial Transformation**
Selective transformation covering specified regions, features, or systems — ears, limbs, hide, tail, sensory apparatus, and combinations thereof. Scope defined at intake. Form only — reproduction remains that of the client's original species regardless of what anatomy is changed. The Reproductive Conversion Compound can be taken simultaneously.

Alric brews to specification; outside convenience stock, expect a production lead time. Reversion standard unless partial permanence is specified and signed for.

**Full Transformation**
Complete conversion to the target species form. Mind is preserved unless the Mental Regression add-on is selected. As with partial transformation, reproduction does not automatically convert — the Reproductive Conversion Compound is bundled automatically with full transformation contracts and administered at the same time. This is not optional and is not waivable: a fully transformed humanoid carrying a humanoid fetus presents serious and well-documented risks to both patient and pregnancy. The bundling is a health requirement, not a preference.

*Convenience stock (both sexes where applicable):* wolfhound, destrier, doe, boar, vixen.
*Custom species:* any species Alric has studied can be commissioned directly. Lead time varies.

**Reproductive Conversion Compound** *(add-on for genital-only and partial; bundled with full)*
A separate compound that converts the client's reproductive biology — gametes, cycle, gestation compatibility — to match the target transformation species. Has no effect on external form; it is purely internal and functional. Can be taken simultaneously with the form potion with no interaction risk.

For genital-only and partial transformations this is an opt-in. For full transformation it is included automatically — see above. Reversion of reproductive conversion requires the partial reversion compound; consult Alric and Dr. Korr.

**Mental Regression Add-On** *(full transformation only)*
Optional. Reduces the patient's cognitive and instinctual state to that of the animal form — appetite, impulse, and perception become those of the beast. Speech, complex memory, and self-reflection are permanently lost.

*This procedure is one-way and irreversible. No reversion elixir or alchemical means can undo it.*

Verification before proceeding:
- Minimum two separate consultations on different days, with a mandatory waiting period between them
- Written consent signed in the presence of Isalia or Lyra as witness, in addition to Dr. Korr
- Dr. Korr must be satisfied the patient is fully lucid and not acting under duress or temporary emotional crisis
- A second medical opinion is strongly preferred; Korr may refuse to proceed without one
- Any doubt → procedure postponed, not denied

Patients who complete this add-on are transitioned into the estate's animal care program and treated with the same standard of care as all residents.

## Reversion Services

*Available at: [[The_Apothecary]] | Clinical oversight: [[Infirmary]]*

Reversion elixirs are available off-shelf. A client does not need a prior estate transformation to purchase — but Dr. Selene Korr will ask questions.

**Standard Reversion**
Covers all estate-produced transformations. Brewed on a standard rotation by Alric. Kept behind the counter; not on open display.

**Partial Reversion**
Targeted system reversion — reverts specific aspects of a transformation while leaving others intact. Specialized compound; requires consultation with Alric and Dr. Korr before purchase. Not kept in standing stock.

**Emergency Reversion**
Accelerated dissolution. Higher cost, higher physical toll on the patient. Used when speed matters more than comfort. Clinical monitoring recommended during administration.

*Full or partial reversal of a transformation performed elsewhere requires assessment by Dr. Korr and Alric before a compound can be confirmed appropriate. Success rate varies by time elapsed since original transformation and the potion type originally used.*

## Medical & Surgical Services

*Location: [[Infirmary]] | Provider: [[Dr_Selene_Korr]]*

Dr. Korr's dual veterinary-human expertise allows her to treat conditions that standard physicians will not touch.

**General Medical Care**
Routine care, wound treatment, fever and infection management, recovery monitoring. Available to staff, residents, and clients in procedure recovery. The infirmary is equipped for both human and animal patients.

**Transformed-Body Medicine**
Conditions specific to partially or fully transformed residents: metabolic strain, joint failure, sensory overload, infections that present differently in non-standard anatomies, and complications arising from long-term transformation. Ongoing care is available for permanent residents.

**Cosmetic & Body Modification**
Surgical alterations to form, features, or function. Handled case-by-case; full consultation required. Reversibility is discussed and documented prior to any procedure.

**Surgical Body Modification & Gender Affirming Procedures**
Permanent anatomical alterations including castration, penectomy, clitorectomy, hysterectomy, gender nullification, and urethral rerouting. These procedures cannot be achieved via transformation potion — potions operate on a species-template model and cannot selectively modify or remove anatomy within a single form. Surgical precision is required.

*Consent & verification:* All procedures in this category are permanent and irreversible. A minimum of one full consultation is required prior to scheduling. Written consent signed in the presence of Dr. Korr and witnessed by Isalia or Lyra. Any concern about the patient's state of mind or the presence of external pressure → procedure postponed pending a second consultation, not denied outright.
