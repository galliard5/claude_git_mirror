Isalia's Manor
==============

A pastoral estate specializing in breeding, transformation services, and magical items.

Description: A pastoral estate on the outskirts of a mid-sized river valley town. For a full catalogue of services offered, see [[Manor_Services]].

Services at a glance: breeding (feral horses and canines), permanent animal transformations (mind preserved unless requested), surrogacy & whelping, erotic modification for slaves/servants, boarding for transformed individuals, and the sale of custom sexual magical items.

**Business Ethics:**
- Only volunteers or certified slaves accepted
- No mental regression without explicit consent
- Transformed retain full legal status and rights
- Reversion potion is cheap and included unless permanence is specified

**Staff Policy:**
- Competence > lineage or title
- Staff treated as equals; no groveling
- Free room, board, medical care, above-average pay
- Bonuses for erotic service roles
- Voluntary permanent roles include stipend
- Transformed staff wear collars with speech enchantment (can be disabled)

Garrison & Security: **Size:** 40 soldiers (half levy, half mercenaries)
**Commander:** [[Captain_Albrecht_Vogt|Captain Albrecht Vogt]]
**Role:** Maintains order, patrols trade routes, enforces Count's authority
**Pay:** Officers are well-compensated; housing and supplies provided

Location: Situated on the outskirts of Silberbach, between the town's sprawling suburbs and the beginning of the farmland. The estate sits along the main road heading southeast out of town, following the Angerap River. Close enough for easy client access, far enough for privacy and discretion.

**Travel Times:**
- To Market Square: 20 minutes on foot, 10 by horse
- To the Old Town gates: 30 minutes on foot, 15 by horse
- To the nearest farms: 5 minutes on foot

Layout: **The Main Building (Two Floors)**

*Ground Floor:*
- **Reception Hall** — The public face of the manor, tastefully appointed
- **The Store** — Where magical items and services are purchased
- **Customer Intake** — Private rooms for consultations and contract negotiations
- **Administrative Offices** — [[Mira_Toren|Mira's]] accounting ledgers, [[Elias_Varn|Elias's]] scheduling, [[Kira_Lann|Kira's]] message center

*Second Floor:*
- **Isalia's Quarters** — [[Isalia_Kreiger|Private chambers]] accessible via main stairs from reception or back stairs from the staff area

**Attached Wing:**
- **[[Infirmary]]** / Veterinary Wing — [[Dr_Selene_Korr|Dr. Selene Korr's]] domain, equipped for both human and animal patients
- **Main Kitchen & Mess Hall** — Staff meals, large hearth, food storage
- **Staff Baths** — Shared bathing facilities with hot water
- **Staff Living Quarters** — Dormitory-style rooms for staff who reside on-site

**Outbuildings:**
- **[[Industrial_Shops]]** — Smithing, alchemy ([[Alric_Dain|Alric's]] lab), enchanting workshop ([[Elowen_Vale|Elowen's]] domain)
- **Livestock Barn** — Feeder animals for the kitchens and breeding programs
- **[[Kennels_and_Stables]]** — Premium boarding for canine and equine clients

**Grounds:**
- **Pastures** — Extensive grazing areas for boarded animals
- **Exercise Runs** — Secure areas for canine clients to stretch
- **Training Yards** — For working with transformed individuals

Atmosphere: **By Day:**
The estate hums with purposeful activity — the clang of the smith, the bubbling of alchemical apparatus, the lowing of livestock. Staff move efficiently between buildings. In the pastures, transformed residents graze or exercise under supervision. The reception hall sees a steady trickle of clients, from nervous first-timers to wealthy regulars.

**By Night:**
The industrial shops fall quiet, but the manor never truly sleeps. The kennels and stables require round-the-clock attention. The mess hall glows with hearth-light and conversation. Isalia's window often burns late, visible from the courtyard.

**The Feel:**
There's an unusual warmth here — staff greet each other as equals, not as servants. The transformed residents are treated with dignity, not as livestock. It's a place of strange purposes made ordinary through routine and respect.
