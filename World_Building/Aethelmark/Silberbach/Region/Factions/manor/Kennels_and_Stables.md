Kennels & Stables
=================

Premium boarding for canine and equine clients.

*Breeding and boarding service definitions: [[Estate_Services#Breeding and Stud Services]] | [[Estate_Services#Boarding]]*

Description: **Overview:**
The luxury boarding facilities for the manor's most valued residents — both paying clients and permanent transformed staff. Located near the main building for easy staff access, with separate entrances for canines and equines.

**Luxury Kennels:**
Premium housing for canine clients and transformed canines. Individual runs with heated floors, clean straw bedding, and outdoor access. A central aisle allows staff to monitor all residents. Larger pens accommodate direwolves and similar sized creatures. The kennels are kept immaculate — no smell, no mess. Staff rotate through for feeding, exercise, and grooming.

**Luxury Stables:**
Premium housing for equine clients and transformed horses. Wide stalls with fresh hay, automatic waterers, and grooming stations. A covered riding ring allows exercise in any weather. Tack room with custom-fitted equipment for each resident. The stable master (a transformed mare named Sable) ensures every resident receives proper care.

**Breeding Suites:**
Private, soundproofed rooms for breeding sessions — whether for paying clients or the manor's own breeding programs. Equipped with restraint frames (padded, comfortable), observation windows, and cleaning facilities. Discretion is paramount.

**The Play Area:**
A broad, straw-strewn circle at the heart of the kennel block, bordered by low wooden rails. This is the primary space for portal board sessions, open breeding, and supervised interaction between residents. The smell here is different from the rest of the kennels — earthier, warmer, unmistakably purposeful.

Along the inner rail stand several mounting posts: polished oak columns, each studded with iron rings at varying heights for restraint, positioning, and board mounting. Posts are spaced well apart to allow simultaneous use without interference. The boards for active portal contracts are mounted here during sessions, secured to the posts at the height specified in each client's contract. Between sessions, boards are stored in a locked cabinet along the east wall — maintained, cleaned, and checked daily by kennel staff.

Torchlight in the play area is kept steady and warm. The straw is turned regularly. Kennel staff are present during all active sessions; the area is never left unsupervised when a board is mounted.

**Exercise Yards:**
Fenced pastures and runs where residents can stretch, play, or train under supervision. Separate areas for different species and temperaments. Staff monitor for safety and to prevent unauthorized breeding.

**Grooming Station:**
A shared facility for bathing, brushing, and general care. Large tubs, grooming tables, and supply cabinets. Transformed residents often assist in caring for each other here.
