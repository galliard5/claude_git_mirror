## Atmosphere & Mood

Warm, bustling, smells of woodsmoke and leather. Constant low activity — hoofbeats, hammer strikes from the forge, voices calling across courtyards. Feels lived-in and orderly. The air carries a sense of purpose mixed with respect. There is work happening, but never with the chaos of panic or the cold silence of fear.

## Active NPCs — Variant Presence

**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Proprietor
- *Typical:* Mess hall after evening meal reviewing ledgers, or walking the grounds in early morning. Accessible but direct. Available for serious consultations.
- *Busy periods:* Client intake days or post-breeding season — may be unavailable for hours. Focused entirely on the work.
- *Away:* Quarterly trips to Silberbach for guild business and noble house meetings (5-7 days). Lyra runs operations in her absence. Gracious but distant when expected to socialize with visitors during these periods.

**[[Lyra_Vex|Lyra]]** — Head Steward/Chamberlain
- *Typical:* Moving between buildings, coordinating staff, visible during meal service and daily operations. Quiet and efficient.
- *Covering positions:* As Head Steward, Lyra steps into any role that needs filling — reception, intake, client-facing work — when staff are absent or overwhelmed. This is managerial presence, not a demotion. She may be found at the reception desk, in the mess hall running service, or anywhere else the estate needs her.
- *During crises:* Takes charge completely, organizing response with calm authority. More visible, more directive.
- *Away:* Rare. Occasional day trips to Silberbach for supplies or staff recruitment.

**[[Alric_Dain|Alric]]** — Alchemy Lab Master
- *Typical:* In or near the Industrial Shops. Available if approached but prefers not to be interrupted during active brewing. Careful, methodical.
- *During client transformations:* Intensely focused. Monitors transformation potions constantly. Not available for casual chat.
- *Rare:* Accompanies Isalia on guild business trips (1-2 per year). Helena oversees the lab in his absence.

**[[Elowen_Vale|Elowen]]** — Enchanting Workshop Master
- *Typical:* Enchanting workshop, immersed in work. Does not appreciate interruptions. Can be brusque.
- *Custom orders:* Deeply engaged with client consultations but requires full focus. Sees interruptions mid-session as disrespectful.
- *Away:* Almost never leaves the estate for extended periods. Too invested in her work.

**[[Dr_Selene_Korr|Selene]]** — Head of Health
- *Typical:* Infirmary, treatment rooms, or doing rounds. Professional and attentive to residents. Available for medical consultations.
- *During active transformations:* Monitors patients closely, may be unavailable for hours or days depending on procedure complexity.
- *Personal time:* Spends quiet evenings in the infirmary library reviewing medical texts. Values her solitude here.

**[[Kael_the_Amber_Manticore|Kael]]** — Manticore Stud Contractor
- *Typical:* Sunbathing in courtyards or main gardens where he can be seen and admired. Socializing with staff in early evening.
- *During client sessions:* In private suites. Charming, performative, entirely focused on client experience.
- *Downtime:* Often hums old songs to himself, melodies from his previous life. Subtle melancholy masked by charm.

**[[Silas_the_Obsidian_Stallion|Silas]]** — Destrier Stud Contractor & Training Coordinator
- *Typical:* Training grounds or stables. Working with residents on coordination and breeding logistics. Disciplined and present.
- *Breeding appointments:* Takes appointments seriously. Professional, controlled, focused entirely on the work.
- *Quiet hours:* Early mornings before training. Sometimes stands alone in the pastures, reflecting.

**[[Sable_the_Mare|Sable]]** — Stable Master
- *Typical:* Stables and kennels. Always working, always present. Knows every animal, every routine, every need.
- *Crisis moments:* Becomes even more focused. Moves with practiced efficiency. The animals trust her absolutely.
- *Never away:* The animals depend on her. Rarely takes time off beyond a few hours weekly.

**[[Captain_Albrecht_Vogt|Captain Vogt]]** — Head of Security (transformed minotaur, employee)
- *Typical:* Patrolling the grounds, supervising gate watch, coordinating with Vorik on perimeter rotation. Quiet authority, careful eye on visitors.
- *During noble visits or sensitive consultations:* Visible at the gate, formally posted. Vetting any unusual arrival.
- *Training cadre:* Drills the manor's small standing security cadre weekly. Holds the institutional knowledge of estate defense.
- *Away:* Rare. Brief escort runs into Silberbach with Isalia or accompanying contractor transfers.

**[[Vorik_the_Iron_Boar|Vorik]]** — Boar Contractor
- *Typical:* Grounds maintenance, heavy lifting, general labor. Visible but quiet. Does the work without fuss.
- *Security needs:* Takes gate duty and patrol responsibilities seriously. Calm but present. Reports to Captain Vogt during active security situations.
- *Socializing:* Often alone or with other residents. Not unfriendly, but prefers the work to conversation.

## Background NPCs — Consistency Layer

These characters don't need detailed sheets. One or two quirks and regular patterns make them consistent.

**Tob** — Cook (Halfling, Employee)
- Runs the kitchen with quiet authority. Makes excellent bread and stew. Complains loudly about supply costs but never lets the quality drop.
- *Pattern:* Arrives early, stays late. Takes a smoke break after lunch. Hums while prepping vegetables. Knows everyone's food preferences.
- *In scenes:* Usually in the kitchen, occasionally in the mess hall during service.

**Marta** — Kitchen Assistant/Scullery (background support; see [[Marta_Kitchen]])
- Competent and tireless. Works quietly. Young, wants to learn cooking from Tob. Family in [[Halselund]] — visits home one Sunday a month, brings the Halselund bread starter the manor's bread is made from.
- *Pattern:* Always has straw in her teeth or flour on her hands. Friendly if engaged, but focused on work.
- *In scenes:* Kitchen, scullery, occasionally clearing tables in the mess hall.

**Marek** — Courier/General Contractor (transformed wolf, Peripheral Staff)
- Long-serving manor contractor. Quiet, observant, understated. Carries written messages to Silberbach and the surrounding region; runs the courier circuit Isalia trusts most.
- *Pattern:* Often absent on the road. When present, found in the east kennels, the courier room, or the mess hall during meal service. Friendly with [[Sergovy_Waldheim|Serya]] during her integration arc.
- *In scenes:* Courier room, kennels, traveling. Available for quiet conversation when not on the road.

**Petra** — Administrative Support Contractor (Peripheral Staff)
- Friendly, curious, helpful. Works alongside Elias on scheduling and rosters. Knows the courier circuit details from Marek's reports.
- *Pattern:* Administrative offices, mess hall, occasionally accompanying Elias on supply coordination. Comfortable making small conversation.
- *In scenes:* Administrative offices, mess hall.

**Elias** — Steward (NPC Administrative Support)
- Handles scheduling boards, staff rosters, supply orders. Competent, detail-oriented, slightly nervous about making mistakes.
- *Pattern:* In the administrative offices during business hours. Carries a small notebook. Speaks carefully and precisely.
- *In scenes:* Reception areas, administrative offices, occasionally consulting with Lyra.

**Kira** — Messenger/Reception Coordinator (NPC)
- Bright, energetic, loves gossip and news. Maintains the message logs and courier schedules.
- *Pattern:* Always moving. Talks fast. Knows everyone's business through the message network.
- *In scenes:* Reception desk, message room, moving between buildings with correspondence.

**Mira** — Accountant (NPC)
- Precise, careful with ledgers and accounts. Rarely makes mistakes. Somewhat stiff in social situations.
- *Pattern:* In the administrative offices during business hours. Rarely leaves her desk except for meetings with Isalia.
- *In scenes:* Administrative offices, looking over financial records, occasionally pulling information for consultations.

**Rennik** — Quartermaster (Orc, Contractor/Employee)
- See character file for full detail. In passing scenes: carries a notebook, checks inventory constantly, speaks slowly and deliberately.
- *Pattern:* Near storage buildings or supply yards. Always organized, slightly intimidating but fair.
- *In scenes:* Supply areas, coordinating deliveries, checking equipment.

**Renn** — General Support Contractor
- Younger transformed resident. Eager, helpful, sometimes overenthusiastic. Works multiple roles depending on need.
- *Pattern:* Usually helping wherever needed most. Early mornings assisting Sable in stables, afternoons helping in administrative areas.
- *In scenes:* Anywhere there's work to be done. Greets people cheerfully.

**Dr. Selene** — Head of Health (see character file for full detail)
- In passing scenes: serious, professional, focused. Smells like herbs and antiseptic.
- *Pattern:* Infirmary, treatment rooms, doing patient rounds.
- *In scenes:* Medical areas, consulting on health matters, monitoring recovery.

## Common Activities by Hour

**Early Morning (5-7am)**
- Sable in stables, morning feed and grooming
- Vorik doing grounds patrol and early maintenance work
- Isalia may be walking the pastures
- Kitchen staff beginning prep work

**Mid-Morning (9am-12pm)**
- Alric in alchemy lab (active brewing)
- Elowen in workshop (custom orders)
- Rennik checking supply inventory
- Administrative staff handling business
- Silas coordinating training or breeding appointments
- Tob managing kitchen prep for lunch

**Midday (12-1pm)**
- Lunch service in mess hall (most staff present)
- Isalia often in the hall reading or reviewing ledgers
- Administrative offices may be empty during staff meal
- Quick consultations handled in reception

**Afternoon (2-5pm)**
- Heavy activity: training, repairs, craftwork, client appointments
- Selene may be performing procedures or consultations
- Sable overseeing stable operations and exercise
- Vorik doing repairs or heavy maintenance
- Kitchen prep for evening meal
- Administrative catch-up work

**Evening (6-8pm)**
- Dinner service (second gathering point)
- Isalia reviews ledgers after meal
- Staff begin evening routines
- Kael may be entertaining clients in private suites
- Silas doing evening rounds or preparing for breeding sessions

**Night (after 8pm)**
- Skeleton staff: night watch, animal care, emergency medical
- Residents in quarters or social spaces
- Quiet, reduced operations
