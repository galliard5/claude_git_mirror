Staff Wing
==========

Staff kitchen, mess hall, baths, and living quarters.

Description: **Overview:**
The heart of daily life at the manor — where staff eat, bathe, and sleep. It's a warm, lived-in space that reflects Isalia's philosophy of treating staff as equals.

**Areas:**

*Main Kitchen:*
A large, well-equipped kitchen with a massive hearth, prep tables, and storage pantries. The cook (a halfling named Tob) runs a tight operation. Meals are served three times daily, with leftovers available for night staff.

*Mess Hall:*
Long wooden tables and benches, capacity for 30-40 people at once. A smaller table near the hearth is informally reserved for senior staff, though Isalia often sits among the workers. A board on the wall posts daily schedules and announcements.

*Staff Baths:*
Communal bathing facilities with partitioned tubs and hot water piped from the kitchen boilers. Separate hours for men and women, though transformed residents with unique needs are accommodated. Clean towels and basic soap provided.

*Living Quarters:*
Dormitory-style rooms on two floors above the mess hall. Most staff share rooms (2-4 per room), with senior staff getting private chambers. Simple but comfortable — bed, chest, small desk. Transformed residents have quarters appropriate to their forms.

*Back Stairs:*
A narrow staircase connecting the staff wing to Isalia's quarters on the second floor of the main building — allowing her direct access without passing through reception.
