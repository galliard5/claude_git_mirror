Elspeth Voss
============

Human (transformation complete), Female. Daughter of Ferrik Voss, an Aldenberg merchant. Arrived at the manor under a debt-obligation contract she had not fully signed — her father had appended forged clauses after the fact using a reheated guild seal.

appearance: Pre-transformation — silk sleeves, leather-bound contract clutched in white-knuckled hands, chin lifted despite visible nerves. Fresh bruises on her wrist in the shape of fingers. Smelled of bergamot and nervous sweat. Carried herself like someone who had decided to stop being afraid and was still working out what that felt like.

Post-transformation — Barbary lioness, gold and black coloration. Full size. Fully female. Speech retained per standard contract. Claws retained — she specified them explicitly.

personality: **Core Trait: Controlled fury.**
Elspeth does not panic and she does not beg. When she arrived she was frightened; by the time she left she had a plan. The consultation did not change her circumstances — it gave her a form that matched what she already was.

**Flaw: Patience as weapon.**
She is willing to wait, to appear docile, to let her father believe he has won — specifically so the reversal lands harder. This is strategic and effective. It is also the temperament of someone who has learned that direct confrontation costs too much.

background: Daughter of Ferrik Voss, a merchant operating under the Aldenberg guild seal. The original debt contract — signed legitimately — specified standard feline transformation for domestic companionship. After Elspeth signed, her father appended additional clauses in different ink: housecat size, no speech, and behavioral diminishment. He reheated the guild seal to reattach it over the forgery.

He told her the additions were temporary, until the debt cleared. The bruises on her wrist suggest the conversation did not remain civil.

Elspeth identified the forgery herself before arrival — she noticed the wax had been reheated. She arrived not to submit but to negotiate, contract in hand, amendments prepared.

Isalia identified the forged seal independently, confirmed it against the manor's own intake records from three weeks prior, and noted that forging a guild seal to compel transformation constitutes something close to attempted murder under regional law. The original contract clauses were reinstated. The appended forgeries were voided.

Isalia then noted that "housecat" is never defined in the contract. The genus Panthera qualifies. Elspeth chose Barbary lioness.
