Anthony Valtor
==============

Human → Transformed Chestnut Gelding, Male. Free Retainer, Imperial Witness. Resilient autonomy despite full gelding.

appearance: A chestnut gelding pony, approximately 15 hands, with a coat the color of polished copper when groomed. His most striking features are magical: a shadow-collar of Primordial steel fused to his throat, pulsing with his heartbeat; a branded pawprint on his flank that glows when lies are spoken nearby; and an indigo-blue dragon scale earring in his left ear — a Mother's Molt from Duchess Iaxiandra herself. His brand reads "7-9-2" beneath the pawprint, a relic of his brief time as livestock. Fully gelded — no genitals remain. Moves with deliberate certainty, each hooffall echoing the rhythm of a battle chant only he can hear.

personality: **Core Trait: Resilient autonomy.**
Anthony didn't just survive his transformation; he claimed it. He refuses to define himself by the circumstances others imposed on him.

**Flaw: Lingering anger.**
His experiences with his father and noble politics make him distrustful of authority. He carries a deep-seated resentment that flares up when he feels managed or coerced.

background: Born Anthony Valtor, minor son of Gerran Valtor (steward to Count Elias Valtor). When his father arranged to send him to religious school, Anthony ran — but not away. He went to Isalia's Manor and paid for his own transformation into a gelding pony, knowing his father would "buy" him back as a disposal. The plan: disappear legally, escape his father's control.

What Anthony didn't know: his father used embezzled imperial silver to pay for the transformation, falsified levy reports, and intended to erase his son entirely. When Duke Romaine and Duchess Iaxiandra arrived to investigate, the Primordial brand on Anthony's flank ignited — revealing the truth through magical memory.

At the imperial tribunal, Anthony was offered three choices: restoration to human form, financial compensation, or "purpose." He chose purpose — accepting a Primordial Pact shadow-collar that bound him as a free retainer and imperial witness. He now serves at Isalia's estate, his brand serving as a living lie-detector and his collar allowing him to testify under Dragon Law.

His father was arrested for embezzlement and falsifying imperial charters. Anthony hasn't visited him.
