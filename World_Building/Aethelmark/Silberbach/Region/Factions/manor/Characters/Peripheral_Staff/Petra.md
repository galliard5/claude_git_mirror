# Petra

Halfling. Scheduling assistant. Owns the manor's calendars, the appointment intake, and the day-to-day logistics of who is in the building when. Reports through Elias_Varn (administrative oversight) with peripheral coordination through Lyra (operational shifts) and Kira (messenger handoffs).

---

## Appearance

Three feet two inches, the smaller side of average for a halfling woman. Mid-thirties. Dark brown hair coiled at the back of her head with two functional pins; not fashionable, just out of the way. Sharp-featured face, alert grey eyes, the focused stillness of someone who tracks several things at once and is doing it right now.

Wears practical office clothing — a dark grey overdress, white linen beneath, a leather belt with a small slate hanging from it for quick notes. Soft shoes that don't make noise on the office floors. A pair of reading lenses on a chain around her neck that she puts on for the schedule book and takes off for everything else.

Her hands are always doing something — turning a page, marking a slate, sorting a stack. When her hands are still, she is paying full attention to whoever has interrupted her, and she will get back to the task as soon as they stop.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Surgical efficiency.**
Petra runs the calendars the way a quartermaster runs supply lists. Every appointment has a category, a duration, a stakeholder set, and a contingency. She knows who needs preparation time, who runs late, who can absorb a late shift, and who absolutely cannot. The schedule on the board is the public version. The schedule in her head is more accurate.

**Flaw: Treats interruptions as opposition.**
Petra protects the calendar against drift, which is good, and also against legitimate adjustments she didn't see coming, which is sometimes a problem. New information that arrives mid-day reaches her and is processed correctly; new information that arrives via someone she hasn't trained yet sometimes gets bounced back as "go through proper channels" when it didn't need to. Senior staff have learned to handle her on this — Lyra rephrases, Elias goes around, Isalia just signs the override and walks past.

---

## Background

Born into a halfling administrative family in the city — her mother was a clerk for the merchants' tax office; her father kept the books for a guildhouse. Petra apprenticed in scheduling and records at sixteen, worked for a noble house in the city for eight years, and came to the manor four years ago when she got tired of metropolitan politics and wanted somewhere with clearer hierarchies.

Hired by Lyra. Tested by Elias_Varn during a six-month probation that he described to her at the end as *unnecessary, but I needed to be sure.* She has held the schedule role since.

She lives in the staff wing, has a small private office adjacent to the main reception area, and keeps a personal cat — *Ledger* — who has the run of her office and is sometimes found asleep on the schedule book. Petra does not move Ledger off the book. She works around him.

---

## The Work

Owns these calendars in priority order:

1. **Isalia's appointments** — consultations, client meetings, staff conferrals. Highest sensitivity. Coordinates with Lyra for any conflicts.
2. **Senior staff calendars** — Korr's medical schedule, Alric's lab availability, Elowen's enchanting work blocks, Vogt's security rotations. Pulls from each weekly and integrates.
3. **Intake calendar** — new client consultations, returning client bookings, transformation procedure scheduling (with Korr/Alric), reversion appointments, infirmary visits. Reception (Liora and Jaskin) routes new requests to her; she fits them in.
4. **Visitor logistics** — noble visits, merchant deliveries, official inspections. Coordinates carriage arrival, room preparation, and senior staff awareness.
5. **The Garden Wing schedule** — companion bookings. She keeps a separate book for these, in her own hand, with vetting flags from Liora and Vix's overrides clearly marked.
6. **The Surrogates' rotation** — coordinates with Lyra and the kennel staff on which surrogate is available for which litter, when the surrogate group rotates feed schedules, etc.

She does not own the kennels' breeding schedule (Sable does), the supply ordering schedule (Mira does), or the messenger routes (Kira does), but she coordinates with all three.

## Voice

Crisp Silberbach-city halfling accent, slightly faster than human speech. Says exactly what she means. Doesn't editorialise. Does not soften.

**Default Conversational Move: Reframe to actionable.**
Petra translates whatever is being said into a calendar question. *You want to see Isalia* becomes *which day, what duration, which urgency.* If the asker can't answer those questions, she helps them work them out. Then she books it.

**Quirks:**
- Taps a fingernail on the slate-edge when calculating — once per option she's considering
- Says *next Tuesday* with absolute precision; says *eventually* almost never
- Asks visitors their name twice when she's writing it — once to hear it, once to spell it
- Ledger the cat naps in her office; she pets him absently while reviewing the day's calendar

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(At her desk, lenses on, schedule book open, slate in left hand, pen in right.)*
*(Doesn't look up.)*
"Tuesday morning is the Brewers, Wednesday morning Kinsky is due, Wednesday afternoon you'd be sharing Korr with the runt's check. Thursday morning is open. Thursday morning works."
*(Now looks up.)*
"Thursday morning at nine. Confirmed?"

## Relationships

**[[Elias_Varn|Elias]]** — Direct supervisor. Mutual professional respect. They confer briefly each morning on the day's adjustments. He trusts her judgment on calendar conflicts and signs off her overrides without re-asking.

**[[Lyra_Vex|Lyra]]** — Cross-coordination. Lyra owns staffing; Petra owns timing. They meet weekly. Friendly enough, neither is performative.

**[[Kira_Lann|Kira]]** — Information handoff. Kira's couriers carry confirmations back to clients after Petra books them. They've worked out a clean handoff system that doesn't require either of them to interrupt each other.

**[[Liora]]** — Reception channel. Liora flags incoming requests; Petra processes. Their working trust is high; Petra has overridden Liora's reads only once in two years and apologised when she turned out to be wrong.

**[[Jaskin]]** — Night handoff. Jaskin handles after-hours requests; Petra picks them up the next morning. Jaskin's notes are spare and precise, which Petra appreciates.

**[[Vix]]** — Garden Wing schedule. Vix has override authority on companion bookings; Petra books them, Vix can refuse them, and Petra adjusts. The relationship works because both of them understand exactly what each is owed.

**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Owner. Isalia signs Petra's overrides without re-asking. Petra has never had her judgment questioned by Isalia in two years. The trust is mutual and built on demonstrated reliability.

## Independent Goal

Run the schedule cleanly. The reward is its own.

---

## Combat Disposition

**Bravery:** Average. Petra is not a fighter. She is also not someone who panics. In a crisis she would go straight to the schedule book and the calendar — *who is in the building, who needs to be moved* — and would be useful in exactly that frame.
**Loyalty:** To Elias. To Isalia. To the calendar.
**Breaking Point:** Someone deliberately deceiving her about a scheduling matter to harm the manor — sending false confirmations, manipulating arrival times, etc. Petra would notice quickly and would not forget.

---

## Daily Routine

- **Pre-dawn:** Up. Reads Jaskin's overnight notes. Works the day's calendar against any shifts.
- **Dawn:** At her desk. Lenses on. The day's schedule confirmed.
- **Mid-morning:** Conferral with Elias_Varn. Brief. Action items identified.
- **Late morning:** Walk-through of the day's high-priority appointments. Speaks briefly to each senior staff member with anything on their calendar.
- **Midday:** Mess hall. Eats quickly. Returns to the desk.
- **Afternoon:** Live schedule management. Most reschedules and intake processing happens here.
- **Late afternoon:** Tomorrow's schedule confirmed. Slate cleared. Notes for Kira's couriers.
- **Evening:** Closes the desk. Final notes for Jaskin's handoff.
- **Night:** Her quarters. Reads administrative histories — the histories of well-run households. It is not glamorous but it is hers.
