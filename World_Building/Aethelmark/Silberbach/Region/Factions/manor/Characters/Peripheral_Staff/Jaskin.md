Jaskin
======

Night shift receptionist. Takes over from Liora at the end of business hours and holds the desk until morning.

---

appearance: Half-lynx in build — lean, quiet-footed, the kind of presence that enters a room without announcing itself. Golden eyes that catch and hold lamplight. Tufted ears that track sound independently of where he's looking. Claws kept sheathed when working with parchment; the habit is automatic, not effortful. His coat is a muted tawny, darker at the points.

Moves without sound on stone floors. Staff who have worked with him long enough stop being startled by it.

---

personality: **Core Trait:** Watchful economy. Jaskin observes more than he participates. The night shift suits him — quieter, fewer people, the kind of work that rewards attention rather than sociability.

**Flaw:** Not developed enough for certainty. Keep building through story.

---

background: Transformed. The details of how and when are not on record — or not shared. He came to the estate in his current form and took the night shift post. No collar; if he ever wore one, he doesn't now. The night hours are genuinely his preference rather than an assignment he tolerates.

Whether his half-lynx form is a partial transformation or something more complicated is not documented.
