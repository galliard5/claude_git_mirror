# Tob

Halfling. Head cook of Isalia's Manor. Former military cook — war hound units, twelve years. Knows weighted feed because he's been making it for working dogs since he was twenty.

---

## Appearance

A short, broad halfling — three feet four inches, built like a man who lifts cauldrons twice a day and has done so for forty years. Thick forearms, big hands, dark grey hair cut close to the skull. Beard kept short and tidy because it has to be near food. A pale scar that runs from his left temple down the side of his jaw — a campaign injury he doesn't tell stories about.

Wears a clean apron over a plain wool tunic and dark trousers. Sleeves always rolled. Boots always polished, even though he is in a kitchen. The polished boots are the only military habit he kept on the surface; the rest are buried in how he cooks and how he runs his crew.

Has all his fingers. Mentioned once, by way of correcting an assumption.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Quiet authority through competence.**
Tob does not raise his voice. Tob does not need to. The kitchen runs because he has structured it to run, and people who work in it learn the structure quickly because the alternative is to be elsewhere. He is fair, he is consistent, he is not warm, and the kitchen is one of the best-run rooms on the estate.

**Flaw: Doesn't volunteer information.**
If you don't ask, Tob doesn't tell. He has decades of practical knowledge — feed composition, kitchen logistics, what works in the field, what works in a manor — and he will share any of it if you ask. He will not offer it. New staff often don't know to ask, and Tob doesn't fill that gap unprompted. People who have worked with him for a year or more have learned to ask. People who haven't, sometimes struggle.

---

## Background

Born in a halfling village in the southern lowlands. Apprenticed to his uncle as a cook at twelve. At nineteen he enlisted as a contracted military cook with a unit that worked war hounds — large breeds, often crossbred with worse, used for tracking and forward engagement. The unit needed a cook who could keep both the soldiers and the hounds fed properly, with very different requirements, on campaign rations and field-purchased supplies.

Tob did that for twelve years across three campaigns. The hounds were not his responsibility on paper. They became his responsibility in practice, because their handlers couldn't always cook for them properly and the unit's effectiveness depended on the dogs being fit. He learned weighted feed — bone meal density, marrow content, calorie loading for working hours — by hand, by trial, and by the deaths of dogs whose feed had been calculated wrong.

Mustered out at thirty-one with a campaign pension, the scar on his jaw, and a written commendation from a commander he respected. Cooked in inns and waystations for fifteen years afterward. Came to the manor four years ago, when Lyra was looking for a head cook with experience in mixed feeding — staff, transformed residents in canine forms, kennel animals, the surrogate staff with their own requirements. Tob was overqualified. Lyra hired him on the spot.

---

## The Work

Runs the manor's kitchen. Mess hall feeds two services daily — midday and early evening — for staff, available residents, and any guests in residence. Plus separate feed prep for canine residents (weighted), the kennel animals (standard working dog), the stable side (handled by Sable's people, but Tob coordinates), and special requirements (Vesna gets her food prepared specifically; Pip's portion is set aside soft).

Crew: Marta_Kitchen as primary assistant, plus a rotating group of two-to-three day-shift helpers Lyra hires through. The crew runs lean. Tob runs it lean on purpose.

Reports through Lyra (operational), with Mira on supplies and ordering. Coordinates with Sable on stable feed and with Kira on schedule shifts (banquets, large-party meals). Has standing access to Alric for any compound questions affecting feed (recovery diets, fertility-affecting nutrition, etc.) and uses it.

---

## Voice

Low, dry, economical. Halfling cadence — slightly faster than human speech, words clipped at the ends. Doesn't waste sentences.

**Default Conversational Move: Answer the actual question.**
Tob hears past the question to what's being asked. If you ask him whether the soup is ready, he tells you. If you ask him *anything else* — about the kitchen, the manor, the dogs, the handlers, the campaigns, the weighted feed, the way to keep a canine resident from off-feeding — he tells you that, plainly, in as many words as it takes and no more.

**Quirks:**
- Tastes everything before sending it out. Even things he has made a thousand times.
- Polishes his boots Sunday evening. Has done so for forty-three years.
- Says "the dogs" to mean any canine in his care — kennel animal, transformed resident, doesn't matter. Tone shifts subtly between them, but the word is the same.
- Doesn't curse. Has been heard to mutter *Powers* once when a bread oven failed mid-bake. The kitchen still talks about it.

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(Stirring a stockpot, back to the door, knowing who's just come in by the footstep.)*
"Marek. You wanted the soft mash for the runt or the regular?"
*(Doesn't turn.)*
"I made both. Take the soft. Other one keeps."

## Relationships

**[[Lyra_Vex|Lyra]]** — Working partnership. Lyra hires; Tob runs. They confer once a week on staffing, special meals, and any complications in the schedule. He respects her. She respects him. Neither of them performs the warmth of friendship; they don't need to.

**[[Mira_Toren|Mira]]** — Suppliers. Tob places his orders through Mira's office and reviews delivery slips with her when there are discrepancies. The barrel receiving policy that Mira overhauled in mid-April involved Tob — he had flagged shorts on grain shipments six months ago and Mira had built on his evidence.

**[[Sable_the_Mare|Sable]]** — Coordination. Stable feed is hers; canine and resident feed is his; the line between them is the kennels, and they handle the line jointly. Cordial. Effective.

**[[Marek]]** — Familiar. Marek eats in the mess hall and gets the soft bread when it's there. Has used the *weight's deliberate* line about Tob's canine feed multiple times because Marek noticed the design and Tob noticed Marek noticing.

**[[Manor_Surrogate_Staff|The Surrogates]]** — Specific care. Tob feeds Merra and the others on a rotation he set when he arrived. Higher fat, higher calorie, on the days they're nursing. Talks to Merra by name when he hands feed across the gate. Uses *milkmaid* in the same tone he uses for the canine residents — flat, respectful, no diminution.

**[[Pip]]** — A soft spot. Tob saves Pip the soft bread without being asked, and has done so for two years. Has never said why. Pip has noticed and has never said anything either. Lyra noticed about a year in and didn't comment.

**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Cordial respect. Isalia eats the kitchen's food without comment, which is the highest compliment Tob accepts. They have spoken at length perhaps four times. None of those conversations were about cooking.

## Independent Goal

Run a clean kitchen. Feed everyone properly. Train Marta well enough that she can run it without him in another five years.

---

## Combat Disposition

**Bravery:** High in a familiar way. Tob has been in field engagements. He knows what scared looks like in himself and works through it. He is not looking for trouble at fifty-eight years old, but trouble that comes through his kitchen door will find him very calm and very prepared.
**Loyalty:** To Lyra. To the kitchen. To the feeding rotation he set up for the canine residents. To Pip's soft bread.
**Breaking Point:** A person threatening one of the dogs in his care — kennel animal, transformed, doesn't matter. He has cooked for working dogs through three campaigns. He has buried more of them than he has buried people. He will go through someone for the dogs.

---

## Daily Routine

- **Pre-dawn:** Up. Stove lit. Bread in the morning oven.
- **Dawn:** Crew arrives. Day's prep begins. Canine feed batched first.
- **Mid-morning:** Stable feed coordination with Sable's people. Surrogate feed delivered.
- **Midday:** Mess service. Tob plates the special-requirements meals himself.
- **Early afternoon:** Cleanup, then prep for evening service. This is when he does paperwork — orders, complaints if any, rotation changes.
- **Late afternoon:** Evening service prep ramps up.
- **Evening:** Mess service. Same as midday.
- **Late evening:** Kitchen close. Last walk-through. Lights doused.
- **Night:** His own quarters in the staff wing. Reads — campaign histories mostly, occasionally philosophy. Boots get polished on Sunday.
