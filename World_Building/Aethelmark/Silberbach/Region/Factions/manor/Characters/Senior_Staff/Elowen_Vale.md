Elowen Vale
===========

Lead Enchanter of Isalia's Manor. Designs and maintains enchanted devices including speech collars and magical equipment.

appearance: An elegant elf with silver hair worn long, her fingers long and calloused from years of rune-carving. Her eyes are a pale grey, almost colorless in certain light. She dresses in simple but exquisitely tailored robes, preferring deep blues and greens. Always wears a thin silver bracelet etched with protective runes — her own work.

personality: **Core Trait: Meticulous craftsmanship.**
Elowen believes magic should be precise, controlled, and ethically applied.

**Flaw: Guarded bitterness.**
After exile from her former elven enclave, Elowen is slow to trust new people and assumes criticism is coming. She can be defensive and sharp with those who don't appreciate the complexity of her work.

background: Born into an ancient elven enclave known for its enchanters, Elowen was a prodigy — and a problem. She asked questions the elders found uncomfortable: Why do we not share our knowledge? Why do we hide from the younger races? Why do we treat transformation magic as taboo?

Her final transgression was crafting a speech-collar for a transformed human who had come to the enclave seeking help. The elders were horrified that she would "debase their craft" for "a human in animal form." She left the same night, taking only her tools and her notes.

Isalia found her in a human city, barely scraping by selling minor enchantments to ungrateful merchants. The kobold offered her a workshop, materials, and something more valuable: respect. Elowen has been at the manor for twenty years. She still writes to her sister in the enclave, but has never received a reply.
