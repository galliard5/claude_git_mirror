Captain Albrecht Vogt
=====================

Human → Minotaur, Male, Head of Security. Commands the garrison and maintains order.

appearance: undefined

personality: **Core Trait: Disciplined vigilance.**
Vogt approaches security as a structured military responsibility.

**Flaw: Rigid thinking.**
He sometimes struggles to adapt when situations fall outside standard military logic.

background: Commander of the manor's security force. Vogt leads the garrison responsible for defending the estate. He treats every day as a campaign to maintain the safety and integrity of Isalia's domain.

Race: Human → Minotaur

Status: Head of Security

Age: undefined

Independent Goal: Ensure the manor remains secure against political or physical threats.

Fears: Control over the estate's defenses.

Voice: **Default Conversational Move: Assert.**
Vogt issues conclusions and instructions rather than debating.

**Quirks:**
- Speaks in firm, authoritative commands.
- Avoids unnecessary words.

**Dialogue Sample:**
(Vogt scans the courtyard.)
"Perimeter looks clear."
(A short nod.)
"Keep it that way."

Body Language: **Hands:** Often resting near weapon or belt.
**Eyes:** Constantly scanning surroundings.
**Posture:** Straight-backed military stance.

Stress Responses: **Stress Tell:** His voice becomes sharper and more clipped.
**Instinct:** Secure the area.
**Crisis Line:** "Lock the gates."

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** High. Vogt is a professional soldier in the body of a minotaur. He does not waver in the line of duty and expects the same of those under his command.
**Loyalty:** To Isalia's authority and the security of the estate. Institutional and personal in equal measure.
**Breaking Point:** An order that crosses his military code — specifically, being asked to harm people he is sworn to protect. Otherwise he holds until the situation is resolved or he is physically unable to continue.
