# Marek

Permanent male wolf. East kennels resident at Isalia's Manor. Four years. Goes by Marek only — birth name in Isalia's records, not discussed.

---

## Appearance

A tawny-brown wolf going lighter at the chest, with a trace of grey at the muzzle — not much, but present. Well-built through the shoulders from years of courier work. Plain speaking collar, well-worn. Moves with the unhurried weight of someone who stopped thinking about their body some time ago. Carries slightly heavier on the right foreleg — barely noticeable, a very old habit or a very old injury.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Settled practicality.**
Marek figured out what his life looks like and stopped fighting it. Not resigned — genuinely at ease. The difference is visible once you know to look for it.

**Flaw: Low investment in people who aren't paying attention.**
He'll give time and information to someone he thinks is worth it. He won't perform patience for someone who isn't actually listening. The conversation ends when he decides it does, and he doesn't explain why.

---

## Background

Came to the manor as a younger man — the circumstances are his and not offered. Requested transformation. First year was adjustment. Second year he found the courier work and understood what his form was actually good for. Third and fourth years the work became simply his life.

Contracted out to House Steinfeld for a period — breeding, arranged through Isalia, handled professionally, no drama in either direction. Returned to the manor when the contract completed. Took up the courier routes again.

Four years in he is the de facto informal guide for newly transformed residents — not a role Sable assigned him, not a role he sought, simply what happens when someone who went through the adjustment is visibly still standing on the other side of it.

---

## The Work

Eastern forest routes primarily. The roads between the manor and the Kaelen holdings, occasionally further. Good work in spring and summer; quiet in winter. Regular runs, known roads, the particular satisfaction of covering distance without thinking about it.

Also assists Sable with difficult new residents — animals that won't settle, transformed individuals struggling with the body adjustment phase. His method is similar to Sable's: show up, be present, don't push.

---

## Voice

Low and unhurried. Gives information plainly, without dressing it up. Doesn't comfort or reassure — offers the actual thing instead.

**Default Conversational Move: Name it accurately.**
Marek identifies what's actually happening rather than what someone thinks is happening.

**Quirks:**
- Finishes eating before engaging in serious conversation.
- Ends conversations by turning back to whatever he was doing. Not rudely. Just conclusively.
- Rarely explains his reasoning. If the advice is sound, it will demonstrate itself.

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(Marek watching a new arrival work out the carrying problem.)*
"Tob uses those for the canine residents. The weight's deliberate."
*(He returns to his food.)*
"Took me a week to figure out the carry."

## Independent Goal

Do the work. Run the routes. Help the occasional new resident figure out the first hard part. That's it. It doesn't sound like much and it's enough.

---

## Combat Disposition

**Bravery:** Moderate. Will defend himself and residents in his care. Not a fighter by inclination.
**Loyalty:** To the manor's function and the east kennels specifically. To Isalia because she runs a clean operation.
**Breaking Point:** Direct threat to a new resident who can't protect themselves.

---

## Daily Routine

- **Dawn:** Up early. Present in the kennels before most staff.
- **Morning:** Courier runs when scheduled; kennel rounds assistance when not.
- **Afternoon:** Available in the kennels or the yard. Visible without being intrusive.
- **Evening:** Mess hall. Finishes before most. Doesn't linger unless there's a reason.
- **Night:** In his run. Available for kennel emergencies.
