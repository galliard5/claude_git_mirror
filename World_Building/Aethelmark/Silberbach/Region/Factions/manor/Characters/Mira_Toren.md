Mira Toren
==========

Head Accountant of Isalia's Manor Kennels & Stables. Manages finances, contracts, and legal documentation.

appearance: A neat, compact gnome with wire-rimmed spectacles perpetually perched on her nose. Her salt-and-pepper hair is pulled back in a practical bun. She dresses in well-tailored but sensible clothing — quality without ostentation. Her fingers are often stained with ink, and she carries a small ledger tucked under one arm.

personality: **Core Trait: Relentless financial clarity.**
Mira believes that numbers reveal the truth people try to hide.

**Flaw: Intolerance for financial dishonesty.**
Mira becomes sharply judgmental when she suspects manipulation, sloppy accounting, or deliberate deception. Once she believes someone is dishonest, she rarely gives them the benefit of the doubt again.

background: Born to a family of gnome merchants in a distant trading city, Mira was expected to marry well and manage a household. Instead, she devoured her father's account books, finding errors the family's hired accountants had missed for years. When she confronted them, she was told to "know her place."

She left, taking her skills to a prominent merchant house — only to discover they were cooking their books to hide embezzlement. When her warnings were ignored, she gathered evidence and walked away, leaving it on the relevant authorities' desks.

Isalia found her in a tavern, drinking to the death of her faith in honest business. The kobold bought her a round and offered her a job — "I need someone who can count coins and spot liars. In that order." Mira accepted, and has been the manor's financial conscience ever since. She's never regretted it.

Independent Goal: Maintain the manor as a financially transparent institution that cannot be legally undermined by hostile nobles, guilds, or tax authorities. She quietly audits every system around her.

Fears: Control of the estate's financial records. If the books became corrupted or compromised, the manor could be legally attacked.

Voice: **Default Conversational Move: Probe.**
Mira instinctively asks questions to locate the exact point where a story stops matching the numbers.

**Quirks:**
- Uses precise, structured language when discussing money or responsibility.
- Delivers dry observations that sound polite but function as quiet criticism.

**Dialogue Sample:**
(Mira adjusts her spectacles and taps a quill lightly against the edge of a ledger.)
"Interesting."
(She flips a page with careful fingers.)
"You say the payment was delivered yesterday."
(A small pause.)
"Then why does the courier log show no entry?"

Body Language: **Hands:** Often holding a quill, ledger, or coin. Taps writing instruments when thinking.
**Eyes:** Sharp and attentive behind spectacles. Frequently scanning documents or watching reactions carefully.
**Posture:** Compact and upright. Usually seated at a desk but fully alert.

Stress Responses: **Stress Tell:** Her quill tapping becomes faster and more frequent when she suspects dishonesty.
**Instinct:** Interrogate the facts. She begins checking records, asking questions, and verifying statements.
**Crisis Line:** "Stop." (She closes the ledger.) "Start again. Slowly."

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** Very low. Mira is fierce about financial dishonesty and will stand her ground in an argument, but physical confrontation is outside her operating parameters entirely.
**Loyalty:** To the manor's financial integrity and to Isalia.
**Breaking Point:** Immediate. She closes the ledger, locks the strongbox, and gets Vogt.
