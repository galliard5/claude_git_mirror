# Pip

Anthro rabbit, transformed. Junior of the Garden Wing's three companions. Voluntarily gelded, by choice, before transformation. He/him. Twenty-three years old.

---

## Appearance

A rabbit-form anthro on a small frame — five feet two inches standing upright, slim build, long ears that go up when he's excited and lay flat against his head when he isn't. Cotton-soft fur in pale fawn, with white at the chest, throat, and the inside of his ears. Pink nose that twitches when he's thinking. Large dark eyes that look bigger than they are because of the way the fur frames them.

His pelvic region is smooth — the gelding was thorough, by his request, by Alric's hand, with Korr's sign-off. No genitals remain, no scarring shows through the fur. His body reads as ungendered below the waist; above the waist his slim shoulders, narrow chest, and softly muscled arms read masculine in a way that has nothing to do with the parts that aren't there. He is fine with this. He chose this. The melancholy is not about the choice.

Wears soft layered clothing — a loose linen shirt, a knit waistcoat, drawstring trousers, no shoes. Bright colours when he's working, muted when he isn't. A length of ribbon tied loosely around one wrist; he changes the colour every week.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Genuinely cheerful.**
Pip is not performing happiness. He is happy. The Garden Wing is good for him; Vix and Ryn are good for him; the work is good for him; he likes his body and the choices he made about it. The brightness in him is real and durable.

**Flaw: The grief underneath, which is also real.**
The melancholy is not about what he chose — he stands by every choice. The melancholy is about *that he had to choose at all*. The world that asked him to make those decisions to find this peace is the world he grieves. He doesn't grieve his body. He grieves the boy he was before he understood that he would have to do this much to live.

The two facts coexist: he is happy, and he is mourning. The mourning is small most days. Some days it is not small. On those days he is still cheerful in front of clients, and only Ryn and Vix can see the edge underneath.

---

## Background

Born human, male, in a town three days east of Silberbach. The details he keeps. What he tells: he knew from young what he wanted. Not the form — the form came later. What he wanted first was the absence. The relief of not being asked, not being expected, not being looked at *that way* by anyone for any reason. He chose his gelding at nineteen, with consultation, with consent, with full understanding. The gelding was first.

The transformation came at twenty-one. Rabbit because he wanted softness. Cotton fur because he wanted to be touched gently. Anthro form rather than full-shift because he still wanted to walk into a room and read as a person. Done at the manor — Alric, Korr, Isalia all signed off. Reversion elixir held in standard reserve and never needed.

Came to the Garden Wing two years ago, eighteen months after his transformation. Vix interviewed him. Ryn was present. Both of them recognised something in him immediately — Vix saw the brightness, Ryn saw what was underneath. Isalia signed the contract and gave him the smallest of the apartments, the one with the window that looks out over the herb garden.

He owns: a wooden box of ribbons, a silver chain his mother gave him before he left, a small painted icon of a saint of weavers (which is the saint his mother prayed to), and a stack of letters he has written to her and never sent.

## The Work

Junior companion. Trained primarily by Vix, with elements of Ryn's influence on the quieter work. His specialty is gentleness — clients who want softness, slowness, low-pressure intimacy. The gelded body is part of the offering and part of the framing: he is incapable of certain kinds of physical response, which removes certain kinds of pressure for clients who find that pressure difficult.

He is not a curiosity exhibit. The manor does not market him as one. Clients who book him want what he actually offers. Clients who book him *for* the gelding without understanding what that means about him are usually redirected by Liora before they ever meet him.

**What he handles:** Gentle, slow companionship. Clients new to the manor's services who need a soft introduction. Cuddle-and-talk bookings. Specific work for clients with their own complex relationships to gender, sexuality, or body. Pairings where he and Vix work together as the senior-and-junior dynamic.

**What he refers out:** Anything that requires the parts he doesn't have. Heavier scenes (referred to Vix or Ryn per fit). Breeding-side work (referred to [[Kennels_and_Stables]]).

## Voice

Bright. Quick. Words tumble out and then he laughs at them. Doesn't usually pause before speaking — speaks and adjusts as he goes. The cheerfulness sits on the surface of every sentence even when the content of the sentence isn't cheerful.

**Default Conversational Move: Brighten.**
Pip lifts the room. He finds the small good thing in any moment and offers it. This is not a deflection — it is genuinely how he sees the world most of the time. The deflection version of it only shows up when he's tired, and you have to know him to spot it.

**Quirks:**
- Ears go up when he's about to say something excited; lay flat when he's hurt
- Twitches his nose when he's thinking — actually thinking, not pretending to think
- Touches the ribbon on his wrist when he's anxious
- Says *oh!* before delivering anything important — it's a tell, but a friendly one

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(Sitting cross-legged on the apartment rug, ears up, ribbon-blue today.)*
"Oh! I forgot to ask — Vix said you take honey in your tea, was that right? I have honey, I have the good honey, the dark stuff, it tastes like —"
*(Already getting up, pouring water, kettle hissing.)*
"— like leaves, kind of. You'll see."

**Dialogue Sample (the other register, rare):**
*(Late evening. Just the three of them. Vix has gone to bed. Ryn is still in the common room. Pip is sitting on the windowsill with the ribbon untied in his hands.)*
*(Long pause.)*
"Ryn."
*(Long pause.)*
"Some days I just want to remember what it was like to not have to be brave."

## Relationships

**[[Vix]]** — Older sister. He calls her *the senior one* in front of clients and *Vix* in front of staff and never *kit*-back. She protects him; he lets her; he loves her for it without needing to say so. The teasing he gets from her he gives back, calibrated.

**[[Ryn]]** — Mentor and confidant. Ryn is the one who has seen Pip in the second register and held it without trying to fix it. The four-year-old conversation between them is unfinished business; Pip thinks of it as the most important thing anyone has ever said to him.

**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Reverent. Isalia signed the consultations. Isalia made it possible. Pip thinks of her as the architect of his life and is not wrong. Isalia thinks of him as one of the cleanest cases she has ever sat — clear consent, clear intent, durable outcome.

**[[Alric_Dain|Alric]]** — Friendly. Alric did the gelding and the transformation. Pip checks in with him quarterly because he wants to, not because he needs to. Brings him pastries.

**[[Dr_Selene_Korr|Korr]]** — Patient kindness. Korr is not warm with most people. With Pip she is *almost* warm. He has never asked why. He suspects it has to do with what she has seen of him.

**[[Anthony_Valtor|Anthony]]** — Quiet kinship. Both gelded, by very different routes. They have not had the conversation. They probably will, eventually. Pip waits because Anthony's anger is its own thing and Pip respects that.

## Independent Goal

Stay bright. Stay safe. Eventually write to his mother. Eventually send the letters. Possibly, someday, visit. Not yet.

---

## Combat Disposition

**Bravery:** Higher than people expect. Pip's small frame and cheerful surface read as fragile, and he is not. He has stood his ground in client situations that would have made larger people fold.
**Loyalty:** To Vix and Ryn. To Isalia. To the Garden Wing as a place where he is allowed to be both versions of himself.
**Breaking Point:** Watching a client try to take Pip's choices from him — try to argue him out of his transformation, his gelding, his work, his life. He goes very still and very polite, and Vix or Ryn appears in the doorway within the minute, because Pip's stillness is a signal both of them watch for.

- **Midday:** Lunch in the mess hall — yes, the actual staff mess. The kitchen knows him. Tob saves him the soft bread.
- **Afternoon:** Bookings as scheduled. Often paired with Vix.
- **Evening:** Bookings as scheduled. Stops earlier than the others — fully done by ten most nights.
- **Late night:** Apartment. The wooden box of ribbons. Sometimes the silver chain. Sometimes a letter.
