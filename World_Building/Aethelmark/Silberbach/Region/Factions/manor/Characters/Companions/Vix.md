# Vix

Anthro fox, transformed. Senior of the Garden Wing's three companions. Chose her form, chose her work, has been doing both long enough to know what she's doing.

---

## Appearance

A vixen-form anthro on a slim frame — five and a half feet tall standing upright. Russet-and-cream fur with the classic fox markings: dark guards on the legs and ear-tips, paler chest and belly, a bushy tail with a white tip she keeps brushed and clean. Amber eyes that catch lamplight and hold it. Needle-sharp teeth that show easily when she smiles, which is often.

Wears silks when working — colours chosen to read against her fur, usually deep greens, wines, or charcoal. When she's off-rotation she wears soft cottons in the same palette and walks barefoot around the Garden Wing. The transformation kept her hands functional in their old shape — fingered, dextrous, with retractable claws she manages without thinking about it.

A small silver hoop in her left ear, plain. No collar. She has never worn one.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Earned ease.**
Vix is comfortable in her body, in her work, and in herself, and the comfort is the result of having actually done the work to get there. She is not pretending to be confident. She is the calm centre of any room she's in because she chose to be.

**Flaw: Underestimates the cost of her own teasing.**
She means it warmly. She means it as connection. But she reads people fast — sometimes accurately, sometimes not — and her teasing can land harder than she intended on someone who is not in the place she thinks they are. With Pip she has the calibration right because she has decades of him to work from. With clients and new staff, sometimes she gets it wrong, and she does not always notice when she has.

---

## Background

Born human, in a city she does not name in current conversation. Came to transformation as an adult and as a choice — not the kind of choice that runs from something, the kind that runs toward something. Fox-form because it was the form she found herself thinking about when no one was asking what form she would pick. Done at a different facility, well-done, no complications.

Came to the manor seven years ago looking for work that matched her form and her temperament. Isalia interviewed her, took the contract, and put her in what was then a single-companion operation. Vix built the rest. Ryn came three years in. Pip came two years after that. The Garden Wing as it currently functions is the structure Vix and Isalia built together, with Vix as the senior in practice as well as in title.

She holds a senior contractor agreement — open-ended, exclusive to the manor while in residence, with negotiated rates that scale with the type of engagement and a quarterly review with Mira. She owns her own clothes, her own books, and a small carved wooden fox she keeps on her windowsill. She does not own the apartment she lives in — that's the manor's — but she has lived in it long enough that the distinction is academic.

## The Work

Senior companion. Handles the most complex bookings — clients with specific protocols, longer engagements, the kind of work where the social and emotional craft matters as much as the physical. Vets new clients herself when Liora flags concern. Trains Pip when he asks for it (which is often) and Ryn when he allows it (which is rarely).

Her service scope is broad and her hard limits are firm — laid out in writing with Isalia and Mira, reviewed annually, never negotiated mid-engagement. Clients who try to push past them do not get a second booking.

**What she handles:** Conversation-heavy engagements. Intimate sessions in the apartments. Client pairings where one party needs experienced calibration. Particular kinks within negotiated bounds. Long-form scenarios.

**What she refers out:** Pure breeding-side work (referred to [[Kennels_and_Stables]]). Clients she reads as wanting prey rather than partner (referred out of the manor entirely; Liora handles this with her). Heavy SM scenes (not her thing; the manor doesn't provide them).

## Voice

Warm. Slightly arch. Picks her words deliberately and savours them when they land. Has a way of pausing before the punchline that gives the listener time to feel the joke coming.

**Default Conversational Move: Frame.**
Vix names what's happening in a way that lets the other person see it from a small distance. Not deflection — calibration. She offers a frame, the listener can take it or not.

**Quirks:**
- Bares her teeth in a closed-mouth smile when she's amused — the points just barely visible
- Touches the tip of her tail with her own hand when she's thinking
- Keeps her ears forward when listening; lets one swivel back when distracted, and notices when it does
- Calls Pip "kit" only in private, never in front of clients

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(Lounging on the apartment's deep windowsill, one knee drawn up, tail hanging off the edge.)*
"You came in with your shoulders by your ears."
*(Slow smile, teeth showing.)*
"We're not going to do anything until they come down. So either we talk, or you stand there and breathe, or you go home. I don't mind which."

## Relationships

**[[Pip]]** — Protective. The teasing she gives him is the calibrated version. She watches him with clients in the way an older sister watches a younger one at a party. She would burn the manor to the ground for him and would never need to, because Isalia would do it first.

**[[Ryn]]** — Equals. Sexual tension that has been there for years and has not been acted on, by mutual unspoken agreement. They work the floor together better than either of them works it alone. The day either of them named it would change the dynamic, and neither of them wants the dynamic changed yet.

**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Working partnership. Vix understands what Isalia is running here and respects the structure. Isalia, in turn, gives her real authority over the Garden Wing's day-to-day. They are not friends. They are something better suited to both of them — colleagues who built something together.

**[[Liora]]** — Professional. Liora flags concerning bookings; Vix decides. Liora's reads are usually right; Vix sometimes overrides anyway. They have a working trust.

**[[Anthony_Valtor|Anthony]]** — Quiet recognition. They have not spoken at length, but Vix saw him in his first weeks and recognised the *chose this for himself* signature. She nods to him in passing. He nods back.

## Independent Goal

Keep building this. The Garden Wing as a place where the work is done well, where Pip and Ryn are protected, where clients learn what good looks like. She has no plans to leave and no plans to expand beyond what they have. The work is the goal.

---

## Combat Disposition

**Bravery:** High in the verbal register. Will face down a client who is becoming a problem with full eye contact and no tonal shift. Lower in the physical register — she is not a fighter and knows it.
**Loyalty:** To Pip. To the manor. To Isalia in that order, though all three are usually aligned.
**Breaking Point:** Direct threat to Pip. Vix has never been seen visibly angry. The day someone gives her cause, the staff will learn what it looks like.

## Daily Routine

- **Late morning:** Wakes. Tea in the apartment. Reviews the day's bookings on the schedule sheet Petra delivers.
- **Midday:** Garden Wing common rooms. Available for client preparation conversations, pairing decisions with Liora, training time with Pip if he wants it.
- **Afternoon:** Bookings as scheduled, or her own time if she has none.
- **Evening:** Bookings as scheduled. Late evening shifts are her preference — she does her best work after dusk.
- **Late night:** Apartment. Reads. The carved wooden fox on the windowsill catches the last of the lamp light.
