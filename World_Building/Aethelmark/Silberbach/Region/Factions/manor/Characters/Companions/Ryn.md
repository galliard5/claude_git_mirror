# Ryn

Anthro stag, partial transformation. Companion at the Garden Wing. He/him. Masculine identity retained alongside female biology — a deliberate outcome, chosen at consultation, not a complication of an incomplete process.

---

## Appearance

Six feet two inches standing — six feet eight to the antler tips, which are present, modest, and sit clean against his skull like part of his bone structure rather than an addition. Muscular through the chest and shoulders, narrower at the hips, the build of a working stag rather than a show one. Coat: warm chestnut on the body, paler cream on the chest and inner thighs, dark socks on the lower legs. White flash on the muzzle.

The masculine read is immediate and stable. Voice is low. Stance is square. The shape of him in a doorway is the shape of him in a doorway. The biology underneath the transformation is female by his choice — he wanted both in his body, and he asked for both, and he received both.

Velvety muzzle, kept clean. Chamomile-scented breath — the result of a tea he drinks twice a day, not a transformation effect. Hands kept ungloved; partial transformation kept full digit function. Wears loose linen shirts that drape over his frame, dark trousers, no shoes inside the Wing.

---

## Personality

**Core Trait: Settled authority without weight.**
Ryn occupies space the way furniture occupies space — naturally, without competing for it. People look to him in a room and don't realise they have. He gives quiet direction and the direction lands. He has not raised his voice in years, possibly ever.

**Flaw: Doesn't claim what he wants until someone else names it for him.**
He knows what he wants. He will not articulate it first. Vix has waited for him to speak about the thing between them for years; he has waited for her in turn; both of them are waiting for the other, and neither will move first. The pattern is general — Ryn lets others reach for him and meets them when they do, but rarely opens his hand first.

---

## Background

Born human, male. Came to transformation in his late twenties with a clear request: he wanted to be a stag, and he wanted to retain his masculine identity, and he wanted female biology underneath it. The combination is rare enough that Alric did the workup himself; Korr signed off; Isalia sat the consultation; the procedure was done at the manor, with consent reviewed at multiple stages.

The reason is his to share or not share. He does not hide it. He simply doesn't bring it up unprompted. What he tells those who ask: he wanted to be a body that could carry both — protective shape, fertile interior. The reason behind the reason is older.

He has been at the Garden Wing four years. Came to the manor through a referral from a client of Vix's who knew Ryn was looking for placement; Vix interviewed him; Isalia closed. Ryn signed an open-ended contractor agreement, similar in shape to Vix's but with different limits. Quarterly review with Mira. He owns three pairs of boots, two saddles for occasional rides, and a small collection of carved wooden game pieces that sit on a shelf in his apartment. He does not own the apartment.

---

## The Work

Mid-tier companion in the manor's framework — meaning the senior to Pip, the parallel to Vix in scope but with different specialisation. His specific draw is the partial transformation: clients who want the stag form and the masculine carriage *and* who want to engage with the female biology beneath it. The combination is unusual at the manor's price point and brings him a specific clientele.

His service scope is narrower than Vix's by his preference. He works fewer bookings, longer sessions, more selective intake. Liora flags candidates to him; he and Vix discuss; he decides.

**What he handles:** Long-form companionship. Specific paired work where the partial-transformation aesthetic is the point. Clients who want him as the masculine party in a scene where his biology is also accessed. Particular kink work within negotiated bounds — primarily around the partial-transformation theme and around being the stag *for* someone.

**What he refers out:** Quick-engagement bookings (referred to Vix or Pip per their preference). Clients who want him in a feminine register (he doesn't do that work; the body is his, the carriage is his, the request is wrong). Pure breeding work (referred to [[Kennels_and_Stables]]).

## Voice

Low and unhurried. Each sentence rests for a beat after it lands. Doesn't fill silence. When he talks, people listen, because the talking is rare enough to be worth attending to.

**Default Conversational Move: Hold.**
Ryn waits. The silence after he says something is part of what he said. People who try to talk over the silence are often answering a question he didn't ask yet.

**Quirks:**
- Takes a slow breath before answering anything more complicated than a simple yes
- Touches the base of his right antler when thinking, the way a human might touch their temple
- Sips chamomile tea throughout the day from a heavy ceramic mug; refills it himself
- When he laughs, it's a single low huff — startling because it's the only quick movement he makes

**Dialogue Sample:**
*(Sitting across from a nervous first-time client, mug in both hands, antler-shadow on the wall behind him.)*
*(Long beat. Sips tea.)*
"Tell me what you actually want."
*(Another beat.)*
"Not what you've decided you're allowed to want. What you want."

## Relationships

**[[Vix]]** — The thing that has not been named. Years of working alongside her with a tension that neither has acted on. He sees her clearly, she sees him clearly, and both of them are waiting for the other to say it first. Both of them know they are doing this. Neither will be the one to break it.

**[[Pip]]** — Mentorship. Ryn teaches Pip the things Pip is ready to learn, and recognises when he isn't ready, and waits. Calls him *kit* only when he needs grounding. Watches over him at parties. Has had the *cheerful surface, melancholy beneath* conversation with him, once, four years ago, and has not needed to repeat it.

**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Quiet respect. She understood the consultation request. She did not flinch. She walked it through the medical staff carefully and gave him the time and the information he needed. He is loyal to her because she earned it.

**[[Alric_Dain|Alric]]** — Professional gratitude. Alric did the alchemical workup. Ryn checks in with him annually because he wants to and because Alric appreciates the data.

**[[Dr_Selene_Korr|Korr]]** — Trust without warmth. Korr is not a warm woman, and Ryn does not need her to be. She does her job. He does his. They nod in passing.

## Independent Goal

Continue. The body is right. The work is right. The waiting on Vix is something he has learned to live inside — possibly indefinitely, possibly until something else changes. He is not in a hurry.

---

## Combat Disposition

**Bravery:** High and quiet. Will not back down. Will also rarely escalate. Most situations resolve before they get to violence because Ryn's presence makes escalation unappealing.
**Loyalty:** To Pip. To Vix. To the Garden Wing as a place. To Isalia for making it possible.
**Breaking Point:** Direct threat to Pip. Ryn would put an antler through someone's chest for Pip and would do it without changing his expression.

## Daily Routine

- **Early morning:** Up before most. Walk on the manor grounds — the lower pasture, sometimes as far as the river path. Comes back with the day's first chamomile brewing.
- **Mid-morning:** Available in the Garden Wing common room. Reading, tea, present without seeking attention.
- **Late morning:** Pip's training time when scheduled.
- **Afternoon:** Bookings as scheduled.
- **Evening:** Bookings as scheduled. Prefers a single evening engagement over multiple.
- **Late night:** Apartment. Game pieces on the shelf. Tea. Sleep.
