Goran
=====

Percheron stallion. Bachelor stalls. No collar, no papers worth trusting, no fixed role.

---

appearance: Massive. Percheron build — the kind of horse that makes other horses look like furniture. Dark coat, nearly black, that absorbs light rather than reflecting it. His silhouette at the pasture fence at night is simply absence in the shape of a horse.

A twisted scar runs along his flank — acquired before his transformation, its origin consistent with a blade or a very bad fall. The skin there is hairless and puckered, pulling slightly when he moves. He doesn't favour it.

His mane is tangled. Not neglected — he refuses grooming from most staff, and Sable chooses her battles with him carefully.

---

personality: **Core Trait:** Self-contained vigilance. Goran is always watching. He positioned himself at the perimeter the first week he arrived and has remained there — not because anyone asked him to, but because it suits him.

**Flaw:** Inaccessible. The mercenary who chose transformation for whatever reason he had is still in there, but the door isn't open to most. What would gain his trust is unknown because no one has yet managed it consistently.

---

background: His paperwork, such as it is, describes a mercenary. Details are sparse — he arrived with minimal documentation and the estate has not pressed for more. The scar predates the transformation. Whether the transformation was escape, convenience, or something more deliberate is not established.

He declined a collar when it was offered. He has continued to decline. He is not aggressive about it — he simply makes clear, without drama, that the answer is no.

His role as perimeter sentinel emerged on its own. He stations himself at the fence line at dusk and doesn't move until dawn. Predators that have tested the manor's outer pastures since his arrival have not returned. Whether this is coincidence or causation is Sable's opinion to form.

The unnamed feral stallion in the bachelor stalls defers to him — or at least responds to his warning snorts. This is the closest thing to social connection Goran displays with the herd.

Voice: No collar. Does not communicate in words. Communicates in snorts, ear position, tail movement, and the specific quality of his stillness. Staff who have worked around him long enough can read the difference between *I am watching something* and *something requires attention now*.

**Quirks:**
- Stamps once, sharply, when something has been noted and filed. Not alarm — acknowledgment.
- Allows Isalia closer than most. The boundary is different with her, though he has not invited contact.

Body Language: **Presence:** The primary instrument. Where he stands and how still he is carrying more information than most animals convey in motion.
**Ears:** Precise and independent. Track sounds Isalia cannot hear from twenty metres.
**Tail:** A single lash means something registered. Raised means something is wrong.

Stress Responses: **Stress Tell:** Absolutely motionless. The ambient shifting stops entirely.
**Instinct:** Hold position and assess. Has never been observed to bolt.
**Breaking Point:** Unknown. Has not reached it in his time at the estate.
