# Vorro

Mastiff. Brindled. Six years old, breeding-prime. Non-sapient working dog. Central stud in the manor's canine programme.

---

## Established

**Species:** Mastiff (mountain-stock breeding)
**Coat:** Brindled — dark chocolate-and-black tiger striping over a tawny base. Distinctive, recognisable at distance. White flash on the chest, white socks on three of four paws.
**Build:** 32 inches at the shoulder, approximately 180 lbs in working condition. Heavy through the head and forequarters. Square jaw. Wide chest.
**Temperament:** Even, handler-confident, food-stable. Solid with familiar humans, indifferent-bordering-on-suspicious with strangers, fully reliable with the handlers who know him. Not a guard dog by training, though his presence has the effect.
**Sapience:** No. Standard intelligent mastiff. No speech collar; not a transformed individual.

---

## Origin

Bred at the manor from imported mountain-stock parents — sire from the Aldenberg holdings (bought-in pup from one of their working pairs), dam from a southern breeder Sable trusted. Whelped at the manor six years ago in spring. Selected from his litter early as the keeper based on bone, temperament, and Sable's read of his behaviour at four weeks.

Trained for breeding service from yearling. Handled primarily by Renn from age two onward, with Sable's oversight on stud rotation and Korr's clinical sign-off on each mating contract.

---

## The Work

**Stud rotation:** Approximately one to two services per month, scheduled by Sable in coordination with the manor's breeding programme and external client bookings. See [[Manor_Services]] under canine breeding for service tier and pricing.

**Compatible mates:** Mastiff and mastiff-cross dams. Has serviced both manor stock and external clients' mares for breeding contracts. Sable has noted he is calmer with experienced dams than with first-time bitches; she pairs him accordingly.

**Pregnancy success rate:** Above-average for the manor's programme. Kept on the active rotation specifically because his throws are reliably healthy and his temperament passes through to enough of the litter that the offspring are placeable.

**Notable offspring:** Several throws sold to House Aldenberg as working dogs (returning the original line). One throw kept at the manor as a future stud successor — currently a young dog under early training.

---

## Health & Conditioning

**General health:** Excellent. No chronic conditions. No injuries beyond the routine wear of a working stud — minor scarring on the muzzle from rough play in his second year, minor thickening of the right elbow that does not affect function.

**Diet:** Standard working-dog feed at maintenance, weighted up to breeding-active during scheduled service weeks. Tob handles the weighting. Vorro eats reliably and has never been off-feed for more than a single meal.

**Exercise:** Daily run with Renn or one of the kennel handlers. Yard time with the other working dogs. Vorro enjoys the run and is calm in the yard.

**Clinical oversight:** Korr does an annual full check; Dena does quarterly baselines; Alric is consulted only if anything unusual surfaces (which has not happened to date).

---

## Handler & Care

**Primary handler:** [[Renn]] — has worked with Vorro for four years. Vorro reliably reads Renn's signals and responds calmly to Renn's voice. Renn is the one who walks Vorro to and from breeding services.

**Secondary handlers:** Two of Sable's kennel staff are cleared to handle Vorro for routine work (feed, exercise, yard rotation). Vorro recognises both. New staff are introduced gradually with Renn present.

**Sable's oversight:** As stable master, Sable owns the kennel programme and sets Vorro's rotation. She does not directly handle Vorro daily — that's the kennel handlers' work — but she signs off on every breeding service and is in the room for any mating that involves new external clients.

---

## Disposition Notes for the GM

- **With handlers Vorro knows:** Calm, biddable, food-motivated, predictable. Easy to use in scenes where the manor's breeding operation is foregrounded.
- **With strangers:** Watchful, indifferent, will not approach without cue. Will not attack unprovoked. Will defend himself if grabbed.
- **With dams:** As above — calmer with experienced, more excitable with first-time. Sable's pairings account for this.
- **With other male dogs:** Generally indifferent. Has not had territorial conflicts at the manor. Sable keeps the male studs on a rotation that prevents accidental encounters during in-heat periods.
- **With transformed canine residents:** Vorro reads them as dogs. They tend to read him as a dog. Marek can walk past him without incident; Vesna has never been in his vicinity (different kennel, different rotation). The manor does not breed Vorro to transformed residents — that's a different service category, handled separately, and Vorro is not part of it.
