Silas, the Obsidian Stallion
============================

Human → Destrier Stud, Male. Controlled intensity defines his service.

appearance: A massive black destrier stallion, his coat gleaming like polished obsidian when groomed. Standing 17 hands high, he possesses the powerful build of a warhorse — broad chest, strong hindquarters, a neck arched with natural pride. His eyes are dark and intelligent, holding a depth that belies his animal form. A silver speech-collar rests against his throat, its metal warm to the touch. Moves with deliberate grace, each step measured and purposeful.

personality: **Core Trait: Controlled intensity.**
Silas carries himself with the disciplined pride of a former knight.

**Flaw: Lingering doubt.**
Though he chose this life willingly, he sometimes wonders what life might have been if he had remained human.

background: Born Silas Aldric, third son of a minor noble house with no inheritance to expect. He was raised to be a knight's squire, then a knight himself — strong, skilled, and utterly without prospects. When his family fell into debt, they arranged a marriage to a merchant's daughter he'd never met. The alternative was the church.

Silas chose a third path. He'd heard whispers of Isalia's manor — a place where one could choose a new life, quite literally. He arrived with coin saved from tournament prizes and a clear request: make me something valuable, something wanted. He chose the form of a destrier stud, a warhorse bred for strength and beauty.

He's been at the manor for seven years now. He doesn't regret his choice — he's valued here, admired, well-cared-for. But sometimes, in the quiet hours before dawn, he remembers the weight of a sword in his hand and wonders what kind of knight he might have become.
