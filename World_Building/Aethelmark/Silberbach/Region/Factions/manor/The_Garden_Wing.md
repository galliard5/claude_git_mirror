# The Garden Wing

The east wing of Isalia's Manor, separated from the main building by an enclosed walkway and the small herb garden the wing takes its name from. House to the manor's three companion contractors and the location of all client-facing intimate-services bookings. Operationally distinct from [[Kennels_and_Stables]], which handles breeding-side work.

The manor calls it *the Garden Wing* in marketing, in records, and in staff conversation. *Brothel* is a word used by clients who prefer plain language and by no one who works there.

---

## Physical Layout

A two-storey east wing of the manor's main complex, originally built as guest quarters and converted to its current use during the manor's expansion seven years ago. Rough capacity: three companion residences, three to four client engagement rooms, common areas, and discreet circulation.

**Ground floor:**
- **Entry hall** — accessed from the enclosed walkway. Small, warm, single attendant station for Liora's daytime hand-off / Jaskin's night arrivals.
- **The common room** — large, comfortable, the social heart of the wing. Deep windows facing the herb garden. Fire kept lit through autumn and winter. Used by the companions during the day and by clients during pre-engagement conversations.
- **Three engagement rooms** — booking-suitable spaces of varying scale. Smallest is intimate-conversation focused; mid is the standard companion booking; largest accommodates extended engagements or special protocols. All three sound-buffered from each other and from the common room.
- **Service corridor** — staff access for cleaning, linen, and the kitchen handoff (food trays come in from the manor's main kitchen).
- **Private side garden access** — the door to the herb garden proper, used by the companions during their personal time.

**Upper floor:**
- **Three companion apartments** — one each for [[Vix]], [[Ryn]], and [[Pip]]. Vix's is largest (senior tenure); Ryn's is the middle and faces north toward the kennels; Pip's is the smallest and overlooks the herb garden through a single deep window.
- **Small library / reading nook** — shared common space. Books accumulated over years; not a formal collection.
- **Linen and storage** — wing-specific household supplies.

**The Herb Garden:**
The wing's namesake. A walled half-acre planted with culinary and medicinal herbs, kept by a rotating schedule between the kitchen (Tob takes the culinary side) and the infirmary (Dena takes the medicinals). The companions have free access. Pip is often there in the morning before bookings. The wall makes the garden private from the main grounds; the wing's residents and authorised staff are the only people who use it.

## Atmosphere

**Visual:** Warm. The Garden Wing's interior is timber and stone with deep red rugs in the common spaces and softer fabrics in the engagement rooms. Lamplight rather than overhead lighting. Plants on most surfaces — Pip is responsible for several of them. Clean without being clinical.

**Scent:** The herb garden carries through the open windows in summer. The fire's woodsmoke in winter. Vix's preferred incense (something resinous, used sparingly) when she's in residence. Ryn's chamomile tea is a constant baseline. The wing does not smell like sex; it smells like a well-kept household with a particular character.

**Sound:** Quiet. The wing is sound-insulated by design — engagement rooms produce no audible carry into the common spaces, and the wing as a whole is set off from the main building such that activity here doesn't travel. The sounds that *are* audible are domestic: the kettle, footsteps on timber, the low murmur of the companions talking among themselves, occasional laughter (usually Pip's).

## Reception & Intake

**Inbound bookings** come through Liora's reception desk in the main building. Liora vets the request — checks against the manor's standing flags, prior client history, and her own read of the inquirer — then routes it to [[Petra]] for scheduling. Petra books it in the dedicated Garden Wing schedule book and confirms with [[Vix]], who has override authority on companion bookings for any reason or no reason.

**Same-day or after-hours bookings** route through Jaskin (night) or Liora (day). Jaskin's notes on after-hours arrivals are processed by Petra the next morning unless Vix is available to handle in real time.

**Client arrival:** Clients are received at the main building first — never at the Garden Wing entry directly. They are walked through the enclosed walkway by the receiving staff, brought into the common room, and met there by their booked companion. The walkway transition is deliberate; it is the architectural equivalent of crossing a threshold.

**Pre-engagement conversation** happens in the common room or, for more sensitive clients, in the small first-floor parlour Vix uses for difficult intake. The companion books the time for this; it is not a free addition to the engagement window.

**Payment** is handled through Mira's office before the engagement, never within the Garden Wing itself. The companions do not handle money in their working space.

## Daily Rhythm

- **Morning:** The companions wake on their own schedules. Vix late, Ryn early, Pip mid-morning. The common room is in use for tea, reading, the day's schedule review. Kitchen delivers breakfast. The herb garden is most-used in the morning.
- **Midday:** The wing is quiet. Companions take the staff mess hall lunch (Tob saves the soft bread for Pip). Vix often eats in the common room with materials from a current booking spread out around her.
- **Afternoon:** First bookings of the day. The wing transitions from social to operational. The common room becomes the pre-engagement holding space.
- **Early evening:** Peak booking hours. Two or three engagements active simultaneously is the high-mark; three is unusual.
- **Late evening:** Bookings wind down. Pip is usually finished by ten. Ryn often holds a single long evening engagement. Vix does her best work after dusk and may be active until midnight.
- **Late night:** The wing returns to social use among the companions. Sometimes all three are in the common room. Sometimes only one is awake, reading. The fire is allowed to die back. Lamp wicks turned low.
- **Overnight:** Quiet. Engagement rooms cleaned by night staff between midnight and dawn. The wing's day cycle resets.

## Confidentiality Protocol

The Garden Wing's client-facing operations are confidential. Specifically:

- **Client identities** are recorded in Petra's separate Garden Wing schedule book, kept in a locked cabinet in her office. Mira's billing records use coded references, never names. Liora uses names at intake; Jaskin uses names overnight; both keep the names in their reception logs only.
- **Engagement details** are not recorded in any manor document. The companions retain their own private memory of engagements; they do not write them down, do not gossip about them with non-companion staff, and do not reveal client information without the client's explicit consent.
- **Recurring clients** receive priority booking and discreet handling — the manor recognises that someone who has used the Garden Wing's services has trusted the manor with information about themselves, and the manor returns that trust permanently.
- **Cross-staff inquiries:** A client cannot be confirmed as having visited the Garden Wing by a third party. If someone asks Liora *was so-and-so here last week*, Liora doesn't answer. Vogt's security people have the same standing.
- **Isalia's access:** Isalia has access to all Garden Wing records as the manor's owner. She uses that access rarely and never casually.
