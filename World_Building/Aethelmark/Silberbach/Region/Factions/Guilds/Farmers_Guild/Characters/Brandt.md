# Brandt

Male, fifties. Farmers Guild carter. Has run the manor's supply route for twelve years. Previous regular contact retired in January 1650; Brandt is now the primary contact. First direct introduction to Isalia: 22 April 1650.

---

## Appearance

Broad, fifties, working clothes. The particular weathered practicality of someone who has been on the same route in all weathers for twelve years. Cart horse patient in the traces — a well-managed animal, comfortable with the routine.

---

## Personality

Unhurried. Reliable. The kind of man who passes along useful information as a matter of course — not as a transaction, just because he's on the road and he knows people who need to know things. Has no nerves about his work in either direction. Does the job, counts correctly, notes what's worth noting.

---

## Estate Relationship

**Familiarity:** Long-established. Twelve years on route. Knows the gate, the yard, the receiving protocol. New to Rennik's stricter count policy (started 20 April) — handled cleanly, no friction.

**Awareness of estate's nature:** Knows and doesn't particularly care. Pays on time, delivers correctly, doesn't make trouble. What happens inside is their business.

---

## Voice

Plain, practical. Doesn't elaborate unless something is worth elaborating. Passes information the way a road passes traffic — it goes where it's going and he notes it in passing.

---

## Relationships

**[[Rennik_Tor|Rennik]]** — Primary contact. Professional, clean.
**[[Isalia_Kreiger|Isalia]]** — Met directly 22 April. Tipped his head — not a bow, the acknowledgment of a working man to someone running a clean operation.
