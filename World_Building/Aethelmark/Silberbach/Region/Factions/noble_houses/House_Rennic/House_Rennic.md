House Rennic
============

Lords of the Eastern Marches. Diplomatic family with discreet ties to Isalia's estate.

Crest & Colors: **Crest:** A golden bridge spanning a green valley, with a white dove carrying an olive branch
**Colors:** Gold and forest green
**Motto:** "Peace Through Strength"

Holdings: **Primary Seat:** Rennic Hall in the Eastern Marches
**Territory:** Border lands between the River Valley and the eastern forests
**Vassals:** Two minor baronies, several marcher lords
**Income:** Border tolls, timber rights, horse breeding

Key Members: - **Lord Edric Rennic** — Current ruler, aging diplomat, values discretion above all
- **Lady Seria Rennic** — His daughter, skilled diplomat, discreet client of [[Isalias_Manor_Kennels_and_Stables]], tests transformed cognition
- **Sir Gareth Rennic** — Lord Edric's younger brother, military commander of the marches
- **Young Lord Tomas R. Rennic** — Heir, currently serving as a page in the Emperor's court
- **Tomas K. Rennic** — Minor cousin, seminary-bound. See [[Tomas_K_Rennic]] for details.

Politics & Secrets: **Alliances:**
- Marriage ties to House Kreiger (Duke Romaine's principality)
- Diplomatic ties to the Emperor's court

**Rivalries:**
- House Valtor (border disputes, diplomatic tension)

**Secret Agenda:**
House Rennic serves as quiet intermediaries between the Empire and the more independent-minded River Valley nobles. Lady Seria's visits to Isalia's manor serve multiple purposes — personal pleasure, political intelligence, and testing whether transformed individuals retain enough cognition to serve as reliable witnesses.

**Relationship to Manor:**
Discreet clients. Lady Seria visits regularly, both for [[Isalias_Manor_Kennels_and_Stables]] services and to assess transformed capabilities. The house values [[Isalia_Kreiger]]'s discretion and has quietly supported the manor's interests at court.
