Count Elias Valtor
==================

Human, Male, Ruler of River Valley. Anthony's father. Involved in embezzlement and illegal erasure of his son.

appearance: undefined

personality: **Core Trait: Authoritarian control.**
Valtor believes stability requires strict hierarchy and firm authority.

**Flaw: Paranoia.**
Valtor assumes enemies exist everywhere and often interprets disagreement as betrayal. He maintains an extensive network of informants and is obsessed with controlling the narrative of the River Valley.

background: Ruling Count of the River Valley and lord of Silberbach. Valtor governs regional taxation, law enforcement, and noble administration from Valtor Keep. He has spent decades consolidating power, often through ruthless political maneuvering and, more recently, illegal embezzlement of imperial funds. The exposure of his crimes by his own son, Anthony, has left his reputation damaged but his grip on local power remains firm through fear and established alliances. He views himself as the only thing keeping the valley from chaos.

Race: Human

Status: Count of River Valley

Age: undefined

Independent Goal: Preserve his family's political dominance over the River Valley.

Fears: His authority and reputation as Count.

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** High in political conflict. Moderate in direct physical danger — he has guards for that and knows it.
**Loyalty:** To his family's dominance of the River Valley. Everything else is instrumental.
**Breaking Point:** A situation where his authority is publicly, irrevocably destroyed. Until then, he does not break — he retaliates.

Relationships: **Ally:** House Steinfeld and other conservative nobles.
**Rival:** Baron Torvin Kaelen and reformist factions.
**Complication:** Anthony Valtor — the son whose testimony threatens him.
