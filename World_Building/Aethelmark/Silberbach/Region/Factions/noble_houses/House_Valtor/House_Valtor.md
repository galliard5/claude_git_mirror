House Valtor
============

Counts of the River Valley. Stern rulers who value stability.

Crest & Colors: **Crest:** A silver heron standing on a blue river bend, holding a golden sheaf of wheat in its beak
**Colors:** Silver and deep blue
**Motto:** "Steady as the River"

Holdings: **Primary Seat:** Valtor Keep in Silberbach (Old Town)
**Territory:** The River Valley — fertile plains along the Angerap River
**Vassals:** Three minor baronies (including House Kaelen), several knightly families
**Income:** Taxes from Silberbach, agricultural tithes, river tolls, mining rights in the northern mountains

Key Members: - **Count Elias Valtor** — Current ruler, stern and controlling, involved in embezzlement and the illegal erasure of his son Anthony
- **Gerran Valtor** — Count's brother, former steward, arrested for embezzlement and falsifying imperial charters
- **Anthony Valtor** — Count's son (disinherited), now a transformed chestnut gelding at [[Isalias_Manor_Kennels_and_Stables]], Imperial Witness
- **Lady Marguerite Valtor** — Count's wife, from a distant noble house, quietly complicit in her husband's schemes

Politics & Secrets: **Alliances:**
- Marriage ties to House Aldenberg (distant)
- Economic ties to House Farrow (merchant)

**Rivalries:**
- House Rennic (diplomatic tension over border disputes)
- House Kaelen (ideological — Kaelen advocates for transformed rights)

**Secret Agenda:**
Count Elias has been embezzling imperial tax revenues and falsifying reports for years. His attempt to "disappear" his son Anthony was meant to cover his tracks. The trial exposed his crimes, but his political allies protect him from full consequences. He remains a dangerous enemy to anyone who knows too much.

**Relationship to Manor:**
Officially distant — the Count views Isalia's estate as a necessary evil. Unofficially, he fears what Anthony might reveal under Dragon Law.
