House Aldenberg
===============

Barony. Controls the mountain passes leading to the mining regions. Known for producing fine steel and weapons.

Details: **Crest:** A silver anvil on a black mountain
**Colors:** Black and silver
**Importance:** Controls the mountain passes to the northern mines. Their forges produce the finest steel in the region. House Valtor depends on them for weapons and armor. Currently neutral in the Valtor-Kaelen dispute, but their support could tip the balance.
