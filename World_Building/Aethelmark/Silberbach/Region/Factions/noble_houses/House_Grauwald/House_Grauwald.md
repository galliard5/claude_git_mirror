House Grauwald
==============

Barony. Small territory known for sheep farming and wool production. Struggling financially, seeking advantageous marriages.

Details: **Crest:** A white sheep on a grey hill
**Colors:** Grey and white
**Importance:** Produces wool for the region's textile industry. A minor house struggling financially after several poor harvests. Desperately seeking advantageous marriages for their children. Would be receptive to offers from Isalia's manor if approached discreetly.
