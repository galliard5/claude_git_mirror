House Steinfeld
===============

Barony. Controls the southern trade routes. Known for horse breeding and cavalry traditions.

Details: **Crest:** A rearing silver stallion on a green field
**Colors:** Green and silver
**Importance:** Breeds the finest warhorses in the region. Their cavalry traditions make them valuable military allies. House Valtor relies on them for mounted troops. The current Baron is a former cavalry commander with strong opinions about honor and duty.

**Dark Secret:**
House Steinfeld has quietly contracted transformed individuals from [[Isalias_Manor_Kennels_and_Stables]] to improve their warhorse breeding lines. The arrangement is discreet — they pay well for the service and maintain strict confidentiality. The Baron justifies it as necessary for producing superior cavalry mounts, but it creates potential leverage for anyone who discovers the truth. If House Kaelen's reform movement gained traction, House Steinfeld's practices could become a political liability.
