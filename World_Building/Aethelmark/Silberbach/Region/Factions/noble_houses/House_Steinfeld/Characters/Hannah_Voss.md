Hannah Voss
===========

Stablemaster of the Steinfeld estate. Has held the position for several years. Rode to Isalia's Manor to escort Viktor home on the return journey.

appearance: Perhaps forty. Weathered in the way outdoor work weathers people — competently, without damage. Easy posture, practical traveling clothes. Moves with the economy of someone who has spent most of her life around horses.

personality: Practical, direct, gives honest assessments without being asked to justify them. Does not make a fuss of things. Fills silence when she has something worth saying; leaves it alone when she doesn't. Reads horses and people with the same instrument.

Race: Human

Status: Stablemaster

background: Long-serving position at the Steinfeld estate. Was stationed at the estate for years before the Baron's death. Rode to Isalia's Manor when Viktor's return was confirmed — part practical (provenance letter, breeding schedule), part personal.

Kept the Greymarch pair in full work condition throughout the transition period. Did not move them for sale without Viktor's explicit word. Maintains full authority over stable operations and the breeding line schedule.

Key Actions:
- Escorted Viktor on return journey; conducted estate business discussion en route
- Flagged deferred maintenance items (Kern roof, Markel well) independently of Lukas
- Recommended Kellerman's crew over Druss for east pasture work
- Added Viktor to the estate breeding schedule at Viktor's own request
- Departed for Isalia's Manor carrying verbal message regarding Corvel/Aldenberg situation and legitimate business (provenance letter request)
- Collected Storm (Mira von Aldenberg, transformed Meerhold dapple-gray stallion) from Isalia's manor on behalf of House Steinfeld — carried falsified but meticulous provenance papers, Meerhold crossbreeding authorization, and registration documents prepared by Lyra. Accepted Isalia's instruction that Storm is not to be sold without his consent.

Breeding Schedule Authority: Full. Viktor has explicitly deferred scheduling to Hannah. Viktor is on the contractor list for natural breeding going forward (post-pregnancy recovery, approximately late summer at earliest).

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** High. Hannah has spent decades managing unpredictable animals and difficult people. She does not startle easily.
**Loyalty:** To the horses, then to Viktor, then to the estate. That order is not negotiable.
**Breaking Point:** A fight with no horses or people she is responsible for at stake. Otherwise she holds her ground and gives a great deal of trouble to whoever is causing the problem.
