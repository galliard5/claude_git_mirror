Edric Bruck
===========

Tenant farmer on the Steinfeld estate. Sixty-three years old. Runs sheep and grain on land his father held before him. Pays his lease early, every time.

appearance: Broad through the shoulders, dressed for outdoor work. Stands with the patience of a man who has watched weather roll in off fields for forty years. His back troubles him — he does not mention this.

personality: Stubborn as fieldstone, loyal the same way — slowly, but once it's there it doesn't move. Does not accept things on someone else's word. Needs to walk around something and look at it from every side. Not hostile. Just thorough.

Race: Human

Status: Tenant Farmer

Age: 63

background: Forty years on this land. Took it over from his father at thirty. Raised three children — elder son in Silberbach trades, younger son Tomis works the farm with him, daughter married south and sends letters. Wife passed eight or nine years ago.

Came to the Baron's funeral. Stood at the back. Left before the meal.

Had flagged the north pasture drainage problem for three years — every year told "next season." The first thing he said to Viktor after opening the gate.

First Meeting with Viktor:
- Skeptical at gate ("I'll believe it when I see it")
- Opened the gate after walking Viktor's coat and bearing
- Invited in for coffee — adjusted to water without ceremony when Hannah corrected him
- Midday meal offered and accepted after drainage issue addressed
- Closed the meeting by opening the gate himself

Practical Concerns Raised:
- North pasture drainage: three years deferred, now authorized (Druss)
- Recommended Druss personally based on neighbor's culvert work
- Flagged Kern widow's roof and Markel's well unprompted — "nothing dramatic, nothing that couldn't have been handled in an afternoon each"

Voice: Says things once. Doesn't elaborate unless asked. Corrections are delivered as plain facts ("Bruck," not "Brock"). Offers hospitality as statements, not questions.

Quirks:
- Watches people approach from a distance before they arrive
- Lets his son speak without endorsing or contradicting
- Named his dog Hund fourteen years ago and has seen no reason to revisit this

Independent Goal: Keep this land producing and pass it to Tomis in better shape than he received it.

Relationships:
- Viktor Steinfeld: cautious respect, alignment established
- Tomis Bruck: quiet partnership, complete trust
- The estate: forty years of institutional memory

Trust Level (3/5): Earned through Viktor's first visit. Will grow with follow-through.

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** Moderate in defense of land and family. Will not seek a fight. Will not abandon one if it comes to his door.
**Loyalty:** To his son and his farm first. To Viktor, conditionally — follow-through earns more.
**Breaking Point:** Any fight where holding serves no practical purpose. Edric is sixty-three with a bad back. He knows the difference between standing your ground and dying for nothing.

- Back trouble: chronic, does not discuss it. Limits sustained heavy labor.
