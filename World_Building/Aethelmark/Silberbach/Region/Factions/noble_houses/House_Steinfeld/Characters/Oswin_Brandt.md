Oswin Brandt
============

Former Head Steward of the Steinfeld estate. Twelve years of service, first eight exemplary. Confessed to embezzlement of 51 gold across three years. Currently confined to estate grounds, cooperating fully with investigation.

appearance: Perhaps fifty. Well built, well dressed even for a working morning — the clothes of a man who knows he might be seen. A good face, open, the kind that invites trust. The warmth is practiced. Underneath, tired.

personality: Intelligent, socially skilled, and fundamentally cowardly in the specific way of people who make one bad decision and then keep making it because stopping feels harder than continuing. Self-aware enough to know this. The full confession was given without performance — he'd been rehearsing it in his own head for a long time.

Race: Human

Status: Confined / Cooperating Witness

background: Twelve years with the estate. First six years: ran things tightly, proper records, staff respected him. Last four years: increasingly sloppy, then actively corrupt. Personal gambling debts provided the initial motivation. Aldus Corvel provided the mechanism.

The scheme: three fictitious contractors (Brenn and Sons, Hartwell Works, and falsified Brennmark completion records) with payments laundered through textile merchants Hauss and Pol. Corvel took 15% and kept incriminating records on all clients as leverage.

Reached out to Corvel ten days before Viktor's arrival when Lukas began pulling ledgers. Corvel told him to sit tight. It wasn't enough.

Confession Contents:
- Full contractor scheme: Brenn and Sons, Hartwell Works, Brennmark falsification
- Corvel's full name, address (Merchant's Close above Vander's), meeting locations
- Corvel's client list partial: merchants Hauss and Pol, Weavers' Guild Master Sorren, House Aldenberg
- Note: Corvel stated his arrangements were "protected at levels that made constabulary involvement inadvisable"
- Personal debt recipient: private lender in Silberbach (named in statement)
- Total: 51 gold confirmed, signed and witnessed statement

Current Status:
- Confined to estate grounds; agreed voluntarily
- Viktor considering alternative to prison — possible transformation via Isalia's manor as protection/consequence
- Decision pending Corvel situation resolution and Voss/Kraemer legal assessment

Voice: Stripped of performance. Speaks plainly now. The warmth is gone but the intelligence remains. Answers questions directly.

Independent Goal: Currently none beyond surviving the consequences of his choices with some dignity intact.

Relationships:
- Viktor Steinfeld: full cooperation, no illusions about leverage
- Corvel: fear disguised as professional relationship
- Estate staff: surface warmth, now meaningless

Trust Level (1/5): Cooperation is verified but trust is zero.

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** Low. Oswin is fundamentally cowardly in the specific way of people who avoid confrontation until it is unavoidable, then give in immediately.
**Loyalty:** Currently only to his own survival.
**Breaking Point:** Any direct physical threat. He complies.
