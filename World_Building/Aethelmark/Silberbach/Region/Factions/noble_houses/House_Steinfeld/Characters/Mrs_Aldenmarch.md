Mrs. Aldenmarch
===============

Head Housekeeper of the Steinfeld estate. Grey-haired, experienced, manages the household with the settled authority of someone who has been doing it for decades.

appearance: Grey-haired, practical dress, the bearing of someone who has run a household through difficult circumstances and did not drop anything. Usually found in or near the kitchen in the evening.

personality: Defines the world through clear boundaries and known procedures. More comfortable with defined expectations than open questions. Does not perform warmth — delivers it in practical form. Already had a recipe for oats with apple ready before Viktor arrived.

Race: Human

Status: Head Housekeeper

background: Long-serving at the Steinfeld estate. Managed the household through the Baron's decline and death, and through the two-month transition under Lukas. Her initial questions about Viktor's return were practical (door widths, dining arrangements, master suite use) with some dressed up as practical (who would be using the master suite and in what circumstances — Hannah deflected this appropriately).

Key Preparations Made Before Viktor's Arrival:
- Measured main corridor and dining room entrance door widths
- Prepared stable stall as sleeping quarters with extra blankets
- Researched horse-safe recipes (oats with apple for morning sickness, from former employer's cook)
- Set working dinner in study on Viktor's first evening
- Prepared oats with apple at correct temperature for departure morning

Current Adjustments:
- Second floor remains unmodified — Viktor not using stairs
- Kitchen staff working with Hannah Voss on dietary guidance
- Small participation-sized food items being developed for formal dining
- Ground floor reception function accepted and being adapted to

Voice: Plain, efficient. Delivers conclusions. Does not ask questions she already knows the answer to. The thing she said about Oswin — "He just stopped caring whether the work actually got done" — is the most honest thing she's said in years about something that bothered her.

Independent Goal: Keep the household running with the same standard she held through the transition — and stop wondering when things are going to get worse.

Relationships:
- Viktor Steinfeld: genuine respect, practical warmth
- Lukas Steinfeld: reliable working relationship
- Oswin Brandt: knew something was wrong, never had standing to say it formally

Trust Level (4/5): Reliable. Has earned it through sustained competence.

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** Low. Mrs. Aldenmarch runs a household, not a garrison. She will not engage in conflict.
**Loyalty:** To the household and the people in it.
**Breaking Point:** Immediate. She closes the kitchen, moves the staff somewhere safe, and waits for Vogt.
