Pell
====

Personal groom and runner to Viktor Steinfeld. Seventeen. Selected by Stablemaster Hannah Voss on Viktor's first day. Rode night courier to Silberbach with the letter to Voss and Kraemer.

appearance: Perhaps seventeen. Broad-shouldered for his age, the beginning of a working man's build. Careful handwriting — was taught properly somewhere. Quiet eyes that track a room without making it obvious.

personality: Does not speak unless he has something worth saying. Receives instructions completely and executes them without fuss. Made his decision to volunteer information about Corvel in approximately four seconds and delivered it steadily.

Race: Human

Status: Personal Groom / Runner

background: Stable hand at the Steinfeld estate. Had already noted Oswin's monthly meetings with a man named Corvel at the village inn — well-dressed, fifties, arrives with full saddlebags and leaves with lighter ones. Knew the older hands had said not to ask about it. Said nothing until directly asked, then said everything.

Rode night courier to Silberbach on Aldric (estate's fastest night horse) carrying the sealed letter to solicitors Voss and Kraemer. Signed as witness on the letter alongside Hannah Voss, under Lukas's authority stamp.

Intelligence Contributed:
- Corvel's description: male, fifties, well-dressed, monthly meetings with Oswin at village inn
- Saddlebags arrive full, leave light
- Last meeting: ten days before Viktor's arrival — same day Lukas pulled first ledger
- Older hands told him not to ask; he didn't

Voice: Economical. Says what he means. Doesn't decorate it.

Quirks:
- Stands straight when given serious instructions
- Nods once to confirm; doesn't over-acknowledge
- Handwriting is careful and clear — not what you'd expect

Independent Goal: Prove Hannah's selection of him was correct.

Relationships:
- Viktor Steinfeld: loyalty, beginning of trust
- Hannah Voss: respects her, wants to be worth her selection
- Lukas Steinfeld: professional, correct

Trust Level (3/5): Promising. Has demonstrated judgment and discretion on day one.

Combat Disposition:
**Bravery:** Moderate. Seventeen, broad-shouldered, and has already made one decision under pressure that most adults wouldn't. Not reckless, but not a coward.
**Loyalty:** To Viktor and to Hannah who selected him. Wants to prove both choices were correct.
**Breaking Point:** Situations well beyond his experience or capability. He will not pretend otherwise.
