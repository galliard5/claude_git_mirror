Transformation Potion
=====================

Alric Dain's standard product line. Not unique items — brewed in batches on a rotating schedule and stocked behind the counter in The Apothecary. Three distinct formulas cover different scopes of transformation: genital-only, partial, and full. All three share the same foundational principles of brewing and specification.

---

## Shared Principles

All three variants are governed by the same core mechanic: **species, sex, and scope are locked in at brew time by Alric, not at administration.** The potion does not determine what the drinker becomes — Alric does, during the brewing process. Once brewed, a batch will only ever produce the transformation it was made for. There is no variation at the point of drinking.

The base formula is the same across all three variants. What differs is the scope parameter Alric sets — how much of the body the transformation is authorised to affect. A skilled alchemist working from the same foundational formula can produce any of the three. The constraint on species is not the formula's capability but Alric's knowledge of the target animal's biology. A species he has studied and brewed before can be produced reliably. One he hasn't requires research first, and the formula has no inherent species restriction beyond that.

**Errors in the brief cannot be corrected after brewing.** Wrong species, wrong sex, wrong scope — these are consultation failures, not potion failures. The client drinks what was specified.

**The two-week rule applies to all three variants.** Reversion elixir cannot be administered until a minimum of two weeks post-transformation. Attempting reversion before integration is complete produces unpredictable and potentially dangerous partial results. Alric is inflexible on this regardless of which variant was administered.

**Mind is preserved across all three variants** unless mental regression is separately specified and consented to. This is estate policy, not a formula limitation.

---

## Variant I — Genital-Only

The narrowest scope. Affects only the client's intimate anatomy — external genitalia and directly connected internal reproductive structures. Everything else remains unchanged: body, face, hands, voice, sensory range, everything above and below the affected area is untouched.

The transformation is complete within the affected region — species-accurate external and internal anatomy for the specified sex, including hormonal baseline for reproductive function. What it produces is not a suggestion of the target species but the actual anatomy, fully integrated.

**Typical use:** Portal panties breeding contracts, erotic modification services, clients who want reproductive function of a specific species without broader physical change.

**Administration:** Consumed orally. Onset within seconds — heat beginning in the abdomen and concentrating in the pelvis. External anatomy reshapes within minutes; internal integration (uterine scaffolding, hormonal systems, tract restructuring) takes up to six hours. The process is not painful in the conventional sense though the sensation of internal rearrangement is consistently described as deeply disorienting. Clients are advised not to be alone during the integration window.

*Taste:* Varies by batch. The wolfhound bitch variant noted by one client as coppery and thick, clinging, resistant — as if the potion itself resisted being swallowed. Whether this is consistent across species variants is not documented.

**Portal compatibility:** When administered to a client with an active portal panties contract, the transformation affects the relocated anatomy at the board as it would any part of the client's body. The membrane reshapes around the change and the portal holds. Standard sequence for breeding contracts: portal panties first, transformation potion second.

---

## Variant II — Partial

A broader scope, stopping short of full transformation. The result is a blended form — human structure retained as the base, with animal characteristics layered over or replacing specific features. What precisely changes depends on the species specified and the parameters Alric sets at brew time: fur, ears, tail, altered hands, changed facial structure, modified musculature. The scope can be tuned toward more human or more animal within the partial range, but the outer limits of the variant are not full transformation.

The result is stable and integrated — not an in-between state that wants to resolve one way or the other, but a finished form that happens to combine both. Clients retain full dexterity, speech, and human-baseline cognitive and social function unless the specific parameters affect those areas.

**Typical use:** Clients who want a persistent aesthetic transformation, cosmetic or identity-driven modification, or a form that remains functional in human society. Also commissioned for staff or contractors whose role benefits from specific animal traits without full species change.

**Administration:** Onset and integration timeline longer than the genital-only variant due to the greater volume of affected tissue. Full integration typically twelve to twenty-four hours. Skeletal restructuring where present — altered proportions, tail integration, ear repositioning — is the slowest element and carries the most discomfort during the process.

---

## Variant III — Full

Complete transformation. The client becomes the specified animal in full — species-accurate body, sensory range, musculature, instincts, and reproductive configuration. Nothing of the original human or non-human body form remains externally. Internal structures — organs, skeletal system, nervous system — are fully replaced with species-appropriate equivalents.

Mind is preserved. The client is still themselves, cognitively intact, inside a body that is now entirely that of the target species. This dissonance is the most significant adjustment period for full transformation clients. The estate's standard care protocols for new full-transformation residents exist largely to manage this window.

**Typical use:** Clients seeking complete species change — whether for personal, contractual, or service reasons. Also the endpoint for clients who default on certain contract terms.

**Administration:** The most demanding of the three variants. Onset is rapid but full integration takes the longest — twenty-four to forty-eight hours for complete restructuring. The process involves total skeletal replacement where species requires significant size or proportion change, full sensory system replacement, and complete muscular restructuring. Clients are kept under medical supervision by Dr. Korr or qualified staff for the duration. Not administered without confirmed aftercare arrangements in place.

## Standard Stock

Pre-brewed inventory in The Apothecary reflects what the estate sells most often. Current standing stock covers the genital-only and full variants in the most commonly requested species — wolfhound, destrier, doe, boar, vixen. Partial variant stock is thinner; it is more frequently commissioned to specific parameters than purchased off-shelf. All three variants for less common species are available on commission with lead time depending on whether Alric has brewed that species before.

## Notes

*Brewed by:* [[Alric_Dain]]. Alric holds all three base formulas and all variant specifications. No other estate staff brews these potions.

*Stocked in:* [[The_Apothecary]], behind the counter. Not on open display.

*Reversion:* Covered by the standard [[Reversion_Elixir]] after the two-week integration window. Included in all standard transformation contracts unless permanence is specified and signed for. Reversion of a partial variant returns the client to their pre-transformation baseline — it does not produce an intermediate state.

*Perchance (Variant I — Genital-Only):* a brewed potion that permanently replaces the drinker's intimate anatomy with that of the specified animal and sex, leaving everything else unchanged
*Perchance (Variant II — Partial):* a brewed potion that permanently layers animal characteristics onto the drinker's body — fur, ears, tail, altered features — while leaving the underlying human form intact
*Perchance (Variant III — Full):* a brewed potion that permanently transforms the drinker into the specified animal in full, mind preserved, body entirely replaced
