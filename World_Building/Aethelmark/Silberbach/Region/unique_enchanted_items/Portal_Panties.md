Portal Panties
==============

A two-component portal enchantment — the estate's flagship service item and the foundation of most long-distance breeding contracts.

---

## Overview

The system physically relocates a client's intimate anatomy to the estate. The wearer moves freely through the world with a smooth groin; their genitalia is at the board — actually there, not mirrored or duplicated. The membrane at the wearer's groin is the portal opening through which the anatomy passed. The panties maintain that connection so the parts remain biologically continuous with the wearer's body: nerves unbroken, blood flowing, biological function intact. The anatomy itself is at the estate. Distance is irrelevant to the portal's integrity. The client in Dornmark is walking around without their parts. Their parts are in Silberbach.

---

## Components

**The Membrane (Wearable Component)**

A translucent panel of faintly luminescent material — somewhere between spun silk and the inner membrane of an egg, threaded through with enchanted spirals that pulse softly in response to body heat. When applied to a client's genitalia, the membrane adheres instantly to the surrounding skin. The edges seal with no visible seam. Under clothing it is indistinguishable from the client's own flesh — no ridge, no texture, nothing to betray its presence except a faint warmth the wearer comes to forget.

The recessed oval panel in the membrane's center accepts the client's anatomy. Correct alignment is essential and verified before activation. Staff handle all fittings. Once the permanent seal is engaged, the membrane cannot be removed without estate staff deactivating it.

**The Board (Estate Component)**

A carved oak panel roughly the size of a breadboard, its surface worked with interlocking spiral channels that pulse in time with the wearer's heartbeat once bonded. Mounted at the estate at whatever height and orientation the client's contract specifies — typically on a mounting post in the kennel play area, though private suite installation is available at additional cost.

The board is a mount and frame — it holds the client's relocated anatomy in place and accessible. What is present at the board is the client's actual flesh, passed through the portal and physically present on the estate side. A hand laid on the board touches the client. A hound mounted at the board is mounted on the client. The parts are still the wearer's — still connected, still biologically integrated through the open portal — they have simply been moved.

## Activation

Fitting and initial alignment are performed by medical staff. Once the membrane is in place and verified:

1. Staff press the palm flat to the board's center panel
2. The spirals flare deep blue and sink into the client's skin — visible for approximately three seconds before dissolving
3. The permanent seal engages with a brief, searing heat that fades to a constant low thrum

The process takes roughly thirty seconds. Clients reliably gasp. It is not painful.

## Properties

- **Relocation, Not Duplication:** The anatomy has physically moved to the board. The wearer's groin is smooth and empty — the parts are gone from their body and present at the estate. They are not copied or mirrored. They are there.
- **Portal Connection:** The membrane holds an open portal between the wearer's groin and the board. The parts remain biologically continuous with the wearer across it — nerves, blood flow, and function unbroken. The anatomy is at the board; the body it belongs to is elsewhere. The panties are what keep them the same body.
- **Biological Function:** Arousal, lubrication, orgasm, and reproductive function all operate normally. The parts are connected to the body that owns them. They behave accordingly.
- **Invisible on the Wearer:** Under clothing, the membrane is undetectable. A moderate indentation remains at the groin — roughly the sensitivity of a nipple to direct touch, fading as the client's nerves remap over the first hour.
- **Permanent Until Removed:** Deactivation requires estate staff only. No known method of self-removal. No rogue enchanter, no reversal potion, no physical force will undo the permanent seal.
- **Distance Irrelevant:** The portal does not weaken with distance. The anatomy is at the board whether the wearer is across the street or on the far side of the empire. The parts do not know or care how far away their owner is — the portal between them is simply open.

## Optional Add-Ons

**Anatomical Reroute** *(medical procedure, 15 minutes, performed by Dr. Korr or qualified staff)*

For clients whose board anatomy will receive fluids — seed, lubricant, biological byproduct from breeding — the reroute diverts the urinary and waste tracts so that bodily function at the board does not create sanitation complications at either end.

Standard reroute: the urethra is shortened and redirected to exit at the perineum, near the anus — a small, unobtrusive opening that keeps waste function entirely clear of the board anatomy. The approach mirrors nullification procedure: clean, permanent, low-complication. Muscle memory and physical sensation are preserved — urination feels functionally normal to the client, only the exit point has changed. Disorienting for the first day or two. Some clients find the arrangement satisfactory enough to retain permanently.

**Transformation Integration**

The portal membrane is compatible with all of the estate's standard transformation potions. Because the anatomy at the board is the client's actual flesh — relocated, not copied — it transforms when transformation potions are administered, the same as any part of the client's body would. The membrane reshapes around the change and the portal holds. There is one body. The transformation happens to it.
