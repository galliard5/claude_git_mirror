Renne
=====

Working hound, Maruvec garrison kennel. Flagged by Varre three weeks before the transformation as the question mark in the pack introduction sequence — the hound most likely to react unpredictably to the transformed squad members, particularly to Isandar's kobold-origin scent profile.

---

Profile
-------

**Temperament:** Cautious, uncertain when confronted with the unfamiliar. Not hostile — she processes new information slowly and needs the pressure removed before she can approach on her own terms.
**Sex:** Female.
**Pack Position:** Within the working twelve but not dominant. Responds to Kess's tone-setting.

---

Key Events
----------

**Pre-transformation flag:** Varre identified Renne as the uncontrolled variable. Kobold scent profile was undocumented in the programme — no data existed on how working hounds would respond to a transformed individual of kobold origin. Brac's familiarity with Isandar might or might not transfer.

**Post-transformation introduction (Day after, Session 06):** Isandar walked to the end of the run line and lay down at the gate unprompted — smaller, less pressure. Renne approached incrementally. Cautious, uncertain, no existing category for what she was reading. Took her time. Eventually pressed to the wire, tail moving. Isandar leaned forward, touched noses through the wire, smelled her back. Renne leaned into the wire. Stayed.

Varre, quietly: "There it is." Then after a long pause: "That's all twelve."

Still watching when Isandar left.

---

Significance
------------

Renne's introduction was the final and most uncertain step in the pack integration sequence. Her settlement means all twelve working hounds have accepted the five transformed squad members. The kobold scent profile now has one documented data point — Renne needed the pressure removed to approach, but once she did, she settled fully.

This data feeds forward to every future programme site that processes a kobold volunteer.
