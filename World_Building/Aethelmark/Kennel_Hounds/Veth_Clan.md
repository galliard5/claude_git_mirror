Veth Clan
=========

The smallest clan and the newest. The Veth arrived roughly sixty years ago — refugees from a warren collapse further south — and occupy the warren's less desirable sections: shallower tunnels, closer to surface noise, less defensible. They have made it work through adaptability and a willingness to take jobs the other clans decline.

Character: Pragmatic to the point of fatalism. The Veth do not have Rixek's deep roots or Sezzin's established surface network. What they have is flexibility and a low threshold for risk, because caution has historically not paid off for them. They are not reckless — they are realistic about what the odds look like, and they bet accordingly.

Faith: The Veth have no strong religious culture. The collapse that displaced them sixty years ago scattered whatever practices the old warren maintained, and what survived the journey was practical knowledge, not doctrine. Some Veth observe bits of various faiths — draconic tradition, surface gods, whatever seemed useful at the time. Nobody enforces consistency. Transformation is just a thing that happens in the world, like weather or bad luck. No elder will raise a theological objection.

Black Market Relationship: Operational and visible. Some Veth members are genuinely embedded in Vauclair's smuggling economy — moving goods, providing muscle for short-term jobs, running errands for people with money and reasons not to use their own people. The clan leadership tolerates this as long as it does not bring trouble to the warren and maintains enough distance to deny it if pressed. It is the worst-kept secret in the warren.

Surface Relationship: Functional but thin. Individual Veth members have surface connections; the clan as a whole does not carry the weight that Rixek or Sezzin do.

Player Implications: A Veth player starts with the least institutional support and the most freedom. Nobody expects much, which means nobody is watching closely — but nobody is in his corner when things get hard. The clan's black market ties become a complication faster than any other clan would face it. Most to prove, least cushion.

Starting Reputation: Garrison — Cold / Warren — Neutral / Street — Warm

Size: Smallest of the three clans

Tunnel Territory: Shallow sections near the surface, less defensible
