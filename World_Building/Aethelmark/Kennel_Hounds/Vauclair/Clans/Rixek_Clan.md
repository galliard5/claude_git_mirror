Rixek Clan
==========

The oldest clan in the Vauclair warren. The Rixek have occupied the deepest tunnels longer than the surface town's current walls have stood. Some elders remember when the surface settlement was timber and a market stall.

Character: Conservative, proud, slow to trust. The Rixek measure worth in clan contribution — what you bring back, what you build, what you protect. Individual ambition that does not serve the clan earns quiet disapproval rather than open hostility. They do not shout. They remember.

Faith: The Rixek observe the old draconic faith with genuine conviction — ancestor veneration, the sanctity of lineage, the belief that one's form is granted by blood and should be honoured accordingly. Permanent transformation sits poorly with this. It is not strictly forbidden, but an elder who believes scales carry ancestral memory will have things to say about voluntarily replacing them. A Rixek player faces quiet religious pressure from family alongside the clan-political pressure of leaving.

Black Market Relationship: Structural and deniable. The oldest tunnel routes under Vauclair run through Rixek territory. Some have been used to move goods around surface checkpoints for generations. The elders do not operate the black market — they permit access to their tunnels, selectively, to people they have decided to trust. The distinction matters to them.

Surface Relationship: Formally cooperative, privately territorial. Rixek members serve in the garrison as a matter of long-standing arrangement, not enthusiasm. The clan views the surface town as a neighbour it has outlasted several times and expects to outlast again.

Player Implications: A Rixek player carries a name with weight — the garrison knows it, other warren kobolds respect the lineage. But the elders will have opinions about a permanent, visible transformation. He left something behind when he walked into the recruiter's office. The clan will want to know what he intends to do about it.

Starting Reputation: Garrison — Neutral / Warren — Warm / Street — Cold

Size: Largest of the three clans

Tunnel Territory: Deepest sections of the warren
