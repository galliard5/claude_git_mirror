Sezzin Clan
===========

The merchant clan. More integrated with surface life than the Rixek, more pragmatic about what that integration requires. Sezzin craftsmen work in surface guild shops. Sezzin traders run market stalls. The clan has a reputation among surface residents for being approachable, reliable, good to do business with.

Character: That reputation is real and carefully maintained. The Sezzin understand that surface integration is an asset and protect it accordingly. They are genuinely more comfortable on the surface than the other clans — and genuinely calculating about it. Warmth and commerce are not mutually exclusive to a Sezzin.

Faith: The Sezzin are observant without being devout. They maintain the forms — festival days, the appropriate offerings, the right words at the right moments — because religious respectability is part of surface integration and community standing. Privately, most Sezzin treat faith as one consideration among many. A permanent transformation is a personal matter to them, neither blessed nor transgression. Some older members will quietly wonder. Nobody will say it to your face.

Black Market Relationship: Transactional and information-focused. The Sezzin move information more than goods — who is buying what, which gate guards are rotating, when a merchant expects a shipment. They do not consider this participation in the black market. They consider it knowing things and occasionally sharing them with people who pay. The distinction is meaningful to them and would not survive legal scrutiny.

Surface Relationship: The most comfortable of the three clans. Sezzin members have surface friends, business partners, the occasional surface romantic entanglement. The clan actively encourages surface presence as good for business.

Player Implications: A Sezzin player starts with the smoothest surface integration — the garrison knows the clan favourably, the market knows the name. But the family will expect the new position to remain useful to clan interests. A hound with a garrison badge and a speaking collar is an asset the clan will want to find a use for. Whether the player lets them is a question that will come up early.

Starting Reputation: Garrison — Warm / Warren — Neutral / Street — Neutral

Size: Mid-sized, most surface-present

Tunnel Territory: Mid-level sections, close to surface access points
