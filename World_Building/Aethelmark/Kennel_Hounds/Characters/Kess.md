Kess
====

Working hound, Maruvec garrison kennel. Pack anchor — the animal the others check back to when they're uncertain. Sable identified this dynamic from observation before the transformation.

---

Profile
-------

**Temperament:** Deliberate, authoritative. Approaches assessment the way a senior officer approaches personnel review — carefully, without rushing, and with decisions that stick. She sets the tone for the pack.
**Sex:** Female.
**Pack Position:** Anchor. The others defer to her reads. When Kess settles, the yard tension drops.

---

Key Events
----------

**Pre-transformation observation:** Kess observed Isandar in the kennel yard the morning of transformation day — a measuring look, held longer than before. She was building her assessment before the introduction sequence began.

**Post-transformation introduction (Day after, Session 05):** Isandar approached at correct distance, held steady. Kess stepped forward once, took one long deliberate breath, pulled back and lay down. Varre: "Good. That's good."

Settled for all five squad members in sequence. Deliberated longest on Perrin — she read something in him. This is worth tracking.

**After introductions:** Observed from the yard, lying in the sun with her run door open. Folding the squad into ordinary kennel life. Pack tone holding.

---

Significance
------------

Kess's acceptance was the gatekeeper event. Once she settled for all five, the remaining working hounds followed her lead. Her deliberation on Perrin is an open thread — she detected something the others may not have.
