DEVELOPMENT NOTES (GM Reference)
=================================

Strongest lines: Stealth and Essence Body Integration are both at T3 and represent Rill's core identity — the scout who became the cover story, the kobold who became the panther. These two capabilities reinforced each other during the facility operation but track different things: Stealth is about deception and behavioral simulation, Essence is about physical comfort in the form.

Watch for branch splits: Stealth has been used exclusively for deep cover / behavioral deception. If Rill begins using it for movement concealment (ambush, patrol) that's a different practice and may warrant a branch split at T2.

Nearest unlocks: Perception T3 needs one more mark and a minor gate. The gate could arrive naturally — a moment where systematic sensory tracking changes a situation's outcome beyond what baseline awareness would have caught. Cunning T3 needs two more marks and a gate — the paddock-rail initiative (offering the collar unprompted) is the first mark in what might become a pattern of Rill extending beyond operational parameters.

Undeveloped foundations: Craft is completely untouched. No making, no formulation, no systems work demonstrated in play. This is consistent — Rill operates, he doesn't build. If Act 3 involves evidence examination or collar modification, Craft may initialize.

Essence Programme Knowledge: Two contact marks but not yet a branch. If Rill begins actively engaging with programme mechanics (collar function, transformation procedure, institutional politics) rather than encountering them incidentally, this initializes as a formal Essence branch at T1.
