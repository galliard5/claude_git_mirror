Laws & Framework
================

Legal and political systems governing Aethelmark.

Slavery Laws: - Exists but is regulated
- Basic living conditions, food, and medical care required
- No mutilation or killing without cause
- Only for serious crimes or debt (with limits)
- Mental regression requires explicit consent

Necromancy Laws: - **Legal:** Speak with Dead, Deathwatch
- **Legal with conditions:** Animate Dead (requires family consent or emergencies)
- **Illegal:** Raise Dead, Soul Trapping (considered soul-theft/heresy)

Transformation Laws: - Permanent transformation via potion
- Reversion potion is cheap and routinely included
- Mind and lifespan preserved unless regression requested
- Transformed individuals retain full legal status

**Penal Transformation:**
Transformation may be imposed as a judicial sentence for sufficiently serious offenses — typically financial crimes, corruption, or abuse of civic position. The sentence is handed down by a magistrate and carried out by a licensed transformation practitioner.

Penal transformation is permanent in practice, though technically reversible. The reversion right remains with the sentencing authority, not the offender. Reinstatement of human form is rarely granted and requires a separate petition to the court.

The transformed individual retains full cognitive function and legal personhood — they remain the person they were, with all associated debts, obligations, and criminal record intact. What changes is their form and, consequently, their practical ability to act in society.

A convicted offender may be assigned to service under a specific party as part of the sentence — typically the party they wronged. A tax collector found skimming levies might be sentenced to equine transformation and assigned as a personal mount to the head collector. A fraudulent contractor might become a draft animal for the municipality they defrauded.

In practice, penal transformations are present among Silberbach's working animals — civic draft teams, personal mounts assigned to officials, labour animals on municipal contracts. The exact number is undocumented and not formally acknowledged in public records. Isalia has cited a figure of roughly a quarter of the city's ox and donkey teams in conversation, though this was stated as leverage rather than as a careful count. The actual proportion is likely lower. The broader point — that the practice is common enough to be unremarkable among those who deal with it — holds regardless of the precise figure.

**Transformation Archives & Imperial Privilege:**
Licensed transformation practitioners are required to maintain sealed records of all contracted transformations. At Isalia's estate, these archives span thirty years and document every noble, civic official, and person of standing who has undergone transformation — voluntary or judicial — through the estate's services.

These records are sealed under imperial privilege and cannot be compelled by regional authority. Retrieval requires an imperial subpoena — a threshold that Count Valtor and House Aldenberg cannot reach through ordinary means. The archives function as a quiet deterrent: those who might otherwise pressure the estate are aware that their own records, or those of their associates, sit in those files.

Town Civic Positions: - **Mayor:** Elected head of the town council
- **Bailiff:** Enforces laws, collects taxes
- **Town Clerk:** Maintains records and correspondence
- **Tax Collector:** Manages local and imperial levies
- **Deputy Justice:** Handles legal matters in Chief Justice's absence
- **Constable:** Commands town watch
- **Market Master:** Regulates market days and vendors
- **River Warden:** Oversees river trade and docking
- **Guildmasters:** Leaders of major guilds (Weavers, Blacksmiths, Brewers, etc.)

Alchemical Ingredients & Potions: **Ingredients:**
- **Primordial Ash:** Used in cautery, binds magical oaths
- **Dragon Bile:** Mixed with ash for brand reinforcement
- **Dreamroot:** Numbing agent, used during transformation
- **Emerald Powder:** Ground gems infused with druidic frost

**Potions:**
- **Truth-Tonic:** Enhances truth-detection in Pact bearers
- **Frost Salve:** Numbs surgical pain
- **Reversion Elixir:** Reverses transformation — cheap, widely available
