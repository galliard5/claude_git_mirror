## Where We Are

Isandar is on the pallet in the preparation room, mid-afternoon, collar fitted and functional, transformation complete. All five squad members are through. The recovery period begins. ## What Happened

**Preparation Day (Session 03 continuation):**
- Physical assessment with Varre at 0700 — light, observational, baseline recorded
- Individual intake reviews with Cault at 1300 — Isandar asked about the lost case directly. Cault gave the full account: fifth-hour arrest, cause unknown, monitoring protocol adjusted after, eleven administrations and one incomplete answer he's been living with. Isandar's answer: no, it doesn't change anything.
- Squad gathered in the corridor after appointments. Drifted to a harbour-side place near the fish market for an early dinner — bread, fish stew, rough wine, the kind of evening that didn't need to be anything except good and was.
- Perrin left after dinner to tell Maret the full truth. Walked back alone. The rest walked back together through the harbour streets.
- Isandar woke early. Checked on Perrin — he came back, was in his bunk, awake, nodded once. That was enough.
- Breakfast with Sable in the near-empty mess. "At least now it's today." Both agreed the waiting was the worst part.
- Walked together to the kennel yard. Sat with Brac at the fence in the early morning light until 0800. Kess observed from across the yard — measuring, not hostile.
- 0800: the preparation room. Five pallets, the rack of vessels, Cault and Varre present. Isandar set his coat aside, glanced at Perrin's ring hand, nodded ready.

**Transformation:**
- Cault administered in sequence. Isandar drank first.
- Process ran approximately six hours. Time became unreliable. Pressure through the spine was the hardest part — not pain, but outside existing vocabulary.
- Held onto the image of Brac at the fence, nose warm against knuckles. That was the anchor.
- Fourth hour was the hardest. Fifth hour: the process tipped — resolving rather than building.
- Through by early afternoon.

**Recovery — first minutes:**
- Inventory taken while lying still. Mind intact — methodical, patient, curious. Still Isandar.
- Senses transformed: smell reads in full layers, hearing differentiated and precise, Brac identifiable through the kennel wall.
- Body: olive-bronze coat (Brac's template, same colour lineage), tail present and responsive (involuntary movement noted), paws, different centre of gravity.
- Voice: absent without collar. Attempted speech — the shelf was empty. Cault fitted the collar without ceremony.
- First words through the collar: "Still me."
- Chromosome variable resolved female. Received with equanimity and genuine happiness — consistent with Isandar's stated curiosity. "Did say I was curious." Flopped back down. Actually happy it turned out that way.
- Perrin: "Good outcome?" Isandar: "Yeah. Yeah it is."
