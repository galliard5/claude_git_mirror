# Camp Rochevaux — Session 4 Checkpoint

**Campaign:** Camp Rochevaux — Kennel Hounds Military Outpost Pilot
**Session:** 4
**In-Game Date:** Night of squad transformation day — second watch, deep hours before dawn
**Location:** Animal enclosure, Camp Rochevaux — Rill is inside the cage
**PC:** Rill Sunclaw, kobold scout, panther form. Collar fitted, voice working. Transformation complete since Session 2.

---

## Summary

The full squad is transformed. Over evening mess the squad worked through what they've lost and what they've become — Renard reframing the question from *what can we still use* to *what are we now.* The buyer thread surfaced Rill's plan: a cage swap, Rill in place of the panther, Creuse none the wiser, the squad following via pine pitch on the axle housing. Tournon approved it. Daubray cut the notch in Rill's left ear — controlled, precise, wrapped — and moved the panther to the stable's back stall. Rill is in the cage. The swap is complete. Creuse doesn't know yet.

---

## Key Events

1. Squad assembled at mess for first time in feral forms — benches wrong for all of them, instincts arriving before decisions, Cook produced a second barely-cooked bowl without comment
2. Gear question raised by Duval — resolved by Renard: stop asking what we can still use, ask what we *are now.* Bows out, blades utility-only held in mouth, feral capability replaces most of it
3. Rill proposed the cage swap plan — the squad worked through it collectively, Renard added the scent trail on the wagon axle
4. Tournon approved the operation, wrote it under his name, set Renard as follow team commander — regulars handle Creuse at the gate, squad stays invisible
5. Daubray cut the ear notch, wrapped it lightly (cage latch story), transferred the panther to the stable stall; Rill entered the cage and latched in

## Character State

- **Form:** Panther, permanent. Collar on and functional.
- **Physical:** Tired. Left ear — minor injury, controlled notch, bandaged lightly. Tender, some directional precision loss on that side. Not debilitating.
- **Sensory calibration:** Near-complete per Daubray — 1 day remaining.
- **Latch:** Investigated from inside. Can lift it with the bridge of the muzzle, slow and awkward, requires time and no audience. Noted.
- **Collar:** Comes off like any collar. Off = understands everything, cannot speak.
- **Emotional state:** Settled. The decision was made in stages, tested at each step, Tournon and the squad behind it.
