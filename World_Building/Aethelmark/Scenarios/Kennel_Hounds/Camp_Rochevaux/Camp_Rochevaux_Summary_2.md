# Camp Rochevaux — Session 2 Checkpoint

**In-Game Date:** Day after Session 1 — transformation day. Sample taken morning, transformation completed by late afternoon/early evening.
**PC:** Rill Sunclaw, kobold scout, now in panther form. Collar fitted, voice confirmed working.

---

## Session Summary

### Morning — Second Enclosure Visit
Rill returned to the panther at dawn as Daubray instructed. Brought jerky. Daubray arrived and observed from a distance — noted the panther was closer than the previous day. Confirmed she was ready to take the sample.

Rill's suggestion: get more jerky, use it to distract the panther during extraction. Daubray agreed — described it as better than anything she'd planned.

### Sample Extraction
Smooth and clean. Rill at the panel with jerky, panther focused on him, Daubray approached the left side with continuous low voice. Needle in and out in under ten seconds. The panther's ear flicked, she looked at Daubray, held it two seconds, returned to Rill and the food. Daubray called it the cleanest donor extraction she'd ever done.

Daubray's instructions to Rill: nothing after midnight, eat well, rest. She'll come find him at first light.

### Afternoon — Hunting and Final Visit
Rill went out with his bow — clean rabbit kill, south slope, heard wild wolves calling at dusk from the creek (two distinct individuals). Returned, fed the rabbit to the panther, sat with her through the evening. She ate in the centre of the enclosure (closer than usual), rested facing him at the near end of the panel. He tried vocalising — low chest resonance, not words. She went still, exhaled fully, answered twice. Third attempt she ignored. The exchange felt like something real without being definable.

### Grenn's Visit (Afternoon)
Grenn came to the enclosure mid-afternoon. Watched Rill offer his hand through the panel — panther crossed to it, sniffed the air around his knuckles, pressed her nose briefly to them. Pulled back. Returned to her spot. Grenn exhaled. Rill advised him to sit with his chosen donor, bring food, give it time. Grenn confirmed his choice: direwolf. Noted Aubert wants the same — they talked. Said he'd tell Aubert about the food.

### Mess — Lunch and Squad Decisions
Squad donor choices finalised over the course of the day:
- **Duval:** grey wolf (confirmed quietly at lunch, day 1)
- **Grenn:** direwolf
- **Aubert:** direwolf (coordinated with Grenn)
- **Perret:** direwolf
- **Renard:** grey wolf

Renard announced his choice at evening mess — quietly, simply, after Perret stated his. The table settled around it without drama.

Rill's observation: *this isn't a hammer you can change out — so what you want to be is more appropriate.* Landed on the whole squad. Perret admitted he'd been thinking about the squad rather than himself. Renard noted Rill's reasoning couldn't be argued with.

### The Transformation
Daubray came to find Rill at first light. They walked to her workspace together. The prepared vial — deep amber, darker than the others, carrying the panther's colour.

Daubray explained the sequence: warmth first, pressure behind the eyes, sensory uptick, then visible changes beginning in the second hour. Cot against the wall for the hard hours. Collar fitted when stable.

Rill drank the vial.

**Transformation timeline:**
- First stage: warmth, interior, spreading outward. Normal.
- 20-30 minutes: Pressure behind the eyes. Vision sharpens dramatically — lamplight too bright, grain of the worktable distinct.
- Sensory uptick: hearing then scent, in layers. Could hear Renard at the enclosure talking to the grey wolf before moving to the cot.
- Second hour: visible changes begin. Fingers, tail, ears first. Deep cellular awareness of own form. Daubray talking throughout — naming each stage, what it means.
- Middle hours: described as large and purposeful. Not painful, not comfortable. Spine, mass, form restructuring. Daubray present the entire time, narrating. Rill nodded to confirm he was with her.
- Scent range the last thing to settle — initially overwhelming, then calibrated.
- Transformation complete by late afternoon. Stable. Collar fitted.

**First words:** *"That was very weird."* Daubray laughed — genuine, released.

Renard came to the shed door. Had been waiting outside in the cold. One pass over Rill, head to tail. Nodded. "Solid." Said the squad was at the mess whenever Rill was ready. Came himself rather than sending anyone.

### Post-Transformation — Testing the Form
Rill moved around the shed. Observations: weight distributed lower, centre of gravity shifted, movement silent without trying, tail longer and more intentional, claws retractable (flex confirmed — scratched floor). The kobold full-body stretch now extended through a longer spine. Body found movement patterns without being told.

Crossed the camp to the mess with Renard. Camp noticed. Stopped in pieces. Tessier in her doorway. Infantry slowing. Renard walked at normal pace, not escorting — beside.

### Evening Mess
Squad received Rill. Key exchanges:

- **Perret:** overwhelmed, genuine. "Hi Rill."
- **Aubert:** immediately asked about the ears, wanted the demonstration repeated. Genuinely curious.
- **Duval:** tested whether Rill could hear him from outside. Confirmed range was different. Satisfied.
- **Grenn:** "You can smell us. All of us. Individually." Connected it to a warren concept — a word for knowing where your people are without looking. Said he didn't think it applied to anything outside the warren. Visibly moved by it, quietly.
- **Cook:** produced a second bowl unprompted — salt pork, barely cooked. No comment. No explanation.

Rill ate both bowls. The barely-cooked salt pork tasted better.

Night vision described to the squad — the dark yard visible in full detail, frost with texture, each log distinct, the enclosure clear at forty yards. The panther's eyes, two points of pale green, watching the mess door.

Rill confirmed he could smell the panther from the mess. Described her scent. Grenn asked what she smelled like — connected it to the pendant and the clan question he hasn't resolved.

Renard noted the night vision has operational implications: ridge crossings that currently cost an hour in low light, split patrols in heavy weather, forward scout without a torch. Duval added three patrol routes that change.

### Final Enclosure Visit
Rill took jerky in his teeth, went to the panther after mess.

She was on her feet when he arrived. She scented the air and went still — she recognised something different about him. Crossed to the panel slowly, stopping twice to scent. Looked at his collar. Held the panel bar with her nose. Made a sound — low, quiet, not a growl. Something between a rumble and a breath.

Rill attempted vocalisation. First attempt too human. Second attempt — low resonant chest sound, the right family. She went completely still for three seconds. Then exhaled fully, body settling. Put her chin on her paws. Answered him. He tried a few more times; she answered twice more, on her terms.

She settled at the near end of the enclosure — closer to the panel than she has ever rested.
