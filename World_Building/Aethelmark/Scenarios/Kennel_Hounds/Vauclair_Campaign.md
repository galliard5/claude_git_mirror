Kennel Hounds — Vauclair Campaign
====================================

The [[Vauclair]] pilot is a border town installation — the first of its kind in Cendrel's eastern frontier. The player character is a young kobold male from the Vauclair Warren who volunteers for the sapient hound transformation and joins the garrison's Hound unit as a first-of-his-kind recruit.

Premise: The programme charter arrived in [[Vauclair]] six months ago. Kennel Master [[Renaud_Bastier]] read it cover to cover, read the alchemist's technical annex, and wrote back to the central office with a volunteer recruitment proposal within the week. Captain [[Elise_Marenne]] approved it with conditions. The programme's central office acknowledged receipt of the proposal eleven weeks later with a form letter.

The player starts pre-transformation — a young kobold at the garrison recruiter, with more ambition than options. [[Renaud_Bastier|Bastier]] intercepts him before the standard recruiter reaches him.

The offer: volunteer as the first sapient hound in Vauclair's garrison. Standard 5-year watch enlistment, pay bonus, speaking collar (his to keep), promotion opportunities equivalent to any other recruit. Permanent transformation. Full cognition retained. Donor hound of his choosing. The sex chromosome inheritance quirk is disclosed — [[Renaud_Bastier|Bastier]] read the annex.

What the Player Doesn't Know Yet: - The programme is an experiment with no established baseline. Bastier has done his homework; that doesn't mean there's a playbook.
- The other hounds will need to accept him. Pack integration is not a formality.
- Vauclair's black market is more organised than the garrison officially acknowledges.
- His Warren clan has its own relationship with that black market.
- A sapient hound with genuine investigative capability is going to become interesting to people he hasn't met yet.

Tone: Gritty and street-level. Adult content enabled. Combat is brutal — wounds linger, injuries affect capability, scars are permanent. The law is a tool people use, not a neutral force. The black market is the town's second economy and most of the interesting people in Vauclair have a relationship with it.

The player character is not special, chosen, or destined. He's a kobold who made an unusual choice and has to make it work.

Rules System: Rules-light. Track the following:

STATUS:
- Health: Healthy / Injured (minor) / Injured (significant) / Incapacitated
- Hunger: Fed / Hungry / Famished
- Fatigue: Rested / Tired / Exhausted
- Morale: Stable / Shaken / Broken

INVENTORY: Simple list. Items have condition (Good / Worn / Damaged).

REPUTATION (tracked separately):
- Garrison — how the watch sees you
- Warren — how your clan sees you
- Street — how the underworld sees you
Each starts Neutral. Range: Hostile / Cold / Neutral / Warm / Trusted

ADVANCEMENT: No formal leveling. Skills develop through demonstrated use. Acknowledged narratively when consistently shown.

Core Tensions: - The garrison is trying to control a black market it doesn't fully understand

- The player's Warren identity doesn't disappear when he transforms
