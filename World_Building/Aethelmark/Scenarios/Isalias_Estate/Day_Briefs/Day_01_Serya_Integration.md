# Session Record — Serya Integration — Day 01

**In-game date:** Montag, 14 April 1650
**Real date created:** 2026-03-20
**Session type:** Estate daily life — integration
**PC:** Serya (formerly Sergovy Waldheim), permanent female wolf, east kennels

---

## What Happened

### Dawn — East Kennels

Serya woke in the east kennels for the first time. Managed to get upright on the second attempt. Sable arrived before she'd finished orienting and took her directly to the east pasture. No preamble.

### Morning — East Pasture

Sable ran Serya through basic body adjustment work: paw isolation, weight distribution, trot at increasing speed. Four lengths of the pasture. Key developments:

- Front-left hitch identified and named — elbow, not shoulder. Sable told her to go with it rather than force a correction.
- Discovered that sound stays fixed in space rather than moving with her head — she moves around the sound, not vice versa.
- Caught fifteen of Sable's twenty-three deliberate signals on first observation (Sable noted this without praise).
- Both ears working independently by end of the session — one tracking the Brewers Guild delivery dispute at the gate while she ran.

**Brewers delivery:** Regular contact (Drest) taken ill. Covering contact didn't know the manifest. Rennik held him in the yard for twenty minutes, cross-referenced the ledger himself, resolved. Covering contact's name not established.

### Midday — Mess Hall

- Tob set a low bowl at the counter end without being asked. System already adapted.
- Carried the bowl in her mouth. Functional if undignified.
- Sat beside Marek (east kennels, wolf, four years, courier work).
- Conversation with Petra (scheduling, Elias's office) — discovered taste works differently now: individual ingredients arrive in sequence rather than as a unified whole. Tob confirmed his ingredients unprompted from the kitchen pass.
- Dena (Korr's infirmary assistant) noted for later.
- Lyra did a full-room sweep from the hearth table, clocked Serya, filed her. No interaction.
- Rennik observed from the far end, cataloguing. No interaction.

**Brief exchange with Kael** (garden wall, before mess hall): Serya stopped and sat rather than walking past. Kael noted she wasn't performing. Said they'd talk properly another time. Assessment ongoing — he's been observing from the eastern paddock.

### Afternoon — Kennel Rounds with Sable

Shadowed Sable through all six east kennel residents. Did not speak unless addressed. Key learning:

**Residents:**
- Run 1: Large mastiff type, older. Established, comfortable. Close approach, forward ears.
- Run 2: Lean grey wolf, younger. Still establishing. Weight-back approach.
- Run 3: Unknown canine. Pacing. Sable used neutral/sideways ears and waited two minutes without engaging until pacing slowed.
- Run 4: Marek. Brief check. Three-second greeting.
- Run 5: Vesna — pale grey wolf, 8 months. Sable stood four feet back, turned body at angle. Vesna came to the gate slowly. Ears half-down, came up slightly at the tips when pressure was removed. Came further than last week — Sable noted this.
- Run 6: Unknown canine. Routine.

**Vesna note:** Came further out than usual. Sable's assessment: Serya's presence (further back, lower, less direct) reduced total pressure on approach. Useful for future kennel work once Serya is settled. Don't ask about her history — she'll share when she's ready or she won't.

**Sable's signals breakdown:** Twenty-three deliberate. Serya caught fifteen. The eight missed were distance and weight — how far Sable stood from each gate, body angle. *"The eye learns faster than the mind does."*

**Key exchange:** Serya asked why Isalia would transform someone as nervous and unsettled as Vesna given the consent process. Sable: *"The consent process protects against the wrong decision. It doesn't protect against a right decision being difficult. Nothing does."*

### Evening — Mess Hall + Feed

Evening meal with staff. Conversation with Dena about the morning session. Described the trot, the taste discovery, the ear movement. *"Does it feel strange, the ear thing?"* — *"Less than it did this morning."*

Feed in the run at sundown. Body's response to it considerably more enthusiastic than expected. Noted without fighting it.
