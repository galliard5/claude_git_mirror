# Vauclair — Session 1 Summary

**In-Game Date:** Day 1 (morning through midday)
**Player Character:** Rill Duskscale, young male kobold, Rixek clan
**Status at Session End:** Pre-transformation, 3 days remaining before potion is ready

---

## Session Events

### Recruitment
Rill arrived at the garrison recruiter's office intending to enlist for standard watch duty. Kennel Master Renaud Bastier intercepted him in the waiting room, clocked him as Rixek, noted he arrived alone, and invited him to a private room off the yard.

Bastier made the offer plainly and once: volunteer for the sapient hound experiment, permanent transformation, five year enlistment, pay bonus, speaking collar, promotion opportunities equivalent to any watch recruit. He disclosed the variables without softening them and offered Rill the chance to return to the recruiter's bench if he declined.

Key exchange: Rill asked *why him specifically*. Bastier's answer — Rixek warren knowledge, arrived alone, young enough not to be set in something else, and he asked the right question.

### Briefing with Corvel
Bastier took Rill to Corvel's shop in the New Town. Corvel covered the technical variables:
- Transformation sequence: skeletal first, then musculature, then integument — approximately seven days, uncomfortable not acutely painful
- The two thin sections in the documentation: sensory transition during the final stage (modelled, not confirmed on a sapient subject) and cognitive continuity (extrapolated from non-sapient test results)
- Sex chromosome inheritance: female donor = XX result; male donor = XX or XY, distribution appears random

Rill asked directly about the thin sections and the chromosome variable. Corvel answered both without simplification.

### Kennel Visit
Bastier took Rill to meet the four working hounds:
- **Fen** — old scenthound, grey around the muzzle, calm and thorough. Assessed Rill methodically and returned to his spot.
- **Morra** — lean mid-sized female, seven years garrison work. Came to the gate immediately. Put her head under Rill's palm once, deliberately, then stepped back and watched him.
- **Vass** — young mastiff cross, still growing into herself. Wariness then enthusiasm — shoved her head against Rill's hand hard enough to rock him back. Bastier almost laughed.
- **Seld** — small terrier cross, out on patrol. Not met this session. Male, difficult, bonds hard to one handler, best animal for tight spaces and warren work.

Rill spent time with each animal in turn. Returned to Morra. Spent time in her run — she stood with her flank against his knee, steady and warm, assessed him with the same deliberate attention he was using on her.

**Decision: Rill chose Morra as his donor.**

Bastier's reaction: took Rill's answer, turned briefly to Morra's run, touched her muzzle with the ease of long habit. Didn't comment on the choice.

### Contract
Signed the following morning at the kennel. Rill read it fully before signing. Standard terms confirmed: five year enlistment, permanent transformation, one-third pay bonus, speaking collar retained regardless of how enlistment ends, no reversion clause.

Corvel took blood samples from Morra and baseline measurements from Rill. Potion will be ready in four days.

Bastier: *"Tommrow. You're still on your own time until then. "*

### Warren — Sekkel
Rill returned to the warren that evening. Went to Sekkel's workshop.

Sekkel already knew — word had moved through warren channels. They said what they thought once, didn't repeat it. Then, over two hours and a shared meal, they talked about ordinary things: the clan elder making unreasonable demands at gatherings, the eastern tunnel brace, whether it would hold through winter.

Before Rill left, Sekkel told him about their kin — a parent's sister who left the warren a generation ago for surface opportunity. Wrote for a while, then less, then not at all. Sekkel has been trying to find out what happened for twenty years. Got as far as the capital, a merchant house, then nothing.

*"I'm not telling you this to stop you. I'm telling you because you should know it exists."*

Rill: *"I'll still be here."*
Sekkel: *"Different, though."*

Parting: Sekkel said *come back when you can* — not *if*. Returned to the bench and the work before Rill reached the door.

### Day 1 — Walking the Town
Rill spent the following morning walking Vauclair's surface streets systematically:
- **Market square** — crowd patterns, chokepoints, where the Sezzin traders work
- **Harbor district** — warehouse understructure noted, gap between old wall and warehouse backing
- **East Gate** — main pass road, serious checkpoint, two guards plus senior watchman, process practiced and slightly slow
- **West Gate** — smaller, quieter, lighter traffic, noticeably less scrutiny
- **Wall walk** — town laid out below, warren entrance visible, kennel identifiable, both gate roads in view

Noted he was cataloguing automatically without deciding to.

Midday: ate at the Silver Eel, near the market square. Warm, quiet, fire going. Nobody paid him attention.
