# Camp Rochevaux — Session 1 Checkpoint

**In-Game Date:** [To be anchored — day of donor briefing, ~5 weeks after Daubray's arrival]
**PC:** Rill Sunclaw, kobold scout, 4 months at Camp Rochevaux, established squad member

---

## Session Summary

### The Briefing
Tournon called the full squad plus Daubray to the command post. Central office acknowledged donor requisition six weeks ago — no animals have arrived, none expected before month's end. Tournon proposed using the seized exotic animals (2 grey wolves, 2 direwolves, 1 black panther) as transformation donors under the argument that the charter specifies capability, not breed.

Daubray stated her position clearly: the transformation process is theoretically species-agnostic, but all her outcome data comes from domesticated hound donors. Wolf and direwolf donors are extrapolation. The panther is a further step beyond that. Her concerns are on record.

Key exchange — Rill asked whether it was simply a matter of swapping the donor sample for a different result. Daubray confirmed: the process is identical, the equipment is the same, the gap is outcome data, not procedure.

### Rill's Decision
Through a series of questions about panther form, mixed-form unit integration, instinct vs. learned coordination, and whether different predator species could be integrated into a functional squad — Rill talked himself into volunteering for the panther transformation. Decision reached organically in the briefing room. When Daubray asked for confirmation, Rill nodded.

Duval noted first: the panther has been calm since day two. Patient, not distressed.

### Post-Briefing
- **Renard** found Rill at the enclosure. Confirmed the decision wasn't pre-planned — Rill made it up in the room. Renard accepted this, noted Rill's question-by-question reasoning, said he couldn't argue with it after the fact. Ordered Rill to put everything on the table with Daubray that afternoon. Revealed he hasn't decided his own form yet. Noted Perret will likely follow whatever Renard chooses.
- **Duval** confirmed his choice quietly at lunch: grey wolf.
- **Grenn** hasn't decided. Cultural weight acknowledged — clan has complicated views on permanent body alteration. Hasn't told them about the programme.
- **Aubert** no strong preference stated. Likely direwolf given his role profile.
- **Perret** hasn't decided. Admitted he'd been thinking about what makes sense for the squad rather than what he wants to be. Rill's observation — *this isn't a hammer you can change out* — landed visibly on him.

### First Contact — The Panther
Rill spent the morning at the enclosure. Brought jerky, fed her through the panel, sat with his back against the timber frame and watched the camp. She accepted the food, returned to her position, and after ~15 minutes lowered herself facing him. Quality of attention shifted from assessment to something quieter — present without requiring active monitoring.

Evening: Rill hunted a rabbit (clean shot, 30 feet), heard wild wolves calling from the creek south of camp. Returned, fed her the rabbit. She tracked him in the dark, ate efficiently, came back and held a low resting posture facing him. Stayed until cold drove him in.

### Daubray's Workspace — Afternoon
Daubray confirmed:
- Donor size affects transformation duration: standard hound = 6-8 hrs, direwolf adds 2-4 hrs, panther estimated 10-12 hrs (watch signs not clock)
- Recovery: first day calibrating new sensory range, weight, balance
- Speaking collar not fitted until stable — Rill will understand everything but cannot respond verbally during recovery
- Daubray will be present throughout, narrating what she's seeing
- Instruction: give it another morning at the enclosure before the sample is taken. Not a condition — care.
- Samples taken tomorrow morning if squad confirms choices today/this evening. Transformations begin the following morning, one at a time from first light.
- Speaking collar is permanently Rill's per Vellancourt's charter.
