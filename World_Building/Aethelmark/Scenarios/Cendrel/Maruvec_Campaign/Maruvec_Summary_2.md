## What Happened

- Cault arrives Thursday morning. Isandar meets him in the kennel first. Cault notes the three-week timeline to Brac, calls it correct.
- Cault gives the honest count: nine good, one hard (undisclosed prior preparation — irregular uptake, seven hours, he came through), one lost (military outpost, arrest in the fifth hour, cause unknown, monitoring protocol adjusted since). No softening.
- Sex chromosome question: Brac is male → outcome is variable, unpredictable from inspection. Isandar admits curiosity about the other side. Cault confirms one prior volunteer chose a female donor deliberately and came through well. Isandar holds with Brac — "how I actually work."
- Dix absent all morning. Isandar goes to The Chains, finds her emerging from a warehouse passage on Longers Wharf. She'd gone to close arrangements with a dock contact before the transformation made the conversation complicated.
- Isandar walks her back without pressing. Points out she'll effectively disappear as far as those connections are concerned — no thread between a kobold recruit and a garrison hound. Dix recalibrates visibly. Thanks him by first name. First time.
- Varre interviews Dix: clipped, direct. Dix discloses the connection ("cargo coordination operation"), says it's closed, says nothing ongoing. Varre accepts. One line in the ledger. Isandar stays in the corner throughout.
- Varre to Isandar on the way out: "Thank you for bringing her in."
- Squad reconvenes on wall. Isandar reports Dix is handled. Practical logistics question raised — doors, latches, reports, signatures, collar in noise, evidence handling, public visibility, field ops, latrines, equipment carry. Nothing in the programme documentation covers any of it.
- Sable writes the list without being asked. Decision: take it to Varre and Cault together this afternoon. Whatever they work out, the next site won't have to start from scratch.
- Perrin: "Someone's going to read this and not have to figure it out from scratch."
