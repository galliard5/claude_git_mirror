## What Happened

- Woke at dawn, moved to window beside Théo — brief quiet exchange about the new hearing, Théo admitted he slept badly (every squad movement arriving clearly in the night)
- Drank from anteroom bowl without difficulty. To the yard at first light.
- Brac introduction: walked to east run fence, sat in the same position as the three-week routine, Brac approached and touched noses through the wire — Varre opened the gate without a word. Entered. Brac leaned shoulder to shoulder and stayed. Isandar smelled him back and leaned in. Both stayed until Varre came for the next introduction.
- Kess introduction: slow approach to gate, correct distance, held steady — Kess stepped forward once, took one long deliberate breath, pulled back and lay down. Varre: "Good. That's good."
- Stepped back, let Varre run remaining squad introductions — Théo, Sable, Perrin in sequence. Kess settled for all five. Deliberated longest on Perrin.
- Settled beside Perrin during his wait — he disclosed Maret cried again (different kind — not grief, something else), that she asked to come to the harness fitting, that Varre said yes. Luc asked to come too and was told no but still asked. Isandar's question about Luc produced the first real laugh from Perrin.
- Returned to Dix and Théo after Perrin's introduction. Lay down in the yard sun between them. Dozed.
