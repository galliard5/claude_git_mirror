## Event Log

### Steinfeld Estate Scenario — Day 1

**Day 1 — Viktor's Return**
- Viktor arrives at Steinfeld estate with Hannah Voss after road journey from Isalia's Manor, including tenant farm visits.
- Edric Bruck visit: gate opened after walking Viktor's bearing. Drainage repair authorised. Monthly tenant runner established. Midday meal. Flagged: Kern roof, Markel well.
- Arrival at estate: Lukas presents embezzlement evidence. Three years of fictitious contractors (Brenn and Sons, Hartwell Works, Brennmark falsification).
- Oswin Brandt confession obtained. Signed statement. Confined to estate grounds voluntarily.
- Pell identified as personal groom and runner. Contributed Corvel intelligence (monthly meetings at village inn, saddlebags arrive full, leave light, last meeting 10 days prior).
- Pell dispatched night courier to Voss and Kraemer (independent solicitors), Silberbach. Letter sealed with Steinfeld signet, witnessed by Hannah and Pell under Lukas's authority stamp.
- Constable Ferris notified of Corvel/Vander's situation by separate letter. Asked to move before Corvel relocates strongbox.
- Hannah Voss departed for Isalia's Manor: provenance letter request (Viktor's foal) + verbal message re: Corvel/Aldenberg for Duke Romaine.
- Estate decisions made: Kellerman (east pasture), Druss (Bruck drainage + Markel well), stablehands to Kern roof, ground floor reception conversion approved, Viktor operating from converted stable stall.
