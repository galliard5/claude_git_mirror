---

the world is a standard dnd setting with various races, but with a dark fantasy overtone.
the story starts in a medium sized town 
there is a kobold warren that is adjacent to/underneath the town. 
there is some tension between them, but they mostly co-exist and support each other, but they each have their own government 
the surface town is the traditional dnd style medival town, It has some minor nobility 
the kobold warren is based more on family clan and is more traditional. 
the warrens would generaly frown upon the transformation for religious reasons 
the town garrison and guard includes members from the warrens as well. the garrison currently has no hounds.

there is also a wider fantasy world outside 
there is some corruption and poverty 
there may also be some banditry 
the concept is i'm a young kobold male about a month into his 5 year enlistment with the guard, he has a normal guard enlistment, promotions and the like as they are available. 
one of the nobles in charge of the town decides the town needs some hounds to suppliment the guard, but not normal ones. he wants to do an experiment and see how well sapient hounds will work, so he comissions a ritualist to develope a transformation ritual. it requires a sample from the being they will be transformed into, and the noble provides a his pack of wolf/hound hybrids. they are told almost no info about it, except the noble is doing an experimental guard formation, and they will get a sizeable bonus and pay increase.  5 guards volunteer for the experiment and i am one of them.

turns out the ritual is permanent, but will keep full mental capabilities, and will maintain his natural lifespan. 
they will also receive a magical collar that allows him to speak, theirs to keep. 

the transformation will take about an amount of time deemed appropriate, but not instantanious. 
the feeling is more disturbing and uncomfortable than painful 

they did not realy plan well for this, so they will have to figure out how best to feed and house them and how to work in their new forms, and also how to be guard/watch dogs for the guard 

since the method is experimental, and hasnt been tested yet they dont know that it creates a genetic sibling of the animal used for the sample. it also means that the new form will inherit the sex chromosomes, so a female source will always be an xx, but a male would result in either xx or xy. will assume that the magic prevents yy. the player isnt informed of this because the fact isnt known before the experiment. the player will end up female. other characters will follow the rules. will include a surprise sex transition, and dealing with the results in his new environment, including heat and possible intercourse and breeding 
the story starts pre-transformation 
i was thinking something rules light, such as tracking inventory and status such as hungry or injured somewhat dark and gritty theme overall
