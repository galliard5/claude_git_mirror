## Three Species

### Humanity
Standard humans with ~15 years extra lifespan (average 125 years). Split between the **Eurasian Axis** and the **Federation** factions.

### Uplifted
Descendants of genetically altered human clones, originally created as expendable soldiers and cheap labor. Over generations they disseminated throughout colonies and were accepted as equals. Take the form of humanoid animals or, more rarely, sapient regular animals. Commonly incorporate some cybernetics.

### Augmented
Originally weapons-of-war created from humanoid clones, but heavily cybernetically enhanced rather than genetically altered. Only **100,000 exist** in the entire system. Capable of self-replication. Highly sought for technical expertise. Introduced to the Resource War by the Eurasians for equipment repair.

## Three Factions

### Eurasian Axis (Vega 5)
Communist superstate modeled on Cold War Russia. Premier Olgovsky leads via terror and military force. Citizens work Mon–Sat, 8 hrs/day for minimum wage, one-room house, one vehicle. Marriages arranged by State. Weekly Gestapo checks for dissidents. Daily emotion-suppression drugs mandatory. Arts and hobbies are criminal. No arts, no holidays.

Two large **Fortress-class space stations** in orbit for defense, repair, and ship construction.

### Federation (Vega 6)
Modeled on modern America. President-led democracy. Citizens work Mon–Fri, 6 hrs/day, receive large houses, own furnishings, and vehicles. Arts encouraged (Venith in particular is famous for massive sculptures). High education standards. Planetary defense grid in orbit (satellites + ground-based launchers).

### Pirate Clans (Vega 9 / Tortuga)
Displaced Federation citizens who lost their homes to Axis occupation after the Resource War. Settled inside the asteroid field of Vega 9 in the hollowed-out city of Tortuga. Modeled on 18th-century pirates, including clothing and attitude. Operate on a code of honor but are otherwise unorganized — clans often fight each other. Unified only in their hatred of the Eurasians.

Run the **Black Market** across the solar system (weapons, armor, vehicles). Governed at Tortuga by the neutral Banking Clan. The first clan to assemble a proper fleet would force others to swear allegiance.

**Pirate Code of Honor:**
1. If quarter is asked, it must be given.
2. Always respect parlay.
3. Pirate Lord is the oldest, most respected pirate — must be obeyed.
4. Only good Eurasian is a dead Eurasian.
5. The Clan is Mother, the Clan is Father. The Clan is family.

## History: The Resource War (449–514 AP)

The Eurasian Axis declared war in 449 AP to seize Vega 6's resources after Vega 5's supply depleted. Key events:

- **Early Axis advantage:** Space-going combat drones gave the Axis a decisive early advantage, destroying 1/4 of the Federation fleet.
- **Axis land invasion:** Axis troops occupied southern Vega 6.
- **Federation counterattack:** Introduction of the first Uplifted forces reversed the tide. Axis forced to retreat.
- **Axis total war:** After losing ground forces, Axis expanded across the solar system, fortified colonies on Vega 1–4, and contested Vega 7.
- **Nuclear phase:** Axis used strategic nuclear weapons; most intercepted in space. One reached Hampshire (Federation's largest city): ~1,000,000 killed.
- **Armistice:** 514 AP. Has held for five years as of 519 AP.
- **Post-war:** Displaced Federation citizens formed the Pirate Clans.

## Xyleon Artifacts

First discovered 515 AP. Two found so far:
1. **Vega 3** (Eurasian mining): Gold sphere, beachball-sized, alien hieroglyphics, inert. Surrounded by faint gravity field. Interior is "liquid metal" that can reconfigure itself and alter matter at the atomic level.
2. **Vega 7** (Federation research): Giant pyramid, house-sized, found in ruins of an alien city in a polar ice sinkhole. Attempted activation caused it to absorb a power cell and reconfigure it as a short-lived power source.

Both the Axis and Federation are hunting for more. Widely believed connected to the large alien ring structures.

## System Hazards

**Gravitational Hurricanes:** Violent surges (~1 AU diameter) that can destroy ships. Appear suddenly, travel outward from the star, and disappear at the asteroid belt. Common February–August. Ships carry scanner suites to detect them. No inhabited world has been struck yet, but research is underway on protective measures.

## Technology

**TL9 GURPS.** Armor and weapons from Ultratech. No ETC weapons (not yet developed). Standard ammunition is free (manufactured by ships' onboard factories). Specialized ammo costs 50× listed price.

**Starships:** Nuclear reactor (15–17 year lifespan). Electromagnetic ion thrusters (efficient). No gravity except centrifuge providing 0.5G for crew quarters. Armored in overlapping layers. Guided missile batteries (range 32,000 miles). Point defense system (anti-missile + EW + gatling). Combat drones (Axis innovation, 75,000-mile range, immune to jamming). Crew 12–50 depending on automation. Onboard microfactory produces standard ammunition.

## Calendar

After Planetfall (AP) calendar. Based on Earth standard (365 days, 12 months, same month names, leap year every 2 years). The Federation and Pirates observe Christmas and New Year's; the Axis suppresses all holidays.

## Setting Context

This is Kai's sci-fi setting — "KaiSF." The player faction in the campaign is the Pirates. The setting uses GURPS TL9 mechanics with Ultratech for equipment. The Tauran Vega writeup is a GM/player reference document detailing the entire solar system, its history, factions, and technology.

Connected characters from this system may include any of the group's GURPS space-campaign characters.
