## Atmosphere & Mood

Dark, damp, cluttered. The hut leans slightly to one side as if tired of standing. Twisted branches hang from cords across the doorway — bones, feathers, dried plants tied in tight bundles. Inside: thick incense, damp rot, shelves cluttered with jars (herbs, and things that used to be alive). A low fire crackles beneath an iron cauldron. The smell of smoke and strange powders. Remote — no one visits.

---

## Key Features

The hut is deep in a forest of tall old pines on higher ground, birch and alder in the lowlands near a river. The witch follows narrow animal trails and old footpaths to reach the main road (north-south). A broken stone marker half-buried in moss marks a long-abandoned road nearby.

**The Shelf:** Among other bones and jars, the PC's skull sits here. This is the strongest secondary anchor — the skull remembers being the kobold and holds identity resonance. The PC can shift awareness here for short periods.

**The Staff Hook:** Where the witch hung her staff (now broken) by the door. The gem hung from the staff as a bauble.

**The Bone Tree:** A dead oak in the forest where dozens of bones hang from branches like wind chimes. The witch leaves offerings here — some sort of communication with other practitioners or forces. Suggests she is part of a tradition, not a lone operator.

---

## The Bone Hill

Adjacent to or very near the hut. A low hill where the witch scattered the PC's remaining bones after the sacrifice:
- **Pelvis:** Buried in the hill. Central structural stabilizer of the anchor network. Now serves as the keystone of an awakened lattice node.
- **Rib:** Hanging on the hill. Life resonance anchor.
- **Femur:** Inside a hollow tree near the hill. Movement resonance anchor.

The hill sits above a secondary seam connected to the ancient reservoir beneath the tower. This seam amplifies magical resonance and strengthens the PC's anchor network. The PC successfully awakened this location as an active stabilizing node in the ancient lattice — their soul network now functions as its focus.

A keystone slab with a depression (where a relic once sat) lies at the center of the node. The depression now glows faintly — the PC has effectively replaced the missing relic.

---

## Current Situation

The witch is gone — taken prisoner and transported to the capital. The hut is empty and unguarded. No one visits. The skull remains on the shelf. The bone anchors remain on the hill. The awakened lattice node continues its quiet work redistributing pressure through the ancient system.

This location is both the PC's most vulnerable point (all secondary anchors are here) and their strongest asset (the active lattice node amplifies their distributed existence).
