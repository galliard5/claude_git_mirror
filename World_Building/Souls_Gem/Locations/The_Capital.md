## Atmosphere & Mood

A vast stone city spreading across a valley. Massive walls ring the perimeter with guard towers every few hundred paces and banners snapping in the wind. A great stone bridge crosses a deep river to the main gate. Inside: narrow streets packed with people, market squares bursting with stalls, smoke from bakeries and forges, temple bells, a thousand conversations layered together. The cathedral towers over everything.

---

## Key Features

**The Walls and Gates:** Heavy wooden gates between thick towers. Long queues of merchants, farmers, and travelers. Guards check documents and inspect cargo. The envoy's banner grants priority passage — the escort was waved through without waiting.

**The Cathedral:** The city's dominant structure. Enormous stone roof, tall corner towers, stained glass windows blazing with light, wide steps descending into a great public square. Pilgrims kneel, priests move through crowds, guards patrol the perimeter.

Beneath the cathedral lies one of the major anchor nodes of the ancient lattice system — far stronger than the hill node the PC awakened. Multiple lattice channels converge here. The node is not damaged and not dormant, but heavily reinforced. The church has spent centuries building around it. Layers of divine architecture surround and incorporate the ancient structure.

**The Cathedral Foundations:** According to guard conversation and a memory extracted from a guard's mind: after a collapse, engineers dug beneath the cathedral floor and found old stone — not temple stone, older. The archbishop ordered most of it sealed off. Engineers continued working down there for years, "rebuilding something." The priests expected the collapse, which is suspicious. The capital watch cousin who shared this story considered it better left alone.

**The Archivists:** The cathedral library contains scholars who "remember things from five hundred years ago like they happened yesterday." If the lattice has a documented history, they would know it.

**The Bishop and the Order:** The envoy serves the bishop's order. The witch is being brought here for judgment. The necromancer's book and the gem (with the PC inside) are evidence. The order's relic vaults may contain artifacts related to the ancient lattice system.

## What the PC Knows From Scouting

The PC rode a crow over the city walls and observed:
- The cathedral's lattice node is real and powerful — felt even from the air
- The cathedral has been built atop and around the ancient structure deliberately
- The city is dense, chaotic, and full of thousands of minds
- The crow could enter freely — guards don't watch birds
- A priest's surface thoughts were accessible (the PC briefly touched one but details of that interaction are in the story)

## Current Situation

The escort has just passed through the city gates. The witch is chained, the envoy leads, the guards ride in formation. They are heading deeper into the city toward the cathedral or a holding facility. The PC is inside the gem in the envoy's saddle pouch, having just entered the most densely populated and magically complex environment they have ever encountered.

The cathedral bells are ringing the hour.
