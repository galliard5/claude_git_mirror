The Necromancer
================

Dead. An unnamed necromancer who occupied a ruined tower north of the witch's forest territory. He was performing rituals that drew on the ancient reservoir buried deep beneath the tower, damaging the divine lattice seal in the process. The witch entered the tower and destroyed him using the gem's power — an act that burned out the gem's outer layers (as far as anyone can tell) and caused a massive magical backlash.

His ritual book survived and was confiscated by the envoy. The book contains detailed notes on corpse anchors, thread control diagrams, soul fragment containment theory, and multi-anchor soul structures. The PC has studied these extensively and learned the theoretical framework for their own anchor network from these pages.

Key details:
- His binding technique was crude — he tore at the dead rather than guiding them, leaving ragged magical edges.
- His ritual nearly breached the ancient lattice, which would have released the reservoir's dormant power upward.
- He copied his diagrams from older, more careful sources — he was a student, not an innovator.
- His soul containment notes included a fourth component (echo stabilization) that the PC had already been doing instinctively.
- He is dead. His body was at the tower when the envoy's inspection team arrived.

Race: Human (presumed)

Status: Dead

Location: Died in the tower. Body presumably still there or buried by the inspection team.

Significance: His book remains the PC's primary source of theoretical magical knowledge. His damage to the lattice is the wound the PC is slowly learning to repair. His tower sits above the largest fracture point in the regional lattice network.
