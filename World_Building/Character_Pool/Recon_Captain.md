## Hooks & Notes

The two headline skills — **EW 17** and **Gunner (Starship) 17** — are an unusual combination for a recon captain. Most recon roles prioritize stealth, observation, and infiltration. This one is also the person who operates the ship's electronic warfare suite and fires the weapons. The 240-point budget let her be comprehensive: the ground operations suite (Scuba, Vacc Suit, Survival, Tracking, Stealth) is a full special-forces package alongside the starship combat role.

The **Honest Face** perk is interesting for someone who runs recon and does cryptography. She looks trustworthy. This could be cover — or it could be genuine, which would be both an asset and a liability in intelligence work.

**Weirdness Magnet** appears on Eve Schallow and Antoine Blumberg in the same Legion setting — it may be a recurring motif in Rick's Legion campaigns, either as a faction trait or just a pattern in Galliard's character choices for that setting. Three out of three named Legion characters have it.

**Xenophilia** creates constant low-level tension for a military officer. She has to make will rolls not to be fascinated by alien species — which in a Legion setting with multiple species means this triggers regularly. The Honest Face plus the Xenophilia paints a picture of someone who consistently ends up trusted by alien contacts in ways that surprise her commanding officers.

The unnamed status is notable at 240 points — she's the most mechanically developed character in this batch without a name on the sheet.
