## Hooks & Notes
The BBQ skill at IQ+4 and Hooch at IQ+2 are the most heavily invested non-medical skills on the sheet after the medical suite — more points than his combat skills, more points than Battle Suit. Antoine is *serious* about his cooking and his brewing. In a Legion campaign, he is the person who sets up camp, feeds everyone, and then performs surgery.

The Weirdness Magnet and Truthfulness together create a specific social texture: strange things happen around him, and he can't convincingly deny that they do. He's the person who always has an inexplicable story and can't make it sound less weird than it is.

DR 4 (Back, piercing/slashing) is the natural shell. In powered armor with DR 75+ on torso, this is largely academic — but it's there, and it's genuinely him rather than equipment.

Kai's "new new new legion game" label implies several previous attempts at a Legion campaign — this was the fourth. The character being a left-handed armadillo medic who makes excellent BBQ and can't lie is possibly why the campaign kept getting restarted.
