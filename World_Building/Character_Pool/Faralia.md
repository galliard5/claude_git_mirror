## The Two Faralia Files
The level 1 NE version (`ravy's evil kobold.pcg`) has different base stats (STR 10, CHA 16) and only Scribe Scroll as a feat. It also has a mule, a cart, and 20 days of trail rations — functional adventuring loadout. The alignment shift from NE to LE and the stat differences suggest either a different design iteration or a rebuild between sessions. The character name "Faralia" is consistent across both files; the filename "ravy's evil kobold" names the concept rather than the character.

A Lich kobold sorcerer is a very specific power fantasy — she's already dead (Undead template), already has all the magical gear, and is running Silent Spell with Vampiric Touch and Spectral Hand (the touch-delivery combo). Point Blank Shot on a lich sorcerer is an unusual choice — possibly for Scorching Ray delivery.
