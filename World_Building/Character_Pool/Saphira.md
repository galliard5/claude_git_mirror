## Hooks & Notes
A young dragon still growing into her power. At level 6 she's a capable melee combatant with multiple natural attacks and flight, but the Draconic Exemplar progression clearly has a lot of headroom. The witty/copper affinity gives her a personality hook — copper dragons are traditionally playful, sarcastic, and fond of riddles. Frightful Presence 4/day is already a battlefield presence tool. Her exceptional Intelligence (18) means she's not just muscle. Good fit for a campaign where a player wants to play a growing dragon from a relatively early stage, or as a younger dragon NPC/ally. The acid affinity and Auran language hint at an aerial/underground elemental setting.
