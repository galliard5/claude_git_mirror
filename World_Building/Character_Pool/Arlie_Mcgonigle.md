## Hooks & Notes
The prosthetic hands and mechanical translator define her identity before anything else — she's a chocobo who built or acquired solutions to the two biggest chocobo limitations, and has invested in steamtech engineering at a level that suggests she could build more. Mechanic (Steamtech) at 17 is near-mastercraft territory for a 200-point character.

The fighting style is pure physical: 10 points in Brawling, full technique suite for kicking, head-butting, and wing-bashing, plate armor on the torso. The gun skills at 1pt each are breadth coverage rather than actual specialization — she can use firearms but they're not the plan. The plan is to be in melee with those talons.

She's 17 years old and already wearing steel plate and repairing steam engines. The TL5 (steampunk) setting means she's operating at the edge of industrial-era technology — cannons, pistols, steam.
