# Icarus Drone

**System:** GURPS 4e, TL9 (GCS native format)
**Original Campaign:** Kai pirate game
**Player:** Galliard
**Created:** Nov 25, 2010 | **Modified:** Mar 2, 2011
**Point Total:** 100

## Overview
The Icarus Drone is not an independent character — it is the **remote body** of Icarus the uplift dolphin, controlled through his Machine Telepathy psionic ability. The drone is what Icarus looks like to anyone standing on the ship. The dolphin is somewhere in a water-filled compartment, psionically driving this.

### IQ Note
The drone's base IQ is listed as 22 in the record (reflecting Icarus's intelligence running through the link), then reduced by Decreased Intelligence 10 (-200 pts, -10 IQ) to an effective **IQ 12** for autonomous function. When Icarus is actively controlling it, the intelligence is his; the reduction represents the drone's own onboard processing without the live psionic link.

## Perception Profile
**Per 22** is the headline. Combined with Discriminatory Hearing (Profiling), Imaging Radar, Sonar, Active IR, and a 240° active arc, the drone functions as an extraordinary sensor platform. It detects things Icarus himself cannot perceive through water — this is part of why the pair works.

## Hooks & Notes
The drone is carrying weapons that require ST 20M to operate — which its ST 20 precisely meets. It's not overbuilt for the load; it's built to carry exactly this. The torso has DR 75 total (30 + 45), making it extremely hard to stop with anything short of dedicated anti-armor fire. Per 22 with multi-spectrum sensors means it's nearly impossible to surprise.

The four legs give it stability advantages that a biped wouldn't have carrying this weapons load. The Indomitable trait means no morale break, no intimidation, no fear — it is entirely indifferent to threat.

The Addiction to Electricity is a clever touch: the drone "craves" its power source. Legal and Cheap means it's not a serious disadvantage mechanically, but it's a flavor detail that makes the drone feel like it has some personality beyond pure utility.

The real hook is the relationship: this thing is a 300 lb dolphin's right hand. When Icarus the pirate captain negotiates, the drone sits in the room making everyone quietly uncomfortable with its sensor array and chaingun. When things go wrong, the dolphin's Per 14 and the drone's Per 22 are both in the fight simultaneously.
