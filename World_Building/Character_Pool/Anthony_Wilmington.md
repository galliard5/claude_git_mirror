## Hooks & Notes
A dragon who went into business. The combination of Research 70%, Business and Finance 55%, Law 45%, and multiple languages including Draconic suggests a character with serious institutional presence — he has the knowledge base of a well-educated corporate operator, combined with P.S. 34 and an electrical field power that fries anyone who gets close. The Reptile Brain (predator) disadvantage is the wildcard — he's suppressed it well enough to run a business, but it's still there. P.B. 25 with Exceptional PB power means he's genuinely striking in appearance, which tracks with the "business tycoon" concept. The name Anthony Wilmington is deliberately mundane, which suggests he operates in a context where dragons pass or blend among humans. The campaign name "Dragon Business Tycoon" implies a setting with significant dragon/human economic interaction — could fit Gallihammer's more modern settings or a modern-day HU campaign.
