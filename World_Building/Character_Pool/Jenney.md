## Notes
Gold skin and gold eyes with no hair is a distinctive visual choice — not a standard human appearance. Possibly a celestial bloodline, a divine blessing made manifest, or just an aesthetic.

The WIS/CHA combination means exceptional healing (paladin lay on hands scales with CHA), better saves (paladin divine grace adds CHA to all saves), and strong social presence. The DEX 8 is the tradeoff — this is a paladin who stands in one place and hits things very hard while allies flank him.

Great Cleave is genuinely powerful in mob scenarios: every kill triggers another attack. Against multiple weaker opponents he can chain through an entire group in a single round. Against single powerful opponents he has the adamantine shock bastard sword for steady high damage.

The Ring of Sustenance means he doesn't sleep or eat — which in a dungeon-crawl setting is a genuine tactical advantage: he's always at full strength, and ambushes during rest don't affect him personally.
