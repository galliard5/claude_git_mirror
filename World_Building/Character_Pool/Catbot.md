## Hooks & Notes
The Catbot is a triumph of misdirection — at SPD 880 (roughly supersonic) she can vanish before most opponents react, her AR 15 makes her nearly immune to normal weapons, and her bio-realistic construction (real fur, artificial blood, big cat vocalizations) means she passes as an organic animal until she opens up with electrical discharge or deploys the wrist blaster. The safecracking, pick locks, pick pockets, and prowl skills paint a clear picture: this is a thief robot disguised as a pet. The $32,500 remaining budget suggests one final small upgrade may have been planned but not recorded. The artificial blood system is the single most telling detail — it's expensive and serves no combat function, only organic deception. Great fit for espionage, heist, or any setting where a "cat" can walk into secure locations without raising alarms.
