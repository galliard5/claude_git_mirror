## Hooks & Notes
Gallibot is Galliard's own droid PC rather than someone else's character — the name makes that explicit. The Fugitive hook means she starts the campaign running from something or someone, which pairs cleanly with a droid bounty hunter: perhaps she was property that escaped, or a hunter who went off-script on a contract.

The pragmatic power-seeker attitude suggests she's not principled about methods — she wants capability and leverage, not loyalty or ideals. Willpower 1 and Presence 1 are the sacrificed stats, meaning she has almost no social or mental resilience beyond the droid's immunity to mind-affecting powers. The Strain Threshold of 12 (boosted by species and Grit) is respectable, but she gets there through durability, not composure.

The Stalker + Stealth 2 + Agility 3 combination is the heart of the build: getting into position unseen, then opening with the blaster rifle from ambush. The full Assassin tree rewards exactly this approach — Sniper Shot, Precise Aim, Dead Accurate, Lethal Blows, and eventually Master of Shadows are all down that same path.

The fusion cutter as a melee weapon is a small but evocative detail — it's a work tool that also cuts through things. Very droid bounty hunter.

Emergency Repair Patches instead of stimpacks: standard droid logistics. Credits at 0 suggesting recent acquisition or having just spent the +1000cr duty bonus.
