## Hooks & Notes
Gia is a 100-point character — starting-level for GURPS, considerably less built-out than Eve Schallow's 205 points. The two share a surname and both come from Legion-setting campaigns (Eve was "Legion Empire," Gia is "Legion War Stories"), suggesting they're related, or the Schalow name is a recurring family within that setting.

Where Eve is a broad-spectrum intelligence operative, Gia is tightly focused: her Close-Quarters Battle technique at 17 against SMGs is exceptional for a 100-point character, clearly her primary investment. She's been built for fighting inside ships, tunnels, and corridors — exactly the environments a 4'5" mouse soldier would be most effective. Low Fighting and Close Combat from Knife cover the zero-reach/prone scenarios the same cramped environments create.

The full explosives suite (Demolition, EOD, Nuclear Ordnance Disposal, Underwater) is unusually comprehensive for someone at this point level — she's spent efficiently to get broad coverage rather than depth. The Tactics 11 and Observation 12 suggest she's operating with some independence rather than just following orders.

Left-handed, grey-haired at 24 — the grey fur is presumably racial for mouse, not age. The Eye for Distance perk pairs naturally with both the Precision Aiming technique and the Forward Observer skill, implying she does her own spotting. The Erotic Art skill alongside the soldier build is a small but noted detail.

The Vacc Suit (Battlesuit) at default suggests she's expected to operate in one but hasn't formally trained yet. At TL11, that's a meaningful gap for a combat soldier.
