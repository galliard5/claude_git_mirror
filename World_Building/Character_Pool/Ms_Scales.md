## Two GMs, One Character
Ms Scales is the same character concept built under two different GMs — Cit and Mich — with identical stats, qualities, skills, adept powers, and nearly identical gear. The two versions reflect how the character was ported between campaigns rather than rebuilt.

**Differences between versions:**
| Feature | Cit's version | Mich's version |

| Weapons | Ares Predator IV (pistol) | Predator IV + **HK-227X SMG** (suppressed, flechette + explosive rounds) |

Everything else is identical. Mich's version reflects a slightly more developed load-out — an extra cyberware piece and a second weapon.

## SURGE 3 Profile
Ms Scales has Surge 3 — 30 points of positive SURGE qualities and 15 points of negative. This is a significant commitment to Unexplained Genetic Expression, meaning her body has mutated substantially beyond baseline human.

| SURGE Quality | Cost | Effect |
|---------------|------|--------|
| **Celerity** | 5 | Movement speed +50% |
| **Satyr Legs** | 10 | Movement speed +50%, +2 STR for kicking, +2 Gymnastics. Digitigrade hooved legs. |
| **Functional Tail — Prehensile** | 10 | A working grasping tail. Useful for climbing, holding items, and general mischief. |
| **Ogre Stomach** | 5 | Can safely eat almost anything organic |
| **Scales** | -5 | She has visible scales — the source of her name |
| **Short** | -10 | Statistically based off Neoteny — she's small |

Combined Celerity + Satyr Legs = **movement speed ×2** over baseline. She's exceptionally fast for a human-framed character. The prehensile tail provides a third manipulation point.

The net picture: a short, scaled, tailed, hooved physical adept who moves at twice normal speed.

## Hooks & Notes

The SURGE loadout is the defining design choice. Satyr Legs + Celerity doubles her movement — she can sprint in ways that baseline humans simply can't match, and the digitigrade leg structure gives her exceptional leverage for climbing and kicking. The prehensile tail is a third hand in cramped spaces, useful for holding a tool while both hands are otherwise occupied. All of this combined with Stealth Group (Improved Ability: Infiltration +2), Chameleon Suit with Thermal Dampening 4, and an Ares Predator with silencer means she gets in, sets the charge, and gets out before the sensors have anything to report.

The Erased (Daily) / Hunted 2 / Records on File triangle is the most compelling narrative element. Someone is actively erasing her identity every day — this requires either significant corporate resources, a very skilled fixer, or magical intervention. Someone else has managed to maintain records on her anyway. Two separate entities are hunting her. She's simultaneously being protected from above and pursued by multiple parties. The SINner quality means she has a legal identity she presumably can't escape, which creates further tension with the daily erasure.

Ten languages with Linguist built in suggests she was designed for operations across multiple cultural theaters — she can operate in virtually any major city in the Sixth World without a language barrier.

The Demolitions skill with foam explosives and detonator caps confirms she's not just a ghost — she's a ghost who leaves things destroyed after she leaves. Saboteur, not just infiltrator.

The two GMs built her nearly identically, which implies the character concept was strong enough to translate cleanly between campaigns without needing significant adjustment.
