## Language Note
Worg and Xeph are both unusual language choices. Worg is the language of intelligent wolves (FR lore). Xeph is the language of a psionic race in the SRD psionics supplement. Both choices feel deliberate — someone who has made contact with psionic cultures and non-human predator cultures.

## Notes
Hrod is the only other player in the PCGen batch. The fact that Galliard built or at minimum imported this character into PCGen suggests they played together in the same campaign. A Chondathan human egoist psion with a Single-Minded psicrystal, a horse, and barely any money is a specific starting situation. The Overchannel feat at level 1 means he's willing to hurt himself to punch above his tier — a consistent theme with Psicrystal Affinity as backup: he pushes hard and has the crystal to help him hold concentration when he does.

The variant file `Mourgram Uthelienn 2` may represent a later point in the campaign.
