## Hooks & Notes
A very low-level character with enormous growth potential. The Nine-tailed Mystic mechanic is distinctive — her mystery spells manifest as literal tails, which is a great visual hook. Sacred Study making all spells effectively 1 caster level higher gives her surprising punch for tier 3. CHA 20 at level 3 ensures she'll scale well as a social oracle. The extreme STR (-3) means she avoids melee entirely. Lure of the Heavens (no tracks) is a minor but flavorful utility. The prophetic ability at this level is unusual. Best used when a low-powered start with long campaign growth is desired, or as a young NPC with a destiny arc. The "voidken" subrace suggests some cosmic/void flavor that pairs well with the Heavens mystery.
