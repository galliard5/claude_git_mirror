# Isalia's Estate — Setting Summary

## Silberbach

A river valley town of 5,000–6,000 souls built on a bend of the Angerap River. Agricultural, textile, and mining hub. The Old Town sits within ancient stone walls containing the Count's Keep, the Mayor's Mansion, and the main temple. The New Town sprawls beyond — markets, guild halls, workshops, taverns. A natural harbor on the inner bend serves river traders. Farms and estates radiate outward from the town, including Isalia's Manor to the southeast.

Ruled by Count Elias Valtor from Valtor Keep. Eleven trade guilds hold significant political influence. The town employs transformed individuals in infrastructure roles and maintains a small squad of transformed watch animals (hounds, wolves, hawks) for law enforcement. Punitive transformation exists for certain crimes.

## Isalia's Manor — Overview

A private estate southeast of Silberbach operated by Isalia Kreiger — a retired 20th-level kobold barbarian, age 70, daughter of Duke Romaine and Duchess Iaxiandra. The manor offers transformation, medical, breeding, boarding, companionship, and enchantment services. It operates under imperial protection through the Kreiger family's personal ties to Emperor Aldric.
